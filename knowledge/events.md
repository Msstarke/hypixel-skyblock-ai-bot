# Hypixel SkyBlock Events Guide

## Event Calendar & Timing Basics

One SkyBlock year = 372 in-game days = 5 real-life days and 4 hours.
Each SkyBlock month = 31 in-game days. Each SkyBlock day = ~20 real-life minutes.
There are 12 months: Early Spring, Spring, Late Spring, Early Summer, Summer, Late Summer, Early Autumn, Autumn, Late Autumn, Early Winter, Winter, Late Winter.

## Recurring Annual Events

### Spooky Festival
- **Schedule:** Autumn 29-31 (3 SkyBlock days = 1 real-life hour). Additional festivals occur around real-world Halloween.
- **Core Mechanic:** All mobs have a 10% chance to drop Green Candy when killed. Green Candy can convert to Purple Candy with a base 10% chance + (MobLevel / 5).
- **Spooky Mobs:** Special mobs spawn at night in Graveyard, Wilderness, Spider's Den, and Obsidian Sanctuary. They drop extra candy.
- **Trick or Treat Chests:** 1% drop from normal mobs, 3% from Spooky mobs. Opening reveals TRICK (hostile mob spawns) or TREAT (1-3 items). Lucky Hoof and Eternal Hoof increase drop rates. Party Chests (Foxy perk) drop 2-4 items with better loot.
- **Candy Drop Boosters:**
  - Candy Relic: +20%
  - Candy Artifact: +15%
  - Candy Ring: +10%
  - Spooky Armor / Bat Person Armor (full set): +25% each
  - Bat Pet: 0.1%-20% (scales with level)
  - Candied Reforge: +1% per armor piece (+4% max)
  - Foxy's Sweet Benevolence perk: +30%
- **Leaderboard Scoring:** Green Candy = 1 point, Purple Candy = 5 points. Bat Pinata candy does NOT count. Score capped at 5,000 on Stranded profiles.
- **Leaderboard Rewards:**
  - Top 50: Golden Treat, 1M coins, Skeleton Horse Pet, Witch Mask, Vampirism VI book, Spooky Disc, Spooky Pie, 50 Carnival Tickets
  - 5,000+ points: Similar but reduced rewards
  - 2,000+ points: Further reduced
  - 100+ points: Basic rewards, 5 Carnival Tickets
  - Unclaimed rewards expire after 10 SkyBlock years
- **Fear Mongerer:** NPC at the Giant Pumpkin event stand. Arrives 1 hour before and departs 1 hour after the festival. Handles leaderboard access and reward claims.
- **Best Strategy:** Use full Bat Person or Spooky Armor, equip Candy Artifact/Relic, use Bat Pet, grind high-level mobs for better Purple Candy conversion. Focus Spider's Den or End for high mob density.

### New Year Celebration
- **Schedule:** Late Winter 29-31 (3 SkyBlock days = 1 hour real-time).
- **The Baker:** Appears behind Hub spawn in the events area. Gives one free New Year Cake per event per player.
- **New Year Cake Bag:** Costs 250,000 coins from the Baker. Holds up to 54 New Year Cakes and grants +1 HP for every unique cake stored inside.
- **Strategy:** Collect one cake every SkyBlock year. Filling the 54-slot bag takes approximately 279 real-life days of consecutive collection. Older year cakes have collector value.

### Season of Jerry / Jerry's Workshop
- **Schedule:** Jerry's Workshop island opens during all of Late Winter (~11 real-life hours every 5 days 4 hours), plus all of real-world December.
- **Season of Jerry Event:** Late Winter 24-26 (defense event). The main "Defend Jerry's Workshop" activity is 1 hour with 5 rounds.
- **Access:** SkyBlock Menu, personal Jerry NPC on your island, Event Stand, or `/savethejerrys` command.
- **Defend Jerry's Workshop:** Lava-themed mobs attack gift piles across 5 waves. Magma Cubes destroy reward piles and can ONLY be damaged with snowballs (ensures fair play for all combat levels). Rewards are presents based on damage dealt.
- **Key Activities:**
  - **Winter Sea Creatures:** Jerry's Workshop is the ONLY location where winter sea creatures (Yeti, Frozen Steve, Frosty, Reindrake, etc.) can be fished up.
  - **Chicken Race:** Gulliver's quest — fly a chicken through sky hoops. Rewards: Red Gift, Gift Compass, Frozen Chicken, Jingle Bells.
  - **White Gifts:** 20 scattered across the island each time it opens. Collect all 20 and talk to St. Jerry for a Green Gift.
  - **Snow Cannons:** 4 cannons around the map, one player per cannon. Lobby-wide announcement when mounted.
- **NPCs:** Jerry, St. Jerry, Sherry, Gulliver, Frosty, Gary, Banker Barry, Einary.
- **Einary's Emporium:** Winter-themed items purchased with North Stars.
- **Fairy Souls:** 5 Fairy Souls are located in Jerry's Workshop.
- **Best Strategy:** Grind winter sea creatures for Yeti Pet and Baby Yeti Pet drops. Use high Sea Creature Chance gear. Participate in defense event for gift rewards that sell well on bazaar.

### Jerry Box (During Mayor Jerry)
- **How to Obtain:** While Mayor Jerry is in office, Hidden Jerrys (Green, Blue, Purple, Golden) spawn when chopping logs, breaking crops, mining, or killing mobs. 6-minute cooldown between spawns.
- **Spawn Odds:** Green 54.55%, Blue 27.27%, Purple 13.64%, Golden 4.55%.
- **Green Box:** 20,000 coins (44%), 2,500 XP, Jerry Candy, Green Jerry Talisman, Jerry-chine Gun, Jerry Stone, Jerry Rune III, Jerry 3D Glasses.
- **Blue Box:** 50,000 coins (44%), 5,000 XP, plus unique items.
- **Purple Box:** 150,000 coins (43%), 300,000 coins (2.16%), 20,000 XP, plus unique items.
- **Golden Box:** 500,000 coins (80.65%), 1,000,000 coins (8.06%), 10,000,000 coins (0.81%), 100,000 XP, plus unique items.
- **Tips:** 25-second pickup priority if another player kills your Hidden Jerry. Cannot be auctioned or sold to NPCs.

### Traveling Zoo
- **Schedule:** Early Summer 1-3 and Early Winter 1-3 (twice per SkyBlock year). Each occurrence lasts 1 real-life hour. Repeats every ~2 days 14 hours real-time.
- **Location:** Hub Event Stand (coordinates -4, 70, -44). Also appears on private islands in Stranded mode.
- **Oringo NPC:** Sells exactly 3 of 6 possible pets each visit. One pet is always Legendary rarity (rotating sequence), two others have random rarities (Common to Epic).
- **Available Pets:**
  - Lion Pet (Foraging)
  - Monkey Pet (Foraging)
  - Elephant Pet (Farming)
  - Giraffe Pet (Foraging)
  - Blue Whale Pet (Fishing)
  - Tiger Pet (Combat)
- **Costs by Rarity:**
  - Common: ~10,000 coins + 64-128 raw materials
  - Uncommon: ~25,000 coins + 1-2 enchanted materials
  - Rare: ~100,000 coins + 16-32 enchanted materials
  - Epic: ~1,000,000 coins + 128-256 enchanted materials
  - Legendary: ~10-18M coins + 512-1,024 enchanted materials
- **Strategy:** Check which pets are offered each visit. Tiger Pet (Combat) and Blue Whale Pet (Fishing) are the most useful. Buy Legendary rarity when available as it is guaranteed once per rotation.

### Hoppity's Hunt
- **Schedule:** Early Spring 1 through Late Spring 31 (entire spring season, ~3 months in-game).
- **Chocolate Eggs:** Spawn at 7 AM (Breakfast), 2 PM (Lunch), 9 PM (Dinner) across all islands. 9 eggs spawn hourly, 279 total throughout event. Max 6 active eggs at once.
- **Finding Eggs:** Search manually or use Egglocator device. Eggs yield random Chocolate Rabbits.
- **Chocolate Rewards by Rarity:** Common: 150s of production, Uncommon: 300s, Rare: 750s, Epic: 1,500s, Legendary: 4,000s, Mythic: 8,000s, Divine: 15,000s.
- **Duplicates:** Grant cumulative bonuses: +10% base per duplicate, up to +100% at 10th duplicate.
- **Best Locations:** Crystal Hollows (especially Crystal Nucleus) is the best place. Gold Mine as alternative for early-game players.

## Mayor-Exclusive Events

### Fishing Festival (Marina / Foxy)
- **Schedule (Marina):** First 3 days of each SkyBlock month during Marina's term (12 festivals total). Duration: 3 SkyBlock days = 1 real-life hour. Repeats every 9 hours 20 minutes.
- **Schedule (Foxy):** Late Summer 22-24 as a bonus festival.
- **Note:** Starts at 6 AM in-game time (not midnight like other events).
- **Core Mechanic:** Players fish up sharks. Shark Bait increases shark catch chance by 20%.
- **Shark Types:**

| Shark | Fishing Level | HP | Drops |
|-------|--------------|-----|-------|
| Nurse Shark | 5 | 2,500 | 2-4 Shark Fins, Nurse Shark Tooth |
| Blue Shark | 10 | 25,000 | 4-8 Shark Fins, Blue Shark Tooth |
| Tiger Shark | 18 | 250,000 | 8-16 Shark Fins, Tiger Shark Tooth, Megalodon Pet |
| Great White Shark | 24 | 1,500,000 | 16-32 Shark Fins, Great White Shark Tooth, Megalodon Pet |

- **Shark Tooth Necklace Progression:**
  - Raggedy (Common): +2 Strength, +5% shark tooth drop
  - Dull (Uncommon): +4 Strength, +10%
  - Honed (Rare): +6 Strength, +15%
  - Sharp (Epic): +8 Strength, +20%
  - Razor-sharp (Legendary): +10 Strength, +25%
- **Exclusive Gear:**
  - Shark Scale Armor (Legendary): 2.5 Sea Creature Chance per piece
  - Rod of the Sea (Legendary): Highest Sea Creature Chance fishing rod in the game
  - Megalodon Pet (Epic/Legendary): Drops from Tiger and Great White Sharks
- **Best Strategy:** Use maximum Sea Creature Chance gear. Bring Shark Bait. Target Great White Sharks at Fishing 24+ for best Shark Fin and Megalodon Pet drops. Fish at the barn or Jerry's Workshop for concentrated water areas.

### Mining Fiesta (Cole / Foxy)
- **Schedule (Cole):** 5 events during Cole's term — Early Summer 1st, Late Summer 1st, Early Autumn 1st, Autumn 1st, Late Autumn 1st. Each lasts 7 SkyBlock days = 2 hours 20 minutes real-time (11 hours 40 minutes total).
- **Schedule (Foxy):** Late Summer 22nd bonus event.
- **Buffs:** +75 Mining Wisdom and double mining drops during events.
- **Special Drops:**

| Item | Rarity | Drop Rate | Use |
|------|--------|-----------|-----|
| Refined Mineral | Rare | 1/375 from ores | Mineral Armor & Talisman |
| Glossy Gemstone | Epic | 1/725 from gemstones | Glossy Mineral Armor & Talisman |

- **Craftable Rewards:**
  - Mineral Talisman: +3 Mining Fortune
  - Mineral Armor (full set): Mine 2 extra adjacent blocks (3 or less breaking power)
  - Glossy Mineral Talisman: +6 Mining Fortune
  - Glossy Mineral Armor (full set): Extra block mining + 50 Mining Fortune on non-ore blocks per piece
- **Mining Locations:** Deep Caverns, Gold Mine, Hub, Dwarven Mines, Crystal Hollows, Glacite areas.
- **Notes:** Mining Fortune does NOT affect Refined Mineral drops. Living Metal Hearts are NOT affected by event bonuses.
- **Best Strategy:** Mine in Crystal Hollows or Dwarven Mines for maximum ore density. Focus gemstone mining for Glossy Gemstones. The double drops and +75 Mining Wisdom make this the best time to level Mining skill.

### Mythological Ritual (Diana / Jerry with Diana perks)
- **Activation:** When Diana is elected Mayor with the Mythological Ritual perk, or when Mayor Jerry rotates to Diana's perks via Perkpocalypse.
- **Duration:** 5 days and 4 hours (one full SkyBlock year — Diana's entire mayoral term).
- **Diana's Perks:** Mythological Ritual (enables burrow hunting), Pet XP Buff (+35%), Huntress' Intuition (+10 Tracking).
- **Core Mechanic:** Equip a Griffin Pet and use a spade to find and dig Griffin Burrows around the Hub Island.
- **Getting Started:** Buy Griffin Pet from Diana for 25,000 coins. Buy Ancestral Spade for 5,000 coins + 32 Gold Ingots. Must be on Hub Island.
- **How to Use Spade:** Hold spade → burrows appear as particles. Right-click (Echo ability, no mana cost) → arrow points to nearest burrow. Left-click burrow to dig it.
- **All mythological mobs have 50% Magic Resistance.**
- **Mythological Creatures despawn after 5 minutes if not killed, or if the spawning player dies.**
- **Ancient Claw is a guaranteed (100%) drop from ALL mythological creatures.**

**Burrow Chain Mechanics:**
- Chains of 4, 6, 8, or 10 burrows depending on spade tier.
- 1st burrow: Arrow marker only (no mob/treasure).
- 2nd+ burrows: 75% chance mob spawn, 25% chance treasure.
- Treasure burrows: Griffin Feather, Crown of Greed, Washed-Up Souvenir, or coins.
- Particle colors: Blue = 1st (start), White = 2nd/3rd (mob), Orange = 4th+ (treasure).
- Arrow colors: Yellow = nearby, Red = medium distance, Black = far away.

**Spade Tiers:**
- Ancestral Spade (RARE): Base 4 burrow chain. Griffin Feathers (66.67%), coins up to 25,000. Buy from Diana for 5,000 coins + 32 Gold Ingots.
- Archaic Spade (EPIC): +2 chain length. Crafted from: 1x Ancestral Spade + 3x Enchanted Ancient Claw + 2x Enchanted Gold Block + 32x Griffin Feather + 1x Hilt of Revelations. Mythos Fragments (1.78%), coins up to 250,000.
- Deific Spade (LEGENDARY): +4 chain length. Crafted from: 1x Archaic Spade + 1x Hilt of Revelations + 1x Braided Griffin Feather + 128x Enchanted Ancient Claw + 128x Enchanted Gold Block + 16x Mythos Fragment. Braided Griffin Feathers (0.03%), coins up to 1,000,000.
- Erudite Reforge (from Daedalus' Notes): +2 chain length on any spade.
- Maximum chain: 10 burrows (Deific + Erudite).

**Griffin Pet (Combat):**
- Buy from Diana for 25,000 coins. Cannot be upgraded by Kat — must use Griffin Feathers.
- Upgrade costs: Common→Uncommon 8 feathers, Uncommon→Rare 16, Rare→Epic 32, Epic→Legendary 64 (total 120).
- Mythic upgrade: Uses Mythic Griffin Upgrade Stone.
- Abilities:
  - COMMON — Odyssey: Creatures/rewards scale with Griffin rarity.
  - UNCOMMON — Legendary Constitution: Permanent Regeneration VII and Strength VIII.
  - EPIC — Perpetual Empathy: Heal nearby players for 0.2% per level of damage received (20% at Lv100).
  - LEGENDARY — King of Kings: +0.14% Strength per level when above 85% HP (~15% at Lv100).
- Best pet item: Four-Eyed Fish (+10 Magic Find, +55 Defense, +2,000 coins per burrow).

**Mythological Creatures by Griffin Rarity:**
- No pet: Minos Hunter only.
- COMMON: + Siamese Lynxes.
- UNCOMMON: + Cretan Bull, Stranded Nymph.
- RARE: + Gaia Construct, Harpy.
- EPIC: + Minotaur, Minos Champion.
- LEGENDARY: + Minos Inquisitor, Sphinx (elusive — boosted by Tracking stat).
- MYTHIC: + Manticore, King Minos (elusive — boosted by Tracking stat).

**Universal Rare Drops (from any mob except Minos Hunter):**
- Antique Remedies: ~1/400 chance (1/100 from Inquisitor). Pet item, +80% pet Strength.
- Crochet Tiger Plushie: ~1/400 chance (1/100 from Inquisitor). Pet item.
- Dwarf Turtle Shelmet: ~1/400 chance (1/100 from Inquisitor). Pet item.

**Mob Spawn Weights (Legendary Griffin):** Minos Hunter 12.35%, Siamese Lynx 18.52%, Minotaur 24.69%, Gaia Construct 24.69%, Minos Champion 18.52%, **Minos Inquisitor 1.23% (1/81)**. With Ender Slayer 9: Inquisitor weight +15%, making chance ~1/70.5.

**Additional Notes:**
- Burrow quality depends on Griffin rarity when burrows are GENERATED (not when dug).
- Reset burrows by talking to Diana and selecting "Reset Burrows".
- 2nd and 3rd burrows: 90% mob spawn, 10% treasure. Later burrows: 75% mob, 25% treasure.
- With Combat Level 10+, 1/250 chance to spawn a Mythological Creature when killing any mob.
- Sphinx won't drop loot if captured with Pocket Black Hole — DON'T use it.

**Detailed Mob Info:**

**Minos Hunter (COMMON):** Lv15-Lv125 depending on Griffin rarity. Easiest mob, basic melee. Drops: Ancient Claw (guaranteed), Hilt of Revelations (0.25-2%).

**Siamese Lynxes (COMMON, requires COMMON+ Griffin):** Spawn as a PAIR (Bagheera & Azrael). Only one can be attacked at a time (indicated by particles above it). After hitting one, particles transfer to partner — must alternate hits. Hitting wrong one heals BOTH for 5% max HP. Both must be killed.

**Cretan Bull (UNCOMMON, requires UNCOMMON+ Griffin):** Rams toward player at high speed. Deals True Damage and True Knockback. After a hit, stops moving for 5 seconds — attack window. True Damage ignores armor.

**Stranded Nymph (UNCOMMON, requires UNCOMMON+ Griffin):** Added in Mythological Ritual v2 update.

**Gaia Construct (RARE, requires RARE+ Griffin):** ~7.5M HP. THREE PHASES based on HP thresholds:
- Stage 1 (100-66% HP): Shield blocks first 5 hits, 6th deals damage. Less lightning.
- Stage 2 (66-33% HP): Shield blocks first 6 hits, 7th deals damage. More lightning.
- Stage 3 (33-0% HP): Shield blocks first 7 hits, 8th deals damage. Most lightning.
- LIGHTNING: Telegraphed by "+" pattern of Iron Blocks on ground. Deals 1,500 + 40% of player's Max HP as True Damage.
- Tip: Use magic weapon to break shield fast, then switch to high-damage weapon. Move away from iron block patterns.
- Drops: Ancient Claw, Enchanted Gold Ingot, Enchanted Iron Ingot (unique to Gaia Construct).

**Harpy (RARE, requires RARE+ Griffin):** Added in Mythological Ritual v2. Has 50% Magic Resistance.

**Minotaur (EPIC, requires EPIC+ Griffin):** ~10M HP. Mechanics:
- Melee attack: +1 Bleed stack.
- Axe Throw: Slow-moving axe every ~1.5 seconds, +3 Bleed stacks, no knockback.
- BLEED: Every 2 seconds, strikes player with 75x Bleed count as damage. Also reduces Healing.
- Tip: Dodge axes (they add 3 Bleed vs 1 from melee). Kill quickly before Bleed stacks become lethal.
- Drops: Ancient Claw, Enchanted Gold Ingot, **Daedalus Stick (0.08% / 1 in 1,250, affected by Magic Find)**.

**Minos Champion (EPIC, requires EPIC+ Griffin):** Uses sword and bow attacks.
- Damaging Aura: 7.5 block radius, 200-400 damage per tick.
- ENRAGE: Gains 5% Damage buff every second alive, caps at 600%. **Despawns at cap with NO DROPS.** Kill time limit: ~2 minutes.
- Lightning Punishment: If player stands still for 8-15 seconds, summons lightning dealing 20% of Max HP as True Damage.
- Drops: Ancient Claw (48x), Enchanted Gold Ingot, Enchanted Ancient Claw, **Minos Relic (0.02% / 1 in 5,000)**.

**Minos Inquisitor (LEGENDARY, requires LEGENDARY+ Griffin, elusive):** ~4M HP (up to 160M for Runic variant). Can also spawn rarely (0.4% chance) when any mob is killed with LEGENDARY Griffin.
- Damaging Aura: 7.5 block radius, 250 damage per tick.
- STAT DEBUFF: Aura reduces player's Health, Intelligence, Speed, Vitality by 33%.
- ENRAGE: 8% Damage buff per second, caps at 600%. **Despawns at cap with NO DROPS.** Kill time limit: 75 seconds.
- Drops: 48x Ancient Claw, 8x Enchanted Gold Ingot, Enchanted Ancient Claw (1/4), **Chimera Book (~1% / 1 in 100, affected by Magic Find)**, Dwarf Turtle Shelmet (1/100), Antique Remedies (1/100), Crochet Tiger Plushie (1/100).
- Tip: Most dangerous mob. 75-second kill timer is extremely tight. Many players call for party help. Bring maximum DPS.

**Sphinx (LEGENDARY, requires LEGENDARY+ Griffin, elusive):** ~40M HP. Added in Mythological Ritual v2.
- Drops: 16x Ancient Claw, 0-16x Enchanted Gold Ingot, Enchanted Ancient Claw, **Brain Food (0.25-0.5%, EPIC item, grants +1 Tracking stat, stackable up to +5)**, Mythological Dye.
- Brain Food is the signature drop — very valuable for increasing Tracking.

**Manticore (MYTHIC, requires MYTHIC Griffin, elusive):** ~125M HP. Base spawn chance 0.26-0.33% with 0 Tracking.
- Drops: 48x Ancient Claw, Enchanted Gold Ingot, Enchanted Ancient Claw, **Fateful Stinger (~0.2%, upgrades Venomous VI → VII on swords)**, Manti-core (crafts into Manticore Claw), Mythological Dye.

**King Minos (MYTHIC, requires MYTHIC Griffin, elusive):** ~100M HP. Has a golden sheep companion. Base spawn chance 0.26-0.33% with 0 Tracking.
- Drops: 48x Ancient Claw, 48x Enchanted Gold Ingot, Enchanted Ancient Claw, **Crown of Greed (~2% / 1 in 50, gives 2x coins from burrows, +10 MF vs myth mobs, 1.25x damage taken)**, Shimmering Wool (from golden sheep), Mythological Dye.

**Key Items:**
- **Chimera** (Ultimate Enchantment, levels 1-5): Copies 20/40/60/80/100% of pet's base stats onto weapon. Does NOT copy pet ability stats, but DOES copy pet item stats. Drops from Minos Inquisitor (~1%). Worth hundreds of millions. Requires Enchanting 31.
- **Daedalus Blade** (LEGENDARY sword): Crafted from 48 Enchanted Gold Blocks + 2 Daedalus Sticks + 1 Sword of Revelations. +4 Damage per Taming level (up to 240 at Taming 60), copies pet combat stats. +200% damage vs Mythological Creatures. +1% Damage and +0.2 Magic Find per Bestiary tier on mythological mobs. Chimera on this weapon grants up to 200% of pet stats (double normal).
- **Daedalus Stick**: Drops from Minotaur (0.08% / 1 in 1,250). Needs EPIC+ Griffin. Used to craft Daedalus Blade.
- **Sword of Revelations** (EPIC): Crafted from 192 Griffin Feathers + other materials. +150% damage and +5 Magic Find vs mythological mobs.
- **Minos Relic** (EPIC Pet Item): Increases ALL pet stats by 33.3%. Drops from Minos Champion (0.02%). Can upgrade to Hephaestus Relic.
- **Antique Remedies** (Pet Item): Increases pet Strength by 80%. Drops from any mythological mob (1/400, 1/100 from Inquisitor).
- **Brain Food** (EPIC): +1 Tracking stat (stackable up to +5). Drops from Sphinx.
- **Fateful Stinger**: Upgrades Venomous VI → VII on swords. Drops from Manticore (~0.2%).
- **Crown of Greed**: 2x coins from burrows, +10 Magic Find vs mythological mobs, but 1.25x damage taken. Drops from King Minos (~2%).
- **Golden Fleece**: +5 Magic Find to mythological mobs, +15 Tracking on Griffin Burrows. Crafted with Shimmering Wool from King Minos.

**Best Setup (Late-game):**
- Pet: Legendary or Mythic Griffin with Four-Eyed Fish.
- Weapon: Daedalus Blade with Chimera V (best Magic Find on mythological mobs, 200% pet stat copy).
- Armor: Sorrow Armor with Magic Find focus, or Storm's/Necron's for combat.
- Spade: Deific with Erudite reforge (max 10 chain).
- Stack Tracking stat for elusive mob spawns (Brain Food +5, Huntress' Intuition +10, Golden Fleece +15).
- Use fast mobility (AOTE/AOTV) to chain burrows quickly.
- SkyHanni mod is practically mandatory (~80% efficiency boost from burrow tracking features).

- Common Griffin: ~12M coins/hour (mostly from Griffin Feathers, ~1.5 stacks/hour).
- Legendary Griffin (max setup): 70-100M+ coins/hour. Main income from Chimera books (~100-200M each, 1/81 Inquisitor spawn, ~1% Chimera drop), Daedalus Sticks (~2M each), Ancient Claws, and coin bags.

### Dark Auction
- **Schedule:** Every 3 SkyBlock days at midnight SkyBlock time (~every 1 hour real-time, starting at X:55). 124 auctions per SkyBlock year.
- **Access:** Sirius appears at coordinates 91, 75, 176 in the Wilderness for 35 seconds at midnight. Click him to enter. Requires 400,000+ coins in purse. If too many players, only the richest enter. Max 12 players per lobby.
- **Mechanics:** 45 seconds of prep, then Sirius makes all players invisible. Each item auction lasts 10 seconds, timer resets with each new bid. Money is removed from purse on bid and refunded if outbid. 5 items per standard auction, 8 during Scorpius (Darker Auctions).
- **Standard Items:**
  - Ender Artifact (20M min, 34.31% chance)
  - Wither Artifact (10M min, 34.31% chance)
  - Nether Artifact (10M min, 34.31% chance)
  - Midas' Sword (100K min, 24.07% chance)
  - Magic 8 Ball (20M min, 18.49% chance)
  - Travel Scroll to Dark Auction (1M min, 12.61% chance)
  - Eccentric Painting Bundle (20M min, 12.61% chance)
  - Dark Purple Dye (20M min, 0.75% — extremely rare)
  - Flower Minion (2.5M min, guaranteed every auction)
  - Legendary/Epic Turtle, Parrot, Jellyfish Pets
  - Enchanted Books: Sharp 6, Giant Killer 6, Protection 6, Growth 6, Power 6 (500K min), Victimize 5 (10M min)
- **Scorpius Exclusive (Darker Auctions):**
  - Level 7 Enchantment books (Protection, Sharpness, Power, Growth, Giant Killer)
  - Counter-Strike 5 book
  - Plasma Nucleus (20M min)
  - Hegemony Artifact (100M min)
  - Midas Staff (100K min)
  - Enchanted Book Bundles (Vicious, Big Brain)
- **Post-Purchase Milestones (from Lucius):**
  - 5 items bought: Shady Ring
  - 10 items bought: Crooked Artifact
  - 15 items bought: Seal of the Family
- **Rules:** Sirius won't sell two pets of the same rarity (but can sell different rarities). If winner disconnects, coins are taken and item goes to Jerry. Bingo players cannot access Dark Auction.
- **Best Strategy:** Bring enough coins to actually win bids. Check if Flower Minion price is worth it (always appears). During Scorpius, prioritize Hegemony Artifact and Level 7 books. Don't overbid on common enchanted books available elsewhere.

### Spooky Festival
- **Schedule:** Autumn 29-31 (1 real hour / 3 in-game days). Once per SkyBlock year.
- **Mechanic:** Kill mobs to earn Green Candy (1 point) and Purple Candy (5 points). Points determine bracket rewards.
- **Point Brackets:**
  - 2,400+ points: Skeleton Horse Pet (Legendary), Vampire Mask, 1,000,000 coins
  - 2,000-2,399 points: Vampire Mask, 500,000 coins
  - 2,001-10,000 points: Spooky Pie, 25,000 coins
- **Ranking Rewards:**
  - Rank 1-50: Golden Treat, Vampirism VI Book, Spooky Disk, Spooky Pie, 200,000 coins
  - Rank 51-250: Vampirism VI Book, Spooky Disk, Spooky Pie, 100,000 coins
  - Rank 251-2,000: Spooky Disk, Spooky Pie, 50,000 coins
- **Fear Mongerer Shop:** Trick or Treat Bag (1 Green), Magic Find I Potion (10 Green), Candy Talisman (16 Green), Intimidation Ring (100 Green), Candy Ring (64 Green), Horseman's Candle (32 Purple), Candy Artifact (32 Purple + Candy Ring), Candy Relic (1,024 Purple + Candy Artifact), Sugar Rush I Book (250K coins + 64 Green).
- **Candy Drop Chance Modifiers (additive):** Base 10% from any mob. Bat Person Armor +20% (full set), Spooky Armor +20%, Bat Pet Lv100 +20%, Candy Relic +20%, Candy Artifact +15%, Candy Ring +10%, Candy Talisman +5%, Candied Armor Reforge +1%/piece, Candy Scavenger +2-10%, Foxy Sweet Benevolence +30%. Max: 84% (114% with Foxy).
- **Spooky Mobs** take exactly 1 HP per hit regardless of damage (3 HP with Ghoul Buster + Bat Pet).

## Shen's Auction

Shen's Auction is a special NPC-run auction on Barrier Street in the Hub. It occurs once per SkyBlock Year (~5 real-life days and 4 hours).

### Shen's Auction (requires Museum Milestone 10)
Three items available each SkyBlock Year with limited quantities:
- **Artifact of Control** — 10 per year. Doubles your Mayor Election vote. Accessory.
- **Pandora's Box** — 20 per year. Accessory that cannot be recombobulated. Gives random stats each day.
- **Shen's Regalia** — up to 40 per year. LEGENDARY accessory granting access to the Regalia Room shop.

### Shen's Special (requires Museum Milestone 20)
Offers 2 randomly-selected items from a pool each SkyBlock Year. Items are per-profile (each profile sees different items). You can reroll displayed items up to 2 times per year using a lever.

**Item pool includes:**
- Clover Helmet, Racing Helmet, Jacob's Helmet
- Giant Fishing Rod (50% chance to fish a sea creature twice)
- Divine Gift, The Art of Peace
- Wizard Wand, Rat Jetpack
- Priceless the Fish, Dirt Rod
- Puzzle Cube
- Shimmersparkle Chestplate
- Rock Skins (Surprised, Blinking, Sus, Sweating)
- Dead Cat Food (pet item, Montezuma only)

### Diaz Mayor Interaction
If Diaz is elected with the "Volume Trading" perk:
- Winners per item are doubled for Shen's Auction and Shen's Special
- 2 additional exclusive items become available in Shen's Special

### Tips
- Shen's items are often very expensive (tens to hundreds of millions)
- Artifact of Control is valuable during contested mayor elections
- Shen's Regalia gives access to a special shop with unique items
- Giant Fishing Rod is one of the best fishing rods for sea creature grinding
- Check the SkyBlock calendar or community timers for the next Shen's Auction

## General Event Tips

1. **Check the SkyBlock Calendar** in-game or use community timer websites to track event schedules.
2. **Mayor elections matter** — Diana (Mythological Ritual), Marina (Fishing Festival), Cole (Mining Fiesta), Foxy (bonus events), Jerry (Jerry Boxes + random perks), Scorpius (Darker Auctions).
3. **Prepare gear in advance** — Most events reward specialized setups (Sea Creature Chance for fishing, Mining Fortune for mining, Farming Fortune for contests).
4. **Events stack with mayor perks** — Foxy can trigger bonus versions of most events. Jerry's Perkpocalypse can randomly activate any mayor's event perks.
5. **Don't miss New Year Cakes** — The HP bonus from the Cake Bag adds up over time and old cakes have collector value.
6. **Dark Auction timing** — Arrives at X:55 real time. Have coins ready in purse (not bank).
7. **Mythological Ritual is the most profitable event** for late-game players — Chimera I from Minos Inquisitor is worth hundreds of millions.
