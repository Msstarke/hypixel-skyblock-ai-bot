# Hypixel SkyBlock Enchantments Reference

> Source: Hypixel SkyBlock Fandom Wiki (scraped March 2026)
> This file is the authoritative reference for enchantment data. Do NOT hallucinate enchantments not listed here.

## General Enchanting Rules

- Enchantments improve gear (Armor, Pickaxes, Drills, Hoes, Shears, Swords, Axes, Fishing Rods, Shovels, Bows).
- The Enchantment Table can apply up to Level V enchantments. Higher levels come from Experiments, Dark Auction, Darker Auction, Slayer rewards, or special sources.
- Enchanted Books are free to combine and do not add anvil uses.
- Only ONE Ultimate Enchantment can be on an item at a time. Applying a new one overwrites the old one.
- Conflicting enchantments cannot coexist on the same item (see exclusivity notes below).
- Some enchantments auto-upgrade by performing actions (Champion, Compact, Cultivating, Expertise, Hecatomb, Absorb) and cannot be combined in an anvil.

## Enchantment Exclusivity Groups (Cannot Coexist)

These enchantments are mutually exclusive -- only one from each group can be on the same item:

### Sword Exclusives
- **Execute** vs **Prosecute** (pick one damage scaling method)
- **First Strike** vs **Triple-Strike** (pick one first-hit bonus type)
- **Giant Killer** vs **Titan Killer** (pick one enemy-stat-based damage bonus)
- **Thunderbolt** vs **Thunderlord** (pick one lightning effect)
- **Life Steal** vs **Syphon** vs **Mana Steal** vs **Drain** (pick one sustain enchantment -- note: Drain conflicts with Life Steal and Mana Steal; Life Steal conflicts with Syphon and Mana Steal; Mana Steal conflicts with Life Steal and Syphon)

### Armor Exclusives
- **Protection** vs **Blast Protection** vs **Fire Protection** vs **Projectile Protection** (pick one protection type per armor piece)
- **Big Brain** vs **Small Brain** (helmet only)
- **Rejuvenate** vs **Respite** (pick one health regen type)
- **Ferocious Mana** vs **Hardened Mana** vs **Strong Mana** vs **Mana Vampire** (pick one Kuudra mana enchant)
- **Thorns** vs **Reflection** (chestplate only)

### Tool Exclusives
- **Silk Touch** vs **Smelting Touch** (pick one block drop modification)
- **Silk Touch** vs **Fortune** (cannot have both)

## Common Enchantment Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Combat enchantments do NOT go on tools**: Sharpness, Smite, Critical, Looting (as combat enchant), etc. are for Swords only. Do not recommend them for pickaxes, drills, or hoes.

2. **Mining enchantments do NOT go on swords/bows**: Fortune, Efficiency, Compact, Silk Touch, Smelting Touch are tool-only enchantments.

3. **Harvesting is HOE-ONLY**: It cannot be applied to axes or other tools. Sunder and Cultivating work on both hoes and axes.

4. **Efficiency max level is X (10)**, not V. Levels 6-10 require Silex items to upgrade, not the enchantment table.

5. **Fortune max level is IV (4)**, not V or higher. It comes from Enchantment Table (I-III) and Crystal Nucleus (IV).

6. **Overload is a BOW enchantment** (also works on swords with certain items), not an armor enchantment. Max level V.

7. **Syphon is NOT listed as a standard sword enchantment** in the current wiki data -- it may be obtained from special sources. Life Steal and Mana Steal are the listed healing alternatives. Drain is the CD-scaling healing option.

8. **Infinite Quiver max is X (10)**, not V. It saves 3% per level (not 10%).

9. **Feather Falling max is X (10)** through combining books (level 20 exists only pre-enchanted on Spring Boots).

10. **Protection/Growth max is VII (7)**, not V or VI. Level VII comes from Darker Auction or Experiments.

11. **Power max is VII (7)** for bows, not V or VI.

12. **Sharpness max is VII (7)** normally. Level 10 exists only pre-enchanted on Aspect of the Jerry Signature Edition.

13. **Turbo enchantments are crop-specific**: Each variant (Turbo-Wheat, Turbo-Carrot, etc.) only boosts its specific crop. They go on Hoes and Axes.

14. **One For All removes ALL other enchantments** from the weapon. Only use it when you want pure raw damage with no other enchant effects.

15. **Looting on Fishing Rods** does NOT affect fishing drops -- it only works when the rod is used as a melee weapon.

16. **Dragon Tracer** only works on Dragons, not all mobs. It replaced the old "Aiming" enchantment.

17. **Champion/Toxophilite/Compact/Cultivating/Expertise/Hecatomb/Absorb** all auto-upgrade through gameplay actions and CANNOT be combined in an anvil.

18. **Tabasco** exists for Swords (max III), Bows (max III), and Fishing Rods (max III). It requires NOT having a Dragon pet equipped.

19. **Lapidary** is for Pickaxes/Drills and boosts Gemstone Fortune and Mining Speed while mining Gemstones specifically.

20. **Prismatic** (formerly called Pristine) grants the Pristine stat for gemstone quality. It is for Pickaxes, Drills, and Gemstone Gauntlet.
