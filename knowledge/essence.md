# Essence Guide

Essence is a currency used to upgrade items via Star Upgrades, convert items into Dungeon Items ("dungeonizing"), and purchase permanent perks from Essence Shops. There are 10 types of Essence, each obtained from different sources. The maximum amount of any Essence type a player can hold is 2 billion.

Purchasing Essence from the Bazaar requires Catacombs level 20, except for Forest Essence which has no requirement.

## Essence Types and How to Obtain Them

### Wither Essence
- Dungeon Reward Chests (primary source)
- Dungeon Hub Races (rewarded by Guildford)
- Dungeon Secrets
- Salvaging items that use Wither Essence for upgrades
- Bazaar (requires Catacombs 20)

### Undead Essence
- Dungeon Reward Chests (primary source)
- Killing Crypt Undeads in Dungeons
- Salvaging items that use Undead Essence for upgrades
- Bazaar (requires Catacombs 20)

### Crimson Essence
- Completing Kuudra runs (primary source, all tiers)
- Salvaging Crimson Isle gear
- Bazaar (requires Catacombs 20)
- Kuudra Boss Collection rewards

### Spider Essence
- Killing Arachne (primary source)
- Killing Lonely Spiders and Cellar Spiders
- Salvaging items that use Spider Essence for upgrades
- Bazaar (requires Catacombs 20)

### Dragon Essence
- Killing Ender Dragons (primary source)
- Killing Lost Adventurers
- Sacrificing items at the Draconic Altar
- Salvaging items that use Dragon Essence for upgrades
- Bazaar (requires Catacombs 20)

### Gold Essence
- Dungeon Secrets
- Killing King Midas and Golden Goblins
- Killing Watcher Undeads with Golden modifier
- Opening Crystal Hollows Treasure Chests
- Completing the Crystal Nucleus
- Salvaging items that use Gold Essence for upgrades
- Bazaar (requires Catacombs 20)

### Diamond Essence
- Dungeon Secrets
- Killing Angry Archaeologists and Diamond Goblins
- Opening Crystal Hollows Treasure Chests
- Completing the Crystal Nucleus
- Salvaging items that use Diamond Essence for upgrades
- Bazaar (requires Catacombs 20)

### Ice Essence
- Killing Winter Sea Creatures
- Killing Frozen Adventurers
- Salvaging items that use Ice Essence for upgrades
- Bazaar (requires Catacombs 20)

### Forest Essence
- Tree Gifts
- Fig and Mangrove tree harvesting
- Agatha's rewards
- Bazaar (no Catacombs requirement)

### Sun Gecko Essence
- Defeating Sun Gecko in the Rift

## Star Upgrades

Star Upgrades enhance item stats using Essence. Each star grants a +2% stat boost to base stats. Dungeon Items receive a +10% stat boost per star while inside the Catacombs. Standard dungeon items can have up to 5 stars. Crimson Isle gear (upgraded with Crimson Essence) can have up to 15 stars across 5 prestige tiers.

Star upgrades require Carpentry level 25 to apply.

### General Star Cost Pattern
The cost roughly doubles with each star level. Higher-tier items cost significantly more. A rough guide for total 5-star costs:
- Low-tier dungeon items: 200-800 Essence per piece
- Mid-tier dungeon items: 600-2,000 Essence per piece
- Top-tier dungeon items (Necron, Wither blades): 1,200-3,350 Essence per piece

### Crimson Isle Star Upgrades (Crimson Essence)

Crimson gear (Terror, Aurora, Fervor, Hollow, Crimson armor) uses Crimson Essence and supports up to 15 stars through 5 prestige tiers (Basic, Hot, Burning, Fiery, Infernal). Each tier has 3 stars.

#### Terror Armor Example (Crimson Essence, per piece approx)
| Tier | Stars | Essence Range |
|------|-------|---------------|
| Basic (1-3) | 1-3 | 30-90 |

Total to max a full Terror set from scratch: ~1,793,505 Crimson Essence + 172 Heavy Pearls + 160 Kuudra Teeth.

## Master Stars

Master Stars are applied on top of the base 5 stars on dungeon items, providing bonus stats in Master Mode dungeons. Items must already have 5 stars before Master Stars can be applied.

### Master Star Items
| Master Star | Stat Bonus (Master Mode) | Drop Source |
|-------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| First Master Star | +5% stats | M3-M7 Reward Chests |
| Second Master Star | +10% total stats | M4-M7 Reward Chests |
| Third Master Star | +15% total stats | M5-M7 Reward Chests |
| Fourth Master Star | +20% total stats | M6-M7 Reward Chests |
| Fifth Master Star | +25% total stats | M7 Reward Chests |

### Applying Master Stars
Each Master Star requires Essence to apply (in addition to the Master Star item):
| Master Star | Essence Cost |
|-------------|-------------|
| First | 300 |
| Second | 350 |
| Third | 400 |
| Fourth | 450 |
| Fifth | 500 |

Master Stars are rare drops from Obsidian and Bedrock chests in Master Mode floors, with drop rates between 0.32% and 2.78%.

## Essence Perks Priority Guide

### Best Perks to Buy First
1. **Wither Forbidden perks** - Permanent universal stat boosts (work everywhere). Cheap and always useful.
2. **Cooled Forges (Ice)** - Extra essence from salvaging helps long-term.
3. **Undead dungeon stat perks** - If you run dungeons frequently, the HP/Defense/Strength/Intelligence boosts add up significantly.
4. **Boss Luck (Undead)** - Better dungeon loot quality.
5. **Class XP perks** - Relevant to your main dungeon class (Archer from Spider, Mage from Ice, Healer from Gold, Tank from Diamond, Berserk from Dragon).

## Essence Farming Tips

### Wither and Undead Essence
- **Run dungeons efficiently**: Higher floors give more essence from reward chests. F7 and M floors are the best sources.
- **Salvage unwanted dungeon drops**: Every dungeon item you don't need can be salvaged for essence at the Salvage NPC in the Dungeon Hub.
- **Dungeon secrets**: Finding secrets in runs can reward Gold and Diamond Essence.
- **Buy from Bazaar**: If you have coins and Catacombs 20, buying essence from the Bazaar is the fastest method.

### Crimson Essence
- **Run Kuudra**: Higher tiers (especially Infernal) give significantly more Crimson Essence per run.
- **Salvage Crimson gear**: Unwanted Kuudra drops can be salvaged.
- The free chest in every Kuudra run always gives Crimson Essence.

### Dragon Essence
- **Kill dragons in the Dragon's Nest**: Participate in dragon fights. Superior and Strong dragons give more.
- **Draconic Altar**: Sacrifice specific items for Dragon Essence.
- **Lost Adventurers**: Kill them when found in dungeons.

### Spider Essence
- **Kill Arachne**: The most efficient source. Use Arachne's Calling or Arachne Crystals to summon her.
- **Spider's Den mobs**: Lonely Spiders and Cellar Spiders drop small amounts.

### Gold and Diamond Essence
- **Crystal Hollows**: Treasure chests, Golden/Diamond Goblins, and Crystal Nucleus completion.
- **Dungeon Secrets**: Some secrets reward Gold or Diamond Essence.

### Ice Essence
- **Winter events**: Fish for Winter Sea Creatures during the Jerry's Workshop event.
- **Frozen Adventurers**: Kill them when they appear.
- Typically the hardest essence to farm since it's mostly seasonal.

### Forest Essence
- **Foraging Islands**: Tree Gifts, Fig/Mangrove harvesting.
- Only essence type buyable on Bazaar without Catacombs 20.

### General Tips
- Always salvage dungeon/Kuudra items you don't need rather than selling to NPC.
- The Cooled Forges perk from the Ice Essence Shop increases essence gained from salvaging by 4% per tier.
- Use the Essence Guide in-game (talk to Malik, Essence Crafting menu) to see all essence uses.
- For expensive upgrades, compare Bazaar essence prices vs farming time to decide the most efficient approach.
- Prioritize starring your most-used gear first. 5-star your main weapon before armor pieces.
