# The Forge

## Overview

The Forge is a location in the Dwarven Mines (coordinates 0, 149, -68) where players can refine materials and craft powerful gear, accessories, and drill components. Heart of the Mountain (HotM) Tier 2 is required to use The Forge. Mining level 15 is required to Fast Travel there.

The Forge can also be accessed remotely via the Abiphone by contacting the NPC Fred.

In the Crystal Hollows, there is a miniature version of The Forge that functions identically.

## Forge Slots

- **HotM Tier 2**: 2 Forge slots (initial unlock)
- **HotM Tier 3**: 3 Forge slots (+1)
- **HotM Tier 4**: 4 Forge slots (+1)
- **Peak of the Mountain Level 2**: 5 Forge slots (+1)

Forging processes cannot be canceled once started.

## Quick Forge Perk (HotM Tier 3)

The Quick Forge perk decreases forge times. It applies dynamically -- leveling it up affects items already forging, and disabling it resets times as if it was never used.

**Formula**: TimeReduction% = min(30, 10 + (Level x 0.5) + floor(Level / 20) x 10)

| Levels | Mithril Powder Cost | Cumulative Cost | Forge Time Reduction |
|--------|-------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | (unlock with Token) | -- | 10.5% |
| 2-10 | 6,745 | 6,745 | 15% |
| 11-19 | 70,068 | 76,813 | 19.5% |
| 20 (max) | 17,025 | 93,838 | **30%** |

**With Blue Cheese Goblin Omelette**: Level 21 is possible, but 30% is the hard cap.

## Cole Mayor Bonus

When Cole is elected as Mayor or Minister with the Molten Forge perk, forge processes started during their term get a further **25% reduction** in forge times. This does NOT apply retroactively to processes started before Cole was elected. This stacks with Quick Forge.

**Maximum combined reduction**: Quick Forge 30% + Cole 25% = ~47.5% total time reduction (multiplicative).

## Refined Materials

These are the foundational forge products used in most advanced recipes.

### Refined Mithril
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 2
- **Materials**: 160 Enchanted Mithril (25,600 raw Mithril)
- **Forge Time**: 6 hours
- **Used In**: Beacons, Mithril Drill SX-R226, Mithril Plate, Pure Mithril, Mithril-Infused Fuel Tank, most higher-tier drills, Secret Railroad Pass

### Refined Diamond
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 2
- **Materials**: 2 Enchanted Diamond Block (51,200 raw Diamond)
- **Forge Time**: 8 hours
- **Used In**: Golden Plate, Drill Motor, Diamonite, Titanium-Infused Fuel Tank, higher-tier Titanium Drills

### Refined Titanium
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 2
- **Materials**: 16 Enchanted Titanium (2,560 raw Titanium)
- **Forge Time**: 12 hours
- **Also Obtained From**: Frozen Corpses (rare drop)
- **Used In**: Titanium accessories, Mithril Plate, drill engines, all Titanium Drills

### Fuel Canister (formerly Fuel Tank)
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 2
- **Materials**: 2 Enchanted Block of Coal (51,200 raw Coal)
- **Forge Time**: 10 hours
- **Used In**: Mithril Drill SX-R226, Mithril-Infused Fuel Tank, Ruby Drill TX-15, Titanium Drill DR-X355, Titanium-Infused Fuel Tank

### Golden Plate
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 3
- **Materials**: 2 Enchanted Gold Block + 1 Refined Diamond + 5 Glacite Jewel
- **Forge Time**: 6 hours
- **Used In**: Mithril Plate, Drill Motor, Amber Material, Mithril Drill SX-R326, Titanium Drill DR-X355

### Mithril Plate
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 3
- **Materials**: 5 Refined Mithril + 1 Golden Plate + 1 Enchanted Iron Block + 1 Refined Titanium
- **Forge Time**: 18 hours
- **Used In**: Drill engines, Mithril Drill SX-R326, Titanium Drills DR-X455/X555/X655, Perfect Plate, Glacite-Plated Chisel

### Drill Motor (formerly Drill Engine)
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 3
- **Materials**: 1 Enchanted Iron Block + 3 Enchanted Redstone Block + 1 Golden Plate + 10 Treasurite + 5 Glacite Jewel
- **Forge Time**: 30 hours (1 day 6 hours)
- **Used In**: Mithril Drill SX-R226, Titanium Drill DR-X355, Ruby Drill TX-15, all Drill Engines

### Pure Mithril (Reforge Stone)
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 3
- **Materials**: 2 Refined Mithril
- **Forge Time**: 12 hours
- **Use**: Applies "Mithraic" reforge to pickaxes (Mining Speed + 10% chance for extra Mithril)

### Diamonite (Reforge Stone)
- **HotM Requirement**: Tier 2
- **Materials**: 3 Refined Diamond
- **Forge Time**: 6 hours
- **Use**: Applies "Fleet" reforge to pickaxes (Mining Speed + Block Fortune + Mining Spread on Blocks)

## Fuel Consumption

- **Most blocks**: 1 fuel per block
- **Aquamarine, Citrine, Onyx, Peridot Gemstones**: 2 fuel per block
- **Glacite, Umber, Tungsten**: 3 fuel per block

Fuel is replenished by adding fuel items to the drill via the Drill Anvil.

## Forge Progression Tips

1. **Start with Refined Materials**: At HotM 2, begin forging Refined Mithril, Refined Diamond, Refined Titanium, and Fuel Canisters. These are the building blocks for everything.

2. **Use All Forge Slots**: Always keep all forge slots active. Queue up refined materials in parallel since most items take 6-12 hours.

3. **Max Quick Forge Early**: Level Quick Forge to 20 (costs ~93,838 Mithril Powder total) for the 30% time reduction. This saves days of waiting on big forges.

4. **Wait for Cole**: If possible, start long forge processes when Cole is Mayor for the extra 25% time reduction.

5. **Mithril Drill SX-R226 First**: This is the first drill and grants +20% Mithril Powder. It also unlocks access to Drill Parts (Fuel Tanks, Engines, Upgrade Modules), which are a major power boost.

6. **Skip the R326**: The Mithril Drill SX-R326 is generally not recommended -- it is very expensive for its stats. Jump to the Titanium Drill DR-X355 instead.

7. **Gemstone Line for Crystal Hollows**: If progressing through Crystal Hollows, use the Gemstone Drill line (Ruby -> Gemstone -> Topaz -> Jasper) since they are cheaper and grant Pristine + Gemstone Fortune bonuses.

8. **Titanium Line for Dwarven Mines**: For Dwarven Mines progression, the Titanium Drills offer the best general mining stats.

9. **DR-X655 is the Sweet Spot**: The Titanium Drill DR-X655 is widely considered the best value endgame drill. It has a Topaz gemstone slot (unlike its predecessors), making it versatile for all mining.

10. **Divan's Drill is a Luxury**: It requires a Divan's Alloy (extremely rare Crystal Hollows drop) plus 50M coins. Only pursue after completing most other mining upgrades.

11. **Drill Parts Matter**: A lower-tier drill with good Drill Parts can outperform a higher-tier drill with no parts. Prioritize getting at least a Mithril-Infused Fuel Tank and Mithril-Plated Drill Engine early.

12. **Fuel Management**: Use Pesto Goblin Omelette (75% fuel save) or Goblin Omelette (25% fuel save) as Upgrade Modules to extend drill usage. The Spicy Goblin Omelette (+20% all powder) is best for powder grinding.

13. **Parallel Forging**: With max Quick Forge and 5 slots, you can dramatically cut total forge times. For example, the Titanium Drill DR-X355's total material forge time drops from ~16 days to ~2.3 days.
