# Chocolate Factory

## Overview
The Chocolate Factory is a Cookie Clicker-style minigame in Hypixel Skyblock, accessible via `/cf`, `/factory`, or `/chocolatefactory`. Players earn Chocolate currency through clicking and passive production, then spend it on upgrades, employees, rabbits, and shop items. It is closely tied to the seasonal Hoppity's Hunt event.

## Core Mechanics
- Click the chocolate rabbit in the UI to earn chocolate (1 per click base, up to 10 with Hand-Baked Chocolate upgrade)
- Hire and upgrade employees for passive Chocolate per Second (CpS)
- Collect unique Chocolate Rabbits for CpS bonuses and multipliers
- Prestige through Chocolate Factory levels (CF1 through CF6) for higher caps and multipliers
- The Rabbit Barn stores unique rabbits and is NOT reset on prestige

## Chocolate per Second (CpS) Sources
- Employee levels (primary source)
- Hoppity's Collection rabbits (base CpS + multiplier)
- Factory level multiplier (1x at CF1, up to 2.5x at CF6)
- Time Tower (temporary boost)
- Coach Jackrabbit (permanent multiplier)
- Booster Cookie (+0.25x CpS multiplier while active)
- Rabbit Pet (+0.05x CpS multiplier)
- Chocolate accessories (+10 to +50 base CpS from 5 accessories)

## Upgrades

### Hand-Baked Chocolate
- Increases chocolate per click from 1 to 10 (max level 10)
- Cost: 500 per level, total 22,500 chocolate
- Available from CF1

### Time Tower (CF2+)
- Temporarily boosts CpS with a multiplier
- Charges build over 8 hours each (7 hours with Einstein rabbit)
- Maximum 3 charges stored
- Each activation lasts 1 hour
- Multiplier: +0.1x per level, up to +1.5x at level 15
- With Mu Rabbit: additional +0.7x bonus
- Total cost across all levels: approximately 1-1.4 billion chocolate

### Rabbit Shrine (CF3+)
- Increases chance of higher rarity rabbits during Hoppity's Hunt by 2% per level
- 20 levels total
- Total cost: approximately 5.56 billion chocolate (10m to 700m per level)

### Coach Jackrabbit (CF4+)
- Permanent always-active CpS multiplier
- +0.01x per level, up to +0.2x at level 20
- Total cost: approximately 3.35 billion chocolate

### Rabbit Barn
- Determines maximum number of unique Chocolate Rabbits you can hold
- NOT reset upon prestige (only upgrade that persists)
- Cost per level: 5,000 x 1.05^(L-1) chocolate
- Essential to upgrade early and often

## Rabbit System

### How to Get Rabbits
1. **Chocolate Eggs** during Hoppity's Hunt (primary source)
2. **Hoppity NPC** sells up to 7 rabbits per SkyBlock year
3. **Factory Milestones** (lifetime chocolate collected)
4. **Shop Milestones** (chocolate spent in shop)
5. **Stray Rabbits** that appear in the factory UI
6. **Hoppity Abiphone calls** (2% daily chance, offers Common-Epic rabbits)

## Stray and Golden Rabbits
Stray rabbits appear in the Chocolate Factory UI for 20 seconds. Click them for chocolate based on rarity (5s to 50s of current CpS).

Golden Rabbits are special stray variants with powerful bonuses:
- **Side Dish**: Opens a chocolate egg
- **Jackpot**: 480x current CpS bonus
- **Chocolate Mountain**: 1,500x current CpS bonus
- **El Dorado**: Small bonus + legendary unlock on 3rd catch
- **Stampede**: Spawns 10 stray rabbits at once
- **Golden Click**: +5 CpS for the rest of the SkyBlock year

## Hoppity's Hunt (Seasonal Event)

### Overview
Hoppity's Hunt is a seasonal event beginning on Early Spring 1st, lasting approximately 3 in-game months. During this event, Chocolate Eggs spawn around the world that players can find and claim for Chocolate Rabbits.

### Egg Types and Spawn Times
| Egg Type | Color | Real-Time Spawn |
|----------|-------|-----------------|
| Breakfast Egg | Gold | 7 AM (XX:00:50 in-game) |
| Brunch Egg | Gold | 7 AM window |
| Lunch Egg | Blue | 2 PM (XX:06:40 in-game) |
| Dejeuner Egg | Blue | 2 PM window |
| Dinner Egg | Green | 9 PM (XX:12:30 in-game) |
| Supper Egg | Green | 9 PM window |

Eggs spawn every 20 real-time minutes across all islands. Maximum 6 eggs active at once; when a new egg spawns, the oldest disappears. Over the full event, approximately 279 total eggs spawn.

### Egg Locations
- Eggs spawn on every island and are synced across servers (you cannot collect the same spawn on multiple servers)
- Crystal Hollows Crystal Nucleus is a popular spot for concentrated egg spawns
- Gold Mine is a good early-game alternative
- Use the Egglocator item (7.5m from Chocolate Shop) to help locate eggs

### Rabbit Hitman
If you miss a Chocolate Egg, the Rabbit Hitman can collect it for you:
- Stores missed eggs in slots (28 total slots available)
- Each claimed Hitman egg spawns a Golden Rabbit approximately 30 seconds later
- 20-minute cooldown per slot
- Total cost for all 28 slots: approximately 291.6 million chocolate
- Some slots locked behind CF level requirements

### Duplicate Rabbit Rewards
When you find a duplicate rabbit or your barn is full, you receive chocolate instead:
- Formula: CpS x Base Amount x (1 + min(Duplicates, 10) x 0.1)
- Each duplicate of the same rabbit adds +10% bonus, capping at +100% for the 10th duplicate

| Rare | 750s |
| Epic | 1,500s |
| Legendary | 4,000s |

| Divine | 15,000s |

## Hoppity NPC
- Located on the Mountain
- Sells up to 7 randomly selected Chocolate Rabbits per SkyBlock year (Common through Legendary)
- Prices: Common 200-250k, Uncommon 500k, Rare 1m, Epic 2.5m, Legendary 5m
- Duplicate purchases grant chocolate based on rarity and your CpS
- Hoppity may call via Abiphone (2% daily chance) offering rabbits, including the exclusive "Abi" uncommon rabbit

## Optimization Strategy

### Early Game (CF1-CF2)
1. Click actively to earn your first 50 chocolate and hire Rabbit Bro
2. Max Hand-Baked Chocolate early (only 22,500 total)
3. Level Rabbit Bro and Cousin first for cheap CpS
4. Buy Egglocator (7.5m) during Hoppity's Hunt for finding eggs
5. Upgrade Rabbit Barn whenever affordable (persists through prestige)
6. Rush to CF2 at 150m lifetime chocolate

### Mid Game (CF2-CF4)
1. Unlock and level Time Tower immediately at CF2 for CpS boost
2. Buy Nibble Chocolate Stick and Smooth Chocolate Bar accessories
3. At CF3, invest in Rabbit Shrine for better egg rarities
4. Upgrade employees evenly, prioritizing highest CpS-per-cost ratio
5. At CF4, start Coach Jackrabbit for permanent multiplier
6. Buy Rich Chocolate Chunk and Ganache Chocolate Slab accessories
7. Collect rabbits actively during Hoppity's Hunt

### Late Game (CF5-CF6)
1. Max Coach Jackrabbit and Time Tower
2. Keep Time Tower active as much as possible
3. Complete Hoppity's Collection for maximum CpS and multiplier bonuses
4. Buy Chocolate Fortune repeatedly (up to 25 times at 2b each)
5. Target mythic rabbits: Einstein, Mu, King, and Sigma are highest priority
6. Purchase endgame cosmetics (Chocolate Dye at 40b, Zorro's Cape at 20b)

### General Tips
- Always keep your Rabbit Barn upgraded ahead of your collection
- During Hoppity's Hunt, prioritize finding every egg spawn
- Upgrade Rabbit Hitman slots to catch eggs you miss
- The Rabbit Shrine significantly helps get rarer rabbits from eggs
- Duplicates are still valuable as they convert to chocolate
- Einstein rabbit is one of the most impactful mythics (faster Time Tower charges)
- Maximum theoretical CpS is approximately 52,922 with all upgrades, collection complete, and Time Tower active
