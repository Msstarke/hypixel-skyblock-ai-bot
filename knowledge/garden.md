# The Garden

The Garden is a personal farming island unlocked at SkyBlock Level 5. It features 24 plots in a 5x5 grid (with the Barn at center), each 96x96 blocks. It is the primary location for farming, composting, crop milestones, and visitor trades.

## Unlocking and Access

- Requires SkyBlock Level 5
- Accessed via the SkyBlock menu or fast travel
- Separate from Private Island (farming fortune does not apply on Private Island)
- The Desk (`/desk`) is the central hub for managing plots, crop upgrades, SkyMart, and milestones

## Garden Levels

The Garden has 15 levels (I-XV). Each level grants 10 SkyBlock XP and progressively unlocks new crops, plots, visitors, pests, and crop growth speed bonuses.

| Level | Max Visitors | Crops Available | Pest Types | Crop Growth Bonus |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|------------|-------------------|
| I | - | 1 (Wheat) | - | - |
| V | 78 | 5 | 6 | +40% |
| X | 120 | 10 | 11 | +90% |
| XV | 137 | 13 | 14 | +140% |

Garden XP is earned from crop milestones and completing visitor offers.

### Crop Growth

- Water is mandatory for crop growth; light is not required
- Dry soil: 0% growth
- Partially moist soil: -40% growth speed penalty
- Nether Wart and Mushroom receive an additional growth boost
- Garden level increases crop growth speed (up to +140% at level XV)

## Plots

24 plots total, unlocked with Compost. Each unlocked plot grants +3 Farming Fortune and +5 SkyBlock XP.

| Tier | Garden Level | Plots |
|------|-------------|-------|
| A | I | 1-4 |
| B | III | 5-8 |
| C | V | 9-20 |
| D | VII | 21-24 |

All 24 plots unlocked = +72 Farming Fortune. Total cost to unlock all plots is approximately 12,103 Compost equivalent. Plots must be cleaned (remove dirt, grass, stone, flowers, wood, leaves) before use.

### Building Restrictions
- Maximum height: 10 blocks per plot
- Teleport/Launch Pads disabled
- Cannot set spawn with beds
- TNT limit: 64 simultaneous entities
- Fire ticking disabled

## Crop Milestones

Each crop has 46 milestone tiers. Only crops harvested in the Garden count (not Private Island or Barn). Pest crop drops also count. Each tier rewards Garden XP, Farming XP, SkyBlock XP, and sometimes Copper.

## Farming Fortune

Farming Fortune determines extra crop drops. The hundreds digit represents guaranteed extra drops; the remainder is the percentage chance for one additional drop. Example: 1,250 Fortune = 12 guaranteed extra drops + 50% chance for a 13th. Only works in the Garden (not Private Island).

## Garden Visitors

137 unique visitor NPCs offer trades in the Garden. They provide Farming XP, Garden XP, Copper, Bits, and unique item rewards.

### Spawn Mechanics
- Maximum 5 visible at once (6th queues hidden)
- Base spawn interval: 15 minutes
- 50+ unique served: 12 minutes
- 100+ unique served: 9 minutes
- Actively farming crops speeds spawns by 3x multiplier
- Mayor Finnegan's Blooming Business: -25% spawn time

## Jacob's Farming Contest

Competitive farming event running every 3 SkyBlock days (~1 hour real time). Three random crops are selected per contest. Duration: 20 minutes.

### Requirements
- Farming skill level X (10)
- Must speak with Jacob once
- Must collect at least 100 crops

### Medal Thresholds

| Medal | Top % | Tickets | Bits |
|-------|-------|---------|------|
| Participation | 100 crops | 1 | - |
| Bronze | 60% | 10 | 40 |
| Silver | 30% | 15 | 50 |
| Gold | 10% | 25 | 60 |
| Platinum | 5% | 30 | 70 |
| Diamond | 2% | 35 | 80 |

Mayor Finnegan's GOATed perk increases bracket inclusion by up to 10% (e.g., Gold becomes top 20%).

### Farming Cap Increase
Earning a Gold medal on a crop allows increasing the Farming level cap by 1 (up to 60). Requires Jacob's Tickets with increasing costs (up to 500 tickets).

## Pests

14 pest types that spawn when breaking crops in the Garden.

Types: Fly, Cricket, Locust, Rat, Field Mouse, Mosquito, Earthworm, Mite, Moth, Slug, Beetle, Firefly, Dragonfly, Praying Mantis.

- 4+ pests on a plot reduce Farming Fortune (up to 8 pests)
- Can be exchanged at Pesthunter Phillip for a temporary Farming Fortune bonus (up to +200 for 30 minutes)
- Pest crop drops count toward Crop Milestones

## Greenhouse

- Unlocked by completing the Carpenter visitor's first offer (requires Garden Level XII)
- Rewards: Greenhouse Blueprint
- Allows growing crops and plant mutations
- Up to 3 greenhouse plots available

## SkyMart

Shop at the Desk that sells farming tools, equipment, and items for Copper. Key purchases include crop-specific hoes (250 Copper each) and various farming equipment.

## Notable NPCs

- **Jacob** - Runs farming contests
- **Anita** - Sells farming cap increases and fortune accessories for Jacob's Tickets
- **Pesthunter Phillip** - Exchanges pest kills for temporary fortune boost
- **Pesthunter Pamela** - Pest-related services
- **Carpenter** - Visitor who unlocks the Greenhouse
- **Sam** - Garden services
- **Jeff** - Garden services
