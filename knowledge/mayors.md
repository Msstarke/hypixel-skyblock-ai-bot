# Mayors and Elections

## How Elections Work

### Election Cycle
- A mayor election is held every SkyBlock year, which lasts 124 real-life hours (approximately 5 days and 4 hours).
- Five candidates appear each election. Players vote, and the candidate with the most votes becomes Mayor for the next SkyBlock year.
- The runner-up becomes the Minister, activating one of their perks (the "minister perk") alongside the Mayor's perks.
- The current Mayor and Minister cannot participate in the following election (exception: if the Mayor is Diaz with "Long Term Investment" perk, the Minister appears as a candidate with all their perks).

### Election Timeline
- Five candidates are revealed when the election starts on Late Summer 27th 00:00.
- The election ends on Late Spring 27th 00:00 of the following year.
- The winning mayor's perks are active from the end of the election until the end of the next election.

### Voting Mechanics
- Players vote by entering the Election Room via the teleport pad in the Community Center.
- Each account gets one vote (not per profile).
- Vote weight is increased by the player's Fame Rank.
- Vote weight is also increased by Voter's Badge and its upgrades.
- Vote weight is doubled with the Artifact of Control.
- Players may change their vote while the booth is open.

### Perk Distribution
- Regular candidates have up to 4 possible perks.
- New candidates start with 1 random perk in their first election.
- If a candidate was NOT elected, their previous perks persist. Before the next election, they have a 50% chance to gain an additional perk. This chance increases to 100% if they did not gain a perk in the last election they appeared in.
- If a candidate WAS elected (served as mayor), they reset to 1 random perk the next time they appear.
- One random perk of each regular candidate is selected as their Minister perk.
- Special candidates always run with ALL of their perks.

### Candidate Selection
- Regular candidates are chosen randomly (5 per election when no special candidate runs, 4 when a special candidate runs).
- The running mayor cannot participate in the election during their term.
- Special candidates appear every 8 SkyBlock years (when the SkyBlock year is divisible by 8), in a rotating sequence.

## Regular Mayors

### Aatrox (Slayer Mayor)
**Focus:** Slayer content

**Perks:**
- **SLASHED Pricing** - Starting Slayer quests is half price.
- **Slayer XP Buff** - Earn 25% more Slayer XP.
- **Pathfinder** - Gain rare drops 20% more often from Slayer bosses. The Magic Find formula becomes: NewMagicFind = (OldMagicFind x 1.2) + 20.

**Best for:** Slayer grinding, farming Slayer boss rare drops (RNGesus drops). Pathfinder is the most valuable perk, significantly boosting rare drop chances from Slayer bosses.

---

### Cole (Mining Mayor)
**Focus:** Mining

**Perks:**
- **Prospection** - Mining minions work 25% faster.
- **Mining XP Buff** - Get +60 Mining Wisdom on public islands.
- **Mining Fiesta** - Schedules 5 Mining Fiesta events lasting 7 SkyBlock days each. During events: +75 Mining Wisdom, 2x drops from mining, and chances for Refined Minerals and Glossy Gemstones.
- **Molten Forge** - Decrease forge times by 25%. Only applies when the forging process is started (not retroactive).

**Best for:** Mining XP, gemstone farming, forge optimization. Mining Fiesta is especially valuable for its 2x drops and Refined Mineral/Glossy Gemstone drops.

---

### Diana (Pet / Mythological Mayor)
**Focus:** Pets and Mythological Creatures

**Perks:**
- **Huntress' Intuition** - Gain +10 Tracking.
- **Lucky!** - Gain +25 Pet Luck.
- **Mythological Ritual** - Diana sells the Griffin Pet (25,000 coins) and the Ancestral Spade (5,000 coins + 32 Gold Ingots). The Griffin Pet lets you find Mythological Creatures and unique items.
- **Pet XP Buff** - Gain +35% more pet XP.
- **Sharing is Caring** - You can have up to 3 EXP Shared Pets active at once, and your EXP Share rate is increased by 10%.

**Best for:** Mythological creature hunting (extremely profitable), pet leveling, pet luck. Diana was the first mayor ever elected (Year 88). Mythological Ritual is one of the most sought-after perks for money-making.

---

### Diaz (Economy Mayor)
**Focus:** Coins and economy

**Perks:**
- **Long Term Investment** - The elected Minister will appear in the next Election with all of their available perks.
- **Volume Trading** - Double the item quantity from Shen's Auction, Shen's Special, and Rift Shen's. Two additional Shen's Special auctions become available.
- **Stock Exchange** - Participate in the Stonks Auction for a chance to win Stock of Stonks. Trade them for extravagant items at the Trade Center.
- **Shopping Spree** - Increase daily NPC buy limits by 10x. Also applies to trades.

**Best for:** Economy manipulation, NPC shop buying (especially for Ironman profiles buying hard-to-get NPC items like Dandelions, Sponge, Wet Sponge), Stock of Stonks farming. Diaz is excluded from Jerry's Perkpocalypse rotation.

---

### Finnegan (Farming Mayor)
**Focus:** Farming

**Perks:**
- **Pelt-pocalypse** - Obtain 1.5x more pelts from Trevor in the Mushroom Desert, hunt a new trapper mob, and purchase items from Talbot. Stacks with Pelt Belt and Jake's Plushie (all drops get +2, then x1.5).
- **GOATed** - Jacob's Farming Contest brackets include up to 10% more players each (5% for Platinum, 3% for Diamond).
- **Blooming Business** - Garden Visitors give out Fine Flour and appear more often. 5 additional unique visitors may visit your Garden. Higher rarity visitors are 20% more likely to show up and provide 10% more Copper.
- **Pest Eradicator** - Pesthunter Phillip's Farming Fortune bonus duration is now 60 minutes. Decreases the spawn cooldown of Pests by 20%.

**Best for:** Farming contests (GOATed), Garden optimization (Blooming Business), pelt grinding, pest farming. Finnegan was added in the 0.16.1 Update and first appeared in Year 241.

---

### Foxy (Events Mayor)
**Focus:** Events

**Perks:**
- **Sweet Benevolence** - Earn +30% more Candy, Gifts, and Chocolate from duplicate Rabbits during their respective events (Spooky Festival, gifts events, Hoppity's Hunt).
- **Extra Event** - Schedules one additional event during the year. Can be: Spooky Festival, Mining Fiesta, or Fishing Festival.
- **A Time for Giving** - Party Chests and Party Gifts can be obtained while active.
- **Chivalrous Carnival** - Schedules a Carnival in the Hub that is active throughout the entire year.

**Best for:** Event-focused play, getting extra event rewards. Foxy is the only regular mayor NOT included in Jerry's Perkpocalypse rotation.

---

### Marina (Fishing Mayor)
**Focus:** Fishing

**Perks:**
- **Fishing XP Buff** - Gain +50 Fishing Wisdom on public islands.
- **Luck of the Sea 2.0** - Gain +15 Sea Creature Chance.
- **Fishing Festival** - Starts a special fishing event during the first 3 days of each month. Fish and fight dangerous Sharks and earn unique Shark loot (including Rod of the Sea).
- **Double Trouble** - For every 1 Sea Creature Chance, gain +0.1 Double Hook Chance.

**Best for:** Fishing XP, sea creature grinding, Shark fishing during Fishing Festival. Marina is one of the few mayors that gives direct stat bonuses.

---

### Paul (Dungeons Mayor)
**Focus:** Dungeons (Catacombs)

**Perks:**
- **Marauder** - Dungeon reward chests are 20% cheaper.
- **EZPZ** - Gain 10 extra bonus score in Dungeons. With this perk, an S+ run on F6 or F7 may have a maximum of 9 deaths (10 with a Legendary Spirit Pet).
- **Benediction** - Blessings are 25% stronger.

**Best for:** Dungeon grinding, cheaper reward chests, easier S+ scores. Paul only has 3 possible perks (fewer than most regular mayors).

## Special Mayors

Special mayors appear every 8 SkyBlock years (approximately 41.3 real-life days) in a rotating sequence. They always run with all of their perks. When a Special Mayor is elected, there is no Minister.

### Scorpius (Dark Auction Mayor)
**Focus:** Dark Auction, bribery

**Perks (always all active):**
- **Bribe** - If Scorpius wins and you voted for him, he offers coins based on your profile playtime:
  - 0-50 hours: 50,000 coins
  - 50-100 hours: 100,000 coins
  - 100-150 hours: 250,000 coins
  - 150-300 hours: 500,000 coins
  - 300+ hours: 1,000,000 coins
  - Bribe amount is based on playtime at the time of CLAIMING, not voting.
- **Darker Auctions** - Dark Auctions have 7 rounds instead of 4, with special items:
  - Round 1 & 3: Ender Artifact, Wither Artifact, Nether Artifact, Spirit Mask, Midas' Sword, Epic/Legendary Parrot/Turtle/Jellyfish Pets, Travel Scroll to Dark Auction, Dark Purple Dye (0.03%), Eccentric Painting Bundle.
  - Round 2 & 4: Enchanted Books (Sharpness 7, Giant Killer 7, Protection 7, Growth 7, Power 7, Counter Strike 3, Big Brain bundle, Vicious bundle) — 12.5% each.
  - Round 5: Plasma Nucleus (50%) or Hegemony Artifact (50%).
  - Round 6: Midas Staff (100%).
  - Round 7: Flower Minion (100%).

**Best for:** Free coins (vote for him!), Dark Auction farming for rare items. No special mayor has ever lost an election. Scorpius has the fewest perks of any mayor (only 2).

---

### Derpy (Chaos Mayor)
**Focus:** Chaos -- doubles rewards but makes the game harder

**Perks (always all active):**
- **QUAD TAXES!!!** - Pay 4x the normal amount of taxes. Only affects the Auction House tax on items sold over 1 million coins (does not affect time/listing fees).
- **TURBO MINIONS!!!** - Minions have double the output.
- **DOUBLE MOBS HP!!!** - ALL monsters have double health. Does NOT affect the Magma Boss (but does affect Magma Glares and Unstable Magmas).
- **MOAR SKILLZ!!!** - Gain +50% more skill experience. Also affects Catacombs and Gabagool Pet experience.

**Best for:** Skill XP grinding (+50% is massive), minion output. Avoid if you rely on combat or mob farming. Plan around the doubled mob HP and quadrupled taxes. Derpy was the first candidate to have 4 perks.

**Tips:**
- Use Derpy's term to grind skills that don't involve killing mobs (farming, mining, fishing, foraging).
- Minion output doubling makes it a great time for minion-based money making.
- Avoid listing expensive items on AH due to 4x taxes.
- Dungeons become harder with doubled mob HP, so plan accordingly.

---

### Jerry (Perkpocalypse Mayor)
**Focus:** Rotating perks from other mayors, stat boosts, hidden Jerries

**Perks (always all active):**
- **Perkpocalypse** - Activates all perks of another mayor every 18 SkyBlock days (6 real-life hours). The rotation includes: Aatrox, Cole, Diana, Finnegan, Marina, and Paul (NOT Foxy or Diaz). The order is randomized each time Jerry is elected.
- **Statspocalypse** - Increases all Combat and Miscellaneous stats by 10% (additive). Affects: Health, Defense, Strength, Intelligence, Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Bonus Attack Speed, Ability Damage, True Defense, Ferocity, Speed, Magic Find, Pet Luck, Sea Creature Chance, Cold Resistance, Heat Resistance.
- **Jerrypocalypse** - Mining, farming, felling trees, and killing mobs has a small chance to spawn Hidden Jerries (Green, Blue, Purple, or Golden). Killing them drops Jerry Boxes. 6-minute cooldown between spawns. Also triggers on Private Islands. Requires 4+ hours of profile playtime.

**Best for:** Getting access to Diana's Mythological Ritual or other desired mayor perks on a rotating basis. The 10% stat boost is universally beneficial. Jerry Boxes can contain Green Jerry Talisman, Jerry Stone, Jerry Rune, Jerry Candy, Jerry 3D Glasses, and Jerry-chine Gun.

## Retired / One-Time Mayors

### Barry the Wizard (Retired Regular)
**Focus:** Enchanting and Alchemy (no longer a candidate as of Year 224)

**Perks (when he was active):**
- **Arcane Catalyst** - Spells deal 15% increased damage. Secret Bonus: All abilities cost 15% less Mana.
- **Astral Negotiator** - Enchanting and anvils cost -15% less experience. Secret Bonus: Gain +1 Magic Find for every year Barry has not been elected (max 20).
- **Magic XP Boost** - Gain +15 Alchemy Wisdom and Enchanting Wisdom. Secret Bonus: Gain +2 Clicks in SuperPairs Experiments.

Barry was the last regular candidate to ever be elected (Year 189, the 101st election). He was retired in August 2022 and now lives in the Rift Dimension at Barry HQ.

---

### Dante (One-Time Mayor)
**Focus:** Tyranny / The Uprising event

Dante appeared in the Year 128 election, replacing Derpy's scheduled special appearance. His listed perks were "Order," "Security," and "Freedom," but when elected, his real perks were revealed:

- **Economy Restoration** - Double AH and Bazaar taxes, 10% coin tax on shops, 10 coins per minute for chatting, 2 coin toll on travelling between islands.
- **Increase Security** - Hired Goons blocked access to official buildings (unless wearing Happy Mask or holding Dante Talisman), cancelled events (Traveling Zoo, Spooky Festival, Season of Jerry), replaced elections with Dante Elections (only Dante could win), set time to permanent midnight.

Before The Uprising, Dante also banned Dungeons, disabled Pets, disbanded the Dwarven Mines alliance, disabled AH/Bazaar, and banned island travel. All changes were reverted when Technoblade became mayor after The Uprising.

Dante is the only candidate to have swapped perks after the election and the only one-time mayor to have replaced a scheduled special candidate.

---

### Technoblade (One-Time Mayor)
**Focus:** Post-Uprising celebration

Technoblade became mayor following The Uprising against Dante. He had 5 perks (technically 11), making him the only mayor with that many:

- **Blood God's Blessing** - +50% Skill XP.
- **Shrine of the Blood God** - Summoned the Blood God Shrine and Shiny Pigs (continued after his term since not all relics were found).
- **Unlimited Speed** - +50 Speed and removed the speed cap (was bugged and removed mid-term).
- **Potato Crown's Radiance** - Farming Minions have double output (was bugged and worked on all minions).
- **Anarchy** - No taxes in the Auction House and Bazaar (removed shortly after election to prevent inflation).

---

### Aura (One-Time Mayor)
**Focus:** Redistribution

Aura appeared in Year 458-468, replacing Derpy's scheduled special appearance. Listed perks were "Universal Income," "Increased Loot," and "Increased Experience," but actual perks were:

- **Fundraising** - Messages cost 7 coins each, Fast Travel costs 2,000 coins (except Hub/Private Island/Garden), Bazaar tax and Slayer spawn costs doubled, Kat upgrades cost 50% more, certain Dungeon drop costs increased (e.g. Necron's Handle from 100M to 265M).
- **Minion Union** - Actually reduced minion speed by 33% (was disabled by a minister vote).
- **Work Better** - Granted +50 Magic Find and +25 Tracking globally (except Rift). All RNG Meter progress doubled, base drop rates of several items increased (Necron's Handle, Divan's Alloy, Quick Claw, etc.).
- **Work Harder** - Increased HP and damage of mobs by 10% (was toggled on/off by minister votes).
- **Work Smarter** - Gain +50% Skill EXP, Dungeon EXP and Slayer EXP (was toggled on/off by minister votes).

Featured a unique Minister Election system where community members became ministers and could enable/disable perks via votes.

## Mayor Recommendations by Activity

### Mining
- **Best:** Cole (Mining Fiesta for 2x drops, +60/+75 Mining Wisdom, faster forge, faster mining minions)
- **Also good:** Derpy (MOAR SKILLZ for Mining XP), Jerry (when Cole perks rotate in via Perkpocalypse)

### Combat / Slayer
- **Best:** Aatrox (half-price quests, +25% Slayer XP, Pathfinder for rare drops)
- **Also good:** Jerry (Statspocalypse +10% combat stats, Perkpocalypse with Aatrox rotation)
- **Avoid:** Derpy (doubled mob HP makes everything harder)

### Farming
- **Best:** Finnegan (GOATed for contest brackets, Blooming Business for Garden visitors, Pelt-pocalypse, Pest Eradicator)
- **Also good:** Derpy (MOAR SKILLZ for Farming XP, TURBO MINIONS for farming minions)

### Fishing
- **Best:** Marina (Fishing Festival for Sharks, +15 Sea Creature Chance, +50 Fishing Wisdom, Double Trouble for Double Hook)
- **Also good:** Jerry (when Marina perks rotate in), Foxy (Extra Event can be Fishing Festival)

### Dungeons
- **Best:** Paul (20% cheaper chests, +10 bonus score, 25% stronger Blessings)
- **Also good:** Jerry (when Paul perks rotate in via Perkpocalypse)
- **Avoid:** Derpy (doubled mob HP in dungeons)

### Pets
- **Best:** Diana (+35% pet XP, +25 Pet Luck, Sharing is Caring for EXP Share)

### Money Making
- **Best:** Diana with Mythological Ritual (burrows are extremely profitable)
- **Also good:** Scorpius (Darker Auctions, free bribe coins), Diaz (Shopping Spree for NPC flipping, Stock Exchange)
- **Derpy:** Good for minion output but bad for AH taxes

### Skill XP
- **Best:** Derpy (+50% all skill XP, including Catacombs)
- **Also good:** Technoblade (was +50% when active), specific mayors for their skill (Cole for Mining, Marina for Fishing)

### Events
- **Best:** Foxy (Extra Event, Sweet Benevolence for +30% event loot, Carnival, Party Chests)

## Tips for Taking Advantage of Mayors

1. **Plan around the election cycle.** Elections last ~5 days. Know when special mayors are coming (every 8 SkyBlock years) and plan your activities accordingly.

2. **Vote for Scorpius when he appears.** No special mayor has ever lost. Voting for Scorpius guarantees you a bribe of up to 1,000,000 coins with 300+ hours of playtime. Always claim the bribe.

3. **Stockpile for Derpy.** When Derpy is mayor, focus on skill grinding (not combat) to take advantage of +50% skill XP. Queue up minion production for the 2x output. Avoid selling expensive items on AH (4x taxes). Avoid combat-heavy content due to doubled mob HP.

4. **Use Diana for Mythological Ritual money.** When Diana has Mythological Ritual, buy the Griffin Pet and Ancestral Spade. Dig burrows in the Hub for extremely valuable drops. This is one of the best money-making methods in the game.

5. **Track Jerry's Perkpocalypse rotation.** Jerry rotates through Aatrox, Cole, Diana, Finnegan, Marina, and Paul every 6 hours. Plan your activities around when the mayor you want is active. Diana's Mythological Ritual during Perkpocalypse is especially valuable.

6. **Cole's Molten Forge timing.** The 25% forge time reduction only applies when you START a forge, not retroactively. Start long forges during Cole's term.

7. **Marina's Fishing Festival.** The festival runs the first 3 days of each SkyBlock month. Fish for Sharks to get unique loot including the Rod of the Sea.

8. **Finnegan for Garden optimization.** Blooming Business brings unique visitors and Fine Flour. GOATed makes farming contests easier by expanding bracket sizes.

9. **Paul for dungeon runs.** The 20% cheaper chests save significant coins over many runs. EZPZ's +10 bonus score makes S+ easier to achieve. Benediction's 25% stronger Blessings improve dungeon performance.

10. **Minister perks matter.** The runner-up becomes Minister and activates one perk. Check which minister perk is active, as it can provide additional benefits alongside the Mayor's perks.

11. **Fame Rank increases vote power.** Spend Bits and Gems in the Community Shop to increase your Fame Rank, making your vote count for more.

12. **Diaz for Ironman profiles.** Shopping Spree (10x NPC buy limits) is extremely valuable for Ironman profiles to buy hard-to-get NPC items like Dandelions, Sponge, and Wet Sponge.

## Special Mayor Rotation Schedule

Special candidates appear every 8 SkyBlock years in this rotating sequence:
1. Scorpius
2. Derpy
3. Jerry

The sequence repeats. A special candidate joins when the SkyBlock year is divisible by 8. The rotation was disrupted once by Dante (Year 128, replacing Derpy) and once by Aura (Year 464, replacing Derpy).

---

## Key Facts
- Mayor Elections were added in the 0.9 Update (September 8, 2020), with the first election in SkyBlock Year 88.
- There are currently 9 regular candidates (Aatrox, Cole, Diana, Diaz, Finnegan, Foxy, Marina, Paul, and one slot formerly held by Barry).
- There are 3 active special candidates (Scorpius, Derpy, Jerry).
- Barry was retired in August 2022. Finnegan was added in November 2022.
- Dante and Technoblade were one-time mayors related to The Uprising event.
- Aura was a one-time mayor added in November 2025.
- No special mayor has ever lost an election.
- If no mayor is elected for any reason, Nancy appears as a placeholder.
