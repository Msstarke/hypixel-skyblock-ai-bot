# Potions & Brewing Guide

## Brewing Mechanics

### Brewing Stand
- Purchasable from the Alchemist NPC for 30 coins, or craftable
- Place water bottles/awkward potions in lower slots, ingredients in upper slot
- Brewing cycle takes 20 seconds per batch
- **No Blaze Powder required** (unlike vanilla Minecraft)
- Custom UI shows remaining brew time on hover

### Brewing Process
1. Start with Water Bottles or Awkward Potions (Nether Wart + Water Bottle)
2. Add a base ingredient to create the potion at its starting tier
3. Optionally apply ONE modifier from each category: Level, Duration, Splash

### Potion Modifiers

**Level Modifiers** (increase potion tier):
| Item | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Glowstone Dust | +1 level |
| Enchanted Glowstone Dust | +2 levels |
| Enchanted Glowstone | +3 levels |

**Duration Modifiers** (extend potion time):
| Item | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Redstone | +8 minutes base |
| Enchanted Redstone | +16 minutes base |
| Enchanted Redstone Block | +40 minutes base |
| Enchanted Redstone Lamp | +16 minutes AND +3 levels |

**Splash Modifiers** (make throwable):
| Item | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Gunpowder | Splash, -50% duration |
| Enchanted Gunpowder | Splash, no duration penalty |

### Duration Bonuses
- **Potion Affinity Talisman**: +10% duration
- **Potion Affinity Ring**: +25% duration
- **Potion Affinity Artifact**: +50% duration
- **Parrot Pet**: +40% duration
- **Alchemy Skill**: +1% per level (up to +50% at Alchemy 50)

### Potion Rarity
- Levels I-II: Common (white)
- Levels III-IV: Uncommon (green)
- Levels V-VI: Rare (blue)
- Levels VII-VIII: Epic (purple)

## Alchemy Skill

Alchemy is one of the easiest skills to max. Leveling it increases potion duration.

### Brewer Perk
Each Alchemy level grants +1% potion effect duration (up to +50% at level 50).

### XP Sources
- Nether Wart brewing: +1 XP
- Awkward Potion: +5 XP
- Brewing higher-tier potions gives more XP

### Leveling Tips
- **Budget**: Enchanted Melons (best XP per coin)
- **Fast**: Enchanted Sugar Cane (quick brewing cycles)
- **Optimal XP**: Enchanted Blaze Rods (highest XP per brew)

### Rewards
- Alchemy 25: +25% potion duration, +36 Intelligence, 1.3M coins
- Alchemy 50: +50% potion duration, +86 Intelligence, 17.2M coins, 700 SkyBlock XP

## God Potion

The God Potion is the single most important potion in Hypixel SkyBlock. It replaces nearly every individual potion.

### How to Obtain
- **Purchase**: Elizabeth at the Community Center Bits Shop for **1,500 Bits**
- Cannot be sold or traded
- Special rarity item

### Duration
- Base: 12 hours minimum
- Formula: **12 + (0.24 x Alchemy Level)** hours
- At Alchemy 50: 24 hours per use
- **Stacks up to 192 hours (8 days)** with multiple uses
- Timer does NOT count down while offline, on Private Island, or in dungeons
- Only ticks during active gameplay in public areas

### Effects Included
The God Potion grants maximum-tier versions of all major buffs:

**Combat**:
- Strength VIII (+75 Strength)
- Critical IV (+25% Crit Chance, +40% Crit Damage)
- Archery IV (+75% bow damage)
- Adrenaline VIII (+300 Absorption, +40 Speed)

**Survivability**:
- Regeneration IX (+60 Health Regen)
- Absorption VIII (+300 Absorption HP)
- Resistance VIII (+60 Defense)
- True Resistance IV (+20 True Defense)

**Utility**:
- Speed VIII (+40 Speed)
- Night Vision I
- Water Breathing VI
- Haste IV (+200 Mining Speed)

**Skill XP Boosts**:
- All six Wisdom potions at Tier III (+20 Wisdom each for Farming, Mining, Combat, Foraging, Fishing, Enchanting)

**Special**:
- Magic Find IV (+75% Magic Find)
- Pet Luck IV (+20% Pet Luck)
- Spirit IV (+40 Speed, +40% Cooldown Reduction)
- Spelunker V (+25 Mining Fortune)
- Agility IV (+40 Speed, +40% Dodge)

**Jerry Candy Bonuses**: +100 HP, +20 Strength, +100 Intelligence, +2 Fertility, +3 Magic Find

### Important Notes
- Individual effects can be toggled on/off in SkyBlock Menu
- Using regular potions while God Potion is active provides NO additional benefit
- Drinking milk removes ALL potion effects (including God Potion)
- God Potion overrides all other potion buffs

## Potion Stacking Rules

1. **Potions do NOT stack** with each other. Using a new potion of the same type overwrites the old one.
2. **God Potion overrides all** individual potions. While active, regular potions provide no benefit.
3. **God Potion duration stacks** - consuming multiple God Potions adds time (up to 192 hours / 8 days).
4. **Dungeon potions are separate** from regular potions. Regular potion timers pause in dungeons.
5. **Potion effects are NOT removed on death** (changed in version 0.19.3).
6. **Milk removes ALL potion effects**, including God Potion. Never drink milk.
7. Different potion types CAN be active simultaneously (e.g., Strength + Speed + Critical all at once).
8. God Potion individual effects can be toggled on/off in the SkyBlock Menu.

## Best Potions by Activity

### General Gameplay / Combat
- **God Potion** (best all-around, always keep active)
- If no God Potion: Strength VIII + Critical IV + Speed VIII + Regeneration IX + Resistance VIII

### Dungeons
- **God Potion** active (timer pauses inside dungeons)
- **Dungeon Potion VII** (35,000 coins from Ophelia) for dungeon-specific buffs
- Both stack since dungeon potions are separate

### Slayer Bosses
- **God Potion** (ensures all combat buffs are maxed)
- Critical IV and Strength VIII are the most impactful if buying individually
- Archery IV for ranged slayer setups (Voidgloom)

### Mining
- **God Potion** (includes Haste IV + Spelunker V)
- If no God Potion: Haste IV (+200 Mining Speed) + Spelunker IV (+20 Mining Fortune)
- Mining Pumpkin for Spelunker V (1 hour)

### Farming
- **God Potion** (includes Farming Wisdom III)
- Speed VIII for faster farm traversal
- Farming Wisdom III for XP boost

### Fishing
- **God Potion** for general buffs
- **Mushed Glowy Tonic** (+30 Fishing Speed, separate from God Potion)

### Money Making / RNG Drops
- **God Potion** (includes Magic Find IV + Pet Luck IV)
- If no God Potion: Magic Find IV + Pet Luck IV (from Fear Mongerer)

### Crimson Isle / Kuudra
- **God Potion**
- **Wisp's Ice-Flavored Water** (+10% healing, +25 True Defense)

### Tips
- Always keep a God Potion active. At 1,500 Bits per 12-24 hours, it is extremely cost-effective.
- Stack God Potion duration to 8 days so you don't have to worry about it.
- For dungeons, buy Dungeon Potion VII from Ophelia every run.
- Level Alchemy to 50 for maximum potion duration (+50%).
- Get a Potion Affinity Artifact for +50% potion duration.
