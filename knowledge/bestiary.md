# Bestiary

The Bestiary (/ ˈbɛs tʃiˌɛr i, ˈbis- /) is a repository for data on Mobs . Collecting kills on Mobs within the Bestiary rewards buffs against that mob, and contributes to the Bestiary Milestone, which grants ❤ Health and Combat XP.

## Mechanics
The Bestiary tracks the kills of 214 mobs across SkyBlock, including sea creatures and event mobs. Each mob has a family, with a max kills cap and milestones that split the necessary kills.
To start tracking Bestiary kills, it is required to talk to Bramass Beastslayer first.

### Kill Credit
Bestiary credit is awarded when the player assists in killing a mob. Bestiary credit is limited to the top 5 players who dealt the most damage to said mob.
Mobs found in Dungeons and the Kuudra boss fight have shared bestiary credit within players in the same instance. The following mobs have shared bestiary credit within the player's dungeon party:
- Lost Adventurer
- Angry Archaeologist
- Shadow Assassin
- King Midas
- Mimic
- Wither Miner
- Wither Husk
- Wither Guard
- Terracotta
The player who fishes up a Sea Creature will always receive credit, regardless of whether they dealt damage to the mob or not.

### Brackets
Each mob has its own kill tier caps, and the amount of mobs needed to maximize a family is determined by its tier cap and kill brackets. Kill scaling per tier is standardized into 7 kill brackets, which can be seen below.
The amount of kills needed to progress towards the next tier, the total amount of kills needed to max the mob, and the tier the mob is capped at is displayed in a family's entry in the Bestiary .

### Rewards
There are 2 types of rewards that feature in the Bestiary : leveling rewards, which are earned after leveling up a mob's family, and milestone rewards, which are earned after reaching a bestiary milestone.

#### Leveling
Leveling rewards are earned after reaching a milestone within a family. Rewards include bonus ✯ Magic Find and ❁ Strength on that mob, additional coins upon the mob's death and a chance for additional experience orbs.

#### Milestone
Milestone rewards are obtained after reaching 10 family milestones. Each milestone grants +1 ❤ Health and every 10 milestones, the player gains +10 SkyBlock XP . Additionally, every 5 milestones, the player will receive increasing amounts of Combat XP, up until milestone C , at which point the player will receive 1,000,000 experience from then onwards. Rewards are stored until the player claims them, and can accumulate to allow the player to claim multiple milestone rewards at once.
The Combat XP is not affected by ☯ Combat Wisdom , it however is affected by all Pet Exp boosts.

## Trivia
- Due to its abscence in internal tracking, the following mobs were not tracked before the Bestiary revamp: Kuudra Mobs , Bal , and Reindrake . Additionally, the stats of [ Lv1 ] Cow , Chicken , Sheep , Pig , and Zombie were merged into their minion counterparts and therefore do not count for Bestiary credit.
- If the player has not moved their camera in the past hour, they will not receive Bestiary credit for any mob kills.
