# Spider's Den

The Spider's Den is a Combat location unlocked after reaching Combat I .

## Location
The Spider's Den can be accessed after the player has attained Combat I by using a Launchpad found in the back of the Graveyard . It can also be accessed with the Portal to The Spider's Den or Travel Scroll to the Spider's Den . This will take the player to a different sub-server containing The Spiders Den.
The Spider's Den has multiple sub-regions: The ⏣ Spider Mound with multiple chasms and stairs containing weaker mobs, ⏣ Grandma's House containing multiple NPCs , the ⏣ Gravel Mines , and ⏣ Arachne's Burrow , ⏣ Archaeologist's Camp with Archaeologist and ⏣ Arachne's Sanctuary containing stronger versions of the spiders found in the den (This is where you can find the Arachne boss). The spiders get stronger in deeper regions of the cave. These enemies include:
- Dasher Spiders , which dash towards players.
- Splitter Spiders , which spawn silverfish upon death.
- Weaver Spiders , which shoot cobweb at the players.
- Voracious Spiders , which have significantly higher health and damage than other spiders.
- Spider Jockeys , which have a Skeleton on their back.
In ⏣ Arachne's Burrow , all spiders have a chance to drop an Arachne's Calling , which can be used to summon the boss Arachne , which has two versions depending on how she's summoned.
On the outskirts of the islands, one may also find Rain Slimes , Toxic Rain Slimes and Gravel Skeletons , with the former spawning on the road between ⏣ Arachne's Burrow and the Spiders Den during rain while the latter spawns near the gravel pit.

## Rain
The weather on the island alternates between 40 minutes of clear weather and 20 minutes of rain. While it is raining, players gain +50 ☂ Fishing Speed and Rain Slimes can spawn. Every third period of rain is a thunderstorm, where in addition to the usual rain changes, players gain +3 α Sea Creature Chance and Toxic Rain Slimes can spawn.

## Tiny Island
The Tiny Island is a feature located in between The End and the Spiders Den. It can be accessed by a Barrier bridge. The island's purpose is to house a single Fairy Soul .

# The End

The End is a location that represents the vanilla Minecraft End dimension .
Like the vanilla Minecraft End Dimension, it is home to Endermen and the Ender Dragon .

## Location
The End can be accessed from ⏣ Spider's Den on the west side via the use of a launchpad, a player may use a portal on their island to access The End, or they may use the Travel Scroll to The End to access The End from anywhere. Players must have Combat XII unlocked in order to access The End. The portal can be located in the ⏣ Spider's Den at -382, 89, -268 .

## Features

### Mobs
The End has Endermen all throughout it. When Endstone is mined without a Stonk or when Ender Pearls are used, there is a small chance an Endermite will spawn.
In the Dragon's Nest, Zealots , Obsidian Defenders and Watchers spawn. Endermites also spawn naturally, as well as being spawned by Watchers .
In the Void Sepulture , much stronger Endermen called Voidling Fanatics and Voidling Extremists spawn.

### Resources
There are 3 main resources found in The End - Obsidian , Endstone , and Ender Pearls .
Obsidian and Endstone can be obtained from mining, and Ender Pearls will drop from slain Endermen and Zealots .
There are three types of Zealots: normal Zealots , Ender Chest Zealots (which drop goodies when killed), and Portal Frame or Special Zealots , which spawn rarely after killing normal or Ender Chest Zealots. They are weaker (2,000 Health) and drop Summoning Eyes .
There are also Ender Armor and Summoning Eyes . Ender Armor is a rare drop from Endermen . Summoning Eyes can be obtained from special Portal Frame Zealots that spawn rarely after killing a normal Zealot (1 in 420 chance) or after killing a Zealot Bruiser (1 in 210 chance) . Zealots also drop a phoenix pet.
Obsidian Defenders mostly drop obsidian, but they occasionally drop the Obsidian Chestplate .
Watchers drop arrows and rarely drop the End Stone Bow .
Both of these mobs have a Pray RNGesus chance to get Rare and Epic combat EXP boosts.
There are also Ender Nodes that can be found which drop additional resources ( Experience Bottles , Grand Experience Bottles , Endstone or Obsidian (sometimes enchanted), Enchanted Ender Pearls , reforge stones, and rarely Shrimp the Fish . Watch out for Nest Endermites though, as they can easily one or two shot a newer player. They drop mite gel, which can be sold for a lot of money.
Pearl Dealer - Has a shop outside the spawn platform featuring Dragon Hunter , Endstone , Obsidian , Remnant of the Eye , Stonk and Silent Pearls .
Guber - Provides a racing quest from one side of the island to another for rewards.

### Dragon's Nest
The Dragon's Nest is the boss room in The End. Its entrance is located at -600, 22, -275 . When eight Summoning Eyes are used on the altar in the center, the Ender Dragon spawns. There are seven Ender dragon types that can spawn, each one has a different amount of health, and their own Armor pieces and Dragon Fragments .
There are several sub-sections of the Dragon's nest, which contain teleport pads.
- " Dragon's View " is one, and contains a Teleport Pad to "Endertower." Dragon's View is a tall sandstone and obsidian tower. It is located at -713, 41, -317 .

### Void Sepulture
The Void Sepulture is an area in The End designed for players wanting to grind Enderman Slayer located at -576, 7, -317 . It houses endermen with much higher health and damage, named the Voidling Fanatic and Voidling Extremist . It is recommended to only go here if you have enough gear with which you know you won't die easily because Remnants of the Eye do not work inside the Void Sepulture. In the Void Sepulture, Voidling Fanatics and Voidling Extremists can drop enchanted ender pearls. Voidling Extremists can also drop Enderman Cortex Rewriter.

### Zealot Bruiser Hideout
The Zealot Bruiser Hideout is an area in The End designed for stronger players to grind for Summoning Eyes without cluttering the Dragon's Nest. The Zealot Bruiser Hideout is the only area in which Zealot Bruisers naturally spawn.

## Location
The Crimson Isle can be accessed only after the player has attained Combat XXII (22) . It is traveled to by the use of a Launchpad found in the back of the ⏣ Spider's Den .
There are 21 main sub-location: ⏣ Barbarian Outpost , ⏣ The Bastion , ⏣ Blazing Volcano , ⏣ Burning Desert , ⏣ Cathedral , ⏣ Crimson Fields , ⏣ Dojo , ⏣ Dragontail , ⏣ Forgotten Skull , ⏣ Kuudra's Hollow , ⏣ Mage Outpost , ⏣ Magma Chamber , ⏣ Mystic Marsh , ⏣ Odger's Hut , ⏣ Ruins of Ashfang , ⏣ Scarleton , ⏣ Stronghold , ⏣ The Wasteland , ⏣ Matriarch's Lair , ⏣ Belly of the Beast , and ⏣ Aura's Lab .

## Features

### Factions
Factions are a new feature of the Crimson Isle. Players can gain reputation with Factions by killing mobs or doing quests (including Elle 's main questline), giving access to special Items and features. There are two factions: the Barbarian Faction and the Mage Faction.

#### Guards
Guards patrol the cities of important Factions. They are passive, until attacked, when they become hostile.

### Bosses
Minibosses and the Magma Boss spawn around the island. They drop rare loot and spawn quickly after they are killed. Similar to the Endstone Protector they will leave iron blocks near death. Miniboss loot will nearly always be Co-op Soulbound . Minibosses include the Magma Boss , Mage Outlaw , Bladesoul , Ashfang and Barbarian Duke X .

#### Bladesoul
Bladesoul is the first boss of the Crimson Isle. It spawns in the ⏣ Stronghold every 2 minutes after it is killed. It has 50,000,000 ❤ Health .

#### Magma Boss
The Magma Boss will spawn in the ⏣ Magma Chamber every 5 minutes after it is killed. It has 200,000,000 ❤ Health .

#### Mage Outlaw
The Mage Outlaw spawns in the ⏣ Courtyard near ⏣ Scarleton every 2 minutes after it is killed. It has 70,000,000 ❤ Health .

#### Barbarian Duke X
The Barbarian Duke X spawns in the ⏣ The Dukedom near ⏣ Dragontail every 2 minutes after it is killed. It has 70,000,000 ❤ Health .

#### Ashfang
Ashfang will spawn in the ⏣ Ruins of Ashfang every 2 minutes after it is killed. It has 50,000,000 ❤ Health , having the least health of all the minibosses, however is a more complicated fight, usually requiring more than one player to defeat it.

### Kuudra
Kuudra is a 4-player fight against a boss, similar to Dungeons . It takes place in ⏣ Kuudra's Hollow , and drops unique loot.

### Vanquishers
Vanquishers are similar to Special Zealots and are mini-withers that have 5,000,000 ❤ Health , which have a small chance to spawn after killing any mob in the Crimson Isle . They drop Nether Stars , which are used to buy Kuudra Keys from the Emissaries in each of the Factions ' settlements.

### Fishing
The fishing mechanic in the Crimson Isle is different from the other islands. ☂ Fishing Speed is capped at 350 instead of 300. Fishing 10 is needed to fish in the ⏣ Crimson Isle as the Starter Lava Rod has that requirement.

#### Trophy Fishing
The Crimson Isle introduces an exclusive feature called Trophy Fishing. When fishing under a condition, Trophy Fishes can be caught. Trophy Fishes comes with a "rarity" of Bronze , Silver , Gold and Diamond . Using an EPIC Kuudra Pet , LEGENDARY Kuudra Pet, Silver Hunter Armor, Gold Hunter Armor, Diamond Hunter Armor, Magma Rod, Inferno Rod or Hellfire Rod can increase the chance of catching Trophy Fishes.

#### Lava Sea Creatures
The Crimson Isle has exclusive Sea Creatures , including the following:
- Magma Slug : Requires Fishing 27
- Moogma : Requires Fishing 28
- Lava Leech : Requires Fishing 30
- Pyroclastic Worm : Requires Fishing 31
- Lava Flame : Requires Fishing 32
- Fire Eel : Requires Fishing 34
- Taurus : Requires Fishing 35
- Thunder : Requires Fishing 36 and Novice Trophy Hunter
- Lord Jawbus Requires Fishing 45 and Adept Trophy Hunter

# The Park

The Park is the first Foraging island, consisting of several large floating islands connected by launch pads. It is the revamped version of the Floating Islands and can be accessed through the same portals to the Floating Islands.

### Birch Park
Birch Park
The ⏣ Birch Park is the first section of the park, and access to it requires Foraging I (1) .
It contains two NPCs: Charlie , who sends new players in search to collect Birch Log and get to all the different sections, and Vanessa , who can be paid 5,000 coins , to make the park rain for a minute.

### Spruce Woods
Spruce Woods
The Spruce Woods is the second section of the Park, and requires completion of the Into the Woods quest to access. The Spruce Woods contains three NPCs: Kelly , who wants Spruce Log , the Joyful Viking , who the player can buy items from, and Gustave , who gives the Woods Racing quest. Spruce trees spawn in the Spruce Woods.

#### Woods Racing
The Woods Race is started by talking to Gustave . The player must travel to the Jungle Island and back in continually fewer amounts of time to complete the quest, which yields rewards including a Polished Pebble , a Hunter Knife , a Wolf Paw , and a Silky Lichen .

#### Melancholic Viking Quest
The Joyful Viking is located on the ⏣ Viking Longhouse in the Spruce Woods. The player can access their shop by using 1x Magical Water Bucket , 1x Raw Fish , and Fish Hat .

### Dark Thicket
Dark Thicket
The Dark Thicket is the third section of the Park and requires completion of the A Helping Hand quest to access. There are four NPCs found in the Dark Thicket: two Campfire Initiates, one Campfire Adept, and Ryan . Dark Oak trees spawn in the Dark Thicket, and a weak wall leads into the Howling Cave .

#### Trial of Fire
The Trial of Fire can be completed to earn various Campfire Badges .

### Savanna Woodland
Savanna Woodland
The Savanna Woodland is the fourth section of the Park and requires completion of the Trial of Fire quest to enter. There are three NPCs located in this section: Worker Xavier , Melody and the Master Tactician Funk . There is a fourth NPC, Romero , hiding in a cave accessed from a ladder near Master Tactician Funk, who only appears at the beginning of the Romero and Juliette quest. Acacia trees spawn in the Savanna Woodland.

#### Melody
Melody is found on a separate island in the Savanna Woodland. They ask for 512x Acacia Log to help fix the Harp . Fixing the Harp allows the player to complete songs to earn rewards.

#### Master Tactician Funk
Master Tactician Funk is located in a tent near the edge of the Savanna Woodland. They sell a Tactician's Sword and Wood Singularity .

#### Gold Mine
A small gold mine can be mined for Gold Ore . During a quest from Romero , mining the Gold Ore has a chance to drop a Yellow Rock . There is also a weak wall that leads to the ⏣ Soul Cave .

### Jungle Island
Jungle Island
The Jungle Island is the fifth section of the Park and requires completion of "The Rebuild" Quest to enter. There are two NPCs here: Juliette, who longs for Romero, and Molbert , who requests Jungle Logs , Carrots , and String to make traps for moles before getting stuck in them himself. Upon completing the Romero Questline, Romero will have moved to the Jungle Island with Juliette. Under the Jungle Island where Romero and Juliette are, there is a secret parkour that leads under the ice pond (There is still no use for this but in the new foraging Forums post the admins confirmed they had more plans to do with the Foraging islands).
All of the main NPCs grant an armor piece:
- Charlie rewards Charlie's Trousers for 64 Birch Logs
- Kelly rewards Kelly's T-Shirt for 128 Spruce Logs
- Melody rewards Melody's Shoes for 512 Acacia Logs
- Molbert rewards the Mole Hat for 512 Jungle Logs

### Romero and Juliette Quest
The Romero and Juliette Quest from Romero and Juliette requires various expensive items to complete. This quest rewards a LEGENDARY Accessory . The Jungle Island is a main location that the quest takes place.

## Fairy Souls
There are 11 Fairy Souls located in The Park. It is recommended to collect them as they are fairly easy to get and reward SkyBlock XP .

# Deep Caverns

The Deep Caverns is a location focused on Mining . The players requires Mining V to enter. Several unique resources can be found: Lapis Lazuli , Redstone , Emeralds , Diamonds , and Obsidian .

## Locations
The Deep Caverns are divided into 6 layers (excluding the Dwarven Mines ) that become progressively more dangerous the deeper traveled.
After discovering a layer for the first time, the player can be quickly travel to them with the Lift Operator .
Each layer has its own resources and its own hostile mobs that increase in difficulty as the player progresses deeper into the caverns.

## Trivia
- The transition sections between levels does not spawn mobs from either floor and prevents them from entering said sections. This can be exploited, as the transition between the ⏣ Diamond Reserve and ⏣ Obsidian Sanctuary allows players to easily and safely mine high quantities of Diamonds .

# Dwarven Mines

The Dwarven Mines is a location that is focused on Mining . It has several unique Dwarven Metals , including Mithril and Titanium : the Heart of the Mountain is also unlocked here.
Mining XII and completion of Rhys ' quest is required to access the Dwarven Mines .

## Overview
The primary focuses of the Dwarven Mines are to complete Commissions , which are tasks given by the King , to level up your Heart of the Mountain , and collect different varieties of Powder : that being ᠅ Mithril Powder , ᠅ Gemstone Powder , and ᠅ Glacite Powder .

## Navigation
Players will arrive in the ⏣ Dwarven Village , where Rhys can be found, as well as several commodities: Bubu sells pickaxes with various stats, Bulvar sells Tier 12 mining minions, Banker Broadjaw allows players to access their Bank , Lumina sells various light sources, Fragilis shows the Handy Block Guide, and Geo allows players to add Gemstones to their items.
The gate to the ⏣ Crystal Hollows can be found at Gwendolyn , who can be found at the outskirts of the Dwarven Village: after reaching Heart of the Mountain IV , the player can pay a coins fee to enter the Crystal Hollows for 6 hours.
Exiting the Dwarven Village will reveal a crossroads: the left path leads to the ⏣ Royal Palace via the ⏣ Palace Bridge , which overlooks the ⏣ Royal Mines ; the right path leads to the rest of the Dwarven Mines via the ⏣ Gates to the Mines , which overlooks ⏣ The Forge .
Beyond the ⏣ Great Ice Wall , the ⏣ Dwarven Base Camp can be found: it is inaccessible without Heart of the Mountain VII and completion of Dulin 's quest, which also grants permanent free entry to the ⏣ Crystal Hollows .

### Dwarven Mines Rail System
The Station Master can be found in the ⏣ Dwarven Village , with several Ticket Master s found around stations in the Dwarven Mines. After paying a small coins fee, depending on your proximity to your desired location, players can enter a minecart and travel to other areas around the Dwarven Mines .

# Crystal Hollows

The Crystal Hollows is a location that is focused on Mining . The primary focuses of the Crystal Hollows are mining Gemstones and collecting Gemstone Crystals to complete the Crystal Nucleus .

## Tasks

## Overview
The Crystal Hollows consists of a large series of tunnels made of Hard Stone , with veins of Gemstones and randomly-generated structures scattered throughout the caves. The tunnels themselves remain the same across lobbies, but the locations of structures will always be different across different servers. At the center of the Crystal Hollows houses the ⏣ Crystal Nucleus , where Gemstone Crystals can be placed.
Talking to Gwendolyn allows you to purchase a pass to the Crystal Hollows for 6h for 10,000 coins : you gain permanent, unrestricted access to the Crystal Hollows after helping Dulin .

### Locations
There are 5 main locations in the Crystal Hollows , being the ⏣ Jungle , ⏣ Mithril Deposits , ⏣ Precursor Remnants , ⏣ Goblin Holdout , and ⏣ Magma Fields . These correspond to their respective Gemstone , that being Amethyst , Jade , Sapphire , Amber , and Topaz . Ruby can also be found in the Crystal Hollows and can be  found everywhere. Additionally, 3 ⏣ Fairy Grotto s spawn in random locations throughout the Crystal Hollows: Jasper can be mined there.
Each primary location has a sub-location, where its respective Gemstone Crystal can be obtained.
- The ⏣ Jungle contains the ⏣ Jungle Temple , where a Jungle Key must be given to access a series of parkour with the Amethyst Crystal at the end;
- The ⏣ Mithril Deposits contains the ⏣ Mines of Divan , where a Metal Detector must be used to find and return Scavenged Items , with the Jade Crystal being rewarded at the end;
- The ⏣ Precursor Remnants contains the ⏣ Lost Precursor City , where all 6 Automaton Parts or a Precursor Apparatus must be given to access the Sapphire Crystal ;
- The ⏣ Goblin Holdout contains the ⏣ Goblin Queen's Den , where any variant of Goblin Egg must be given to King Yolkar the Amber Crystal without being caught;
- The ⏣ Magma Fields contains ⏣ Khazad-dûm , where Bal must be defeated to claim the Topaz Crystal .
Gemstone Crystals can be placed in the ⏣ Crystal Nucleus to or used to forge Perfect Gemstones . You can see the status of Gemstone Crystals in the tablist: a Crystal can either be Not Found , Not Placed , or Placed . A Gemstone Crystal must be found but Not Placed to be used in the Forge to forge a its respective Perfect Gemstone.

### Mining
While inside the Crystal Hollows, broken ores and blocks are not replaced with bedrock. Instead, they will physically be broken be replaced with air, similar to vanilla Minecraft . Blocks can be broken everywhere except the ⏣ Crystal Nucleus and in specific structures: Torches , Ladders , and Hard Stone can also be placed.
Stone is replaced by Hard Stone inside the Crystal Hollows , which has a higher Ⓟ Breaking Power and a unique Collection. Gemstones , Mithril , and most Ores can spawn inside the Crystal Hollows. Ores that require a Furnace to be smelted into an ingot, such as Iron Ore and Gold Ore , do not require Smelting Touch or a Furnace to be converted to an ingot.
Yields from most vanilla ores are increased within the Crystal Hollows.

### Treasure and Loot Chests
Upon mining blocks, there is a small chance to spawn a treasure chest, containing various rewards. These chests can spawn anywhere except near the bedrock wall. The player must aim at the critical particles on the face of the chests 5 times in a row in order to open a chest. These treasure chests spawned from mining will only last for around 1 minute before disappearing.
Loot chests on the other hand can spawn in set locations within structures; these chests can be opened directly with a small time delay and contain about twice the gemstones and powder of what a treasure chest gives on average.
The loot within such a certain structure chest is always the same for all players who open it within that Crystal Hollows instance. However, the amount of Mithril and Gemstone Powder within each slot of the chest varies between players. Chests from mining do not share this property.
Treasure and Loot chests will only contain the loot found within the area. For example, Magma Fields chests might contain Yoggie , Topaz or Ruby .
Treasure and Loot chests have a 5% chance to be a rare loot chest, which can contain better drops. If the chest is not a rare loot chest, it will contain common loot.

## Structures
Each part of the Crystal Hollows has its own main special location, excluding Magma Fields , which will almost always generate in every lobby:
- The Jungle Temple generated in the Jungle
- The Mines of Divan generated in the Mithril Deposits
- The Goblin Queen's Den generated in the Goblin Holdout
- The Lost Precursor City generated in the Precursor Remnants
- 3 Fairy Grottos generated anywhere within the Crystal Hollows
Each of these structures house their respective Crystal . A special task must be completed to obtain the Crystal.
Khazad-dûm is the one location that can spawn in any part of the Crystal Hollows, making it harder to find.
There are also smaller structures which generate in random locations. Examples include:
- A structure which resembles the Deep Caverns , including the lift and the layered structure of the original Deep Caverns island
- A dragon skull from the Spider's Den
- A replica of the Dark Auction
- A miniature version of The Forge including a working forge NPC
- A copy of the pre- Hub overhaul Tavern
A full list of Structures can be found at Crystal Hollows/Special Locations .
Certain structures contain loot chests. Loot chests always generate in the same positions within the structures, similar to Dungeon Secrets . Loot Chests also generate the same items for every player, meaning if a player finds a chest with good loot such as a lot of Gemstone Powder or a Blue Goblin Egg, they can send the coordinates to chat to lead others to the chest.

## Trivia
- The Crystal Hollows are internally referred to as the Greater Mines in the SkyBlock API.
- The Crystal Hollows are the only place Dirt , Andesite , Diorite , and Granite (and other blocks) can be mined, however, only Dirt drops itself and all others drop Hard Stone instead.
- Ores within a few blocks of the Bedrock border may not get auto-smelted without Smelting Touch .
- Attempting to get into the Crystal Hollows using an Ender Pearl will result in the player being teleported out with the following message, even if they do have a valid Crystal Hollows Pass: You have been teleported out of the Crystal Hollows because you did not have a valid pass!

- A player cannot claim rewards from the same loot chest on multiple profiles.

# Glacite Tunnels

This article needs more information. Please help expand it .
See more stubs.
The Glacite Tunnels are a location focused on Mining . It has several unique minerals, including Glacite , Umber , and Tungsten .

## Accessing
In order to access the Glacite Tunnels , the player must forge a Secret Railroad Pass after talking to Dulin . The player must be Heart of The Mountain VII in order to forge a Secret Railroad Pass .

## Mechanics
In the Glacite Tunnels , the player can mine Dwarven Metals like Umber , Tungsten and Glacite . The Glacite Tunnels also contain Gemstones , exclusive to this area, such as Onyx , Aquamarine , Citrine and Peridot . Inside the Tunnels, you accumulate ❄ Cold , which reduces your ⸕ Mining Speed by 0.5 per ❄ Cold . Additionally, the players ✦ Speed is decreased by 25% for every 25 ❄ Cold . Upon reaching 100 ❄ Cold , the player freezes to death and is returned to ⏣ Dwarven Base Camp .

### Mineshafts
Glacite Mineshafts can be found by mining blocks within the ⏣ Glacite Tunnels . The base chance of spawning a Glacite Mineshaft is 0.05% , or 1 in 2,000 . A pity system was implemented in Version 0.20.1 to provide a more consistent spawn rate for Glacite Mineshafts . Each pity counter begins at 2,000 and decreases by the numbers below for each block type. Once a Glacite Mineshaft has spawned, the counter is reset to 2,000 .
