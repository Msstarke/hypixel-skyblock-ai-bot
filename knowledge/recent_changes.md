# Hypixel SkyBlock Recent Changes & Patch Notes

*Last updated: March 2026. Covers major updates from July 2025 through March 2026.*

## Version 0.24.2 — Hub Revamp & Year of the Witch (February 24, 2026)

### Hub Revamp
- The Hub island has been completely redesigned with "lanes" for each skill to better direct players toward their chosen path
- Museum relocated from the mountain to Hub Village with new categories: Combat, Farming, Mining, Fishing, Foraging, Dungeoneering, Hunting, Special Items
- Museum now has sort buttons per category, donate-from-home-screen, and 46 new donatable items added

### Year of the Witch Event
- New annual event occurring once every 12 SkyBlock years (starting Year 476)
- Mobs drop rare stew ingredients (Waterborne Lice, Dirty Fingernails, Void Ooze, Festering Flies, etc.)
- Brew special stews at Alcina in The Hub for Alchemy XP and Magic Find bonuses
- Year of the Witch Shop sells: new talisman, two Mixins, ingredient sack, Witch Pet
- Pig Pet completely revamped with better Shiny Pig and Potato Farming performance
- Flying Pig Pet Item now grants +40 Potato Fortune and +20 Speed
- Shiny Shards guaranteed from Shiny Pigs; reduced from Legendary to Rare rarity

### Frozen Corpse RNG Meter
- New guaranteed-drop system accessible via `/rng` or Grandpa Wolf in Glacite Tunnels
- Progression tiers: Lapis (500 XP), Umber/Tungsten (2,500 XP), Vanguard (25,000 XP)
- 10% retroactive XP applied
- New talisman line from Grandpa Wolf milestone rewards
- Completely rewritten loot tables emphasizing higher-tier corpse profitability

### Mining Changes
- Frosted Scute salvageable for 100 Ice Essence; both Scute and Shattered Locket now have 5M NPC sell price
- Pendant of Divan Legacy Tiered Bonus: +2.5 Gemstone Spread
- Mining Fiestas redistributed to months 2, 4, 6, 8, 10
- New deployable line: Dwarven Lantern -> Mithril -> Titanium -> Glacite -> Will-o-wisp
- **Dead Man's Chest HOTM Perk nerfed: +1-50% reduced to +0.5-25%**

### Dungeoneering Changes
- Spirit Mask added to Master Mode Floor 4 Obsidian Chest (15M cost); removed from Dark Auction
- Golden/Diamond Heads now purchased from Ophelia (Diamond Heads carry over upgrades)
- Gold Essence (250) and Diamond Essence (1,000) added to Boss Collection tiers
- Default dungeon class changed to Berserk
- Shadow Assassin Cloak speed adjusted: Base 3->10, Fragged 5->15

### Fishing Changes
- Fishing Baits now consumed only on successful catches (major QoL)
- Sea Creatures no longer attack players unless provoked
- Removed idle-catch prevention mechanic (no more forced movement)
- Golden Fish Tablist Widget added to Crimson Isle
- Earlier-tier Fishing Minion speeds adjusted for smoother progression

### Combat Changes
- Kuudra Pet perk unlock reordered: Trophy Bait moved to Uncommon (more accessible)
- Wolf Talisman rarity increased to Uncommon
- Blood God Crest infinitely purchasable, capped at +7 Strength

### Quality-of-Life
- Perishable minion fuels (Hyper Catalysts) now retrievable from minions
- Hoppity can pull coins from Bank if Purse insufficient
- 20-second AH hold items hidden until period expires
- Recombobulation disabled for: Wisp Upgrade Stones, Enchanted Bookshelves, Bejewelled Handles, Soul String, Griffin Feathers

### Stranded Mode End-of-Life
- Warning added when creating Stranded profiles
- Six NPCs added, mob HP/damage halved, Builder's collection requirement removed
- All Fortunes now functional on Stranded
- Golden/Diamond Goblin spawn chance increased 10x
- Minimum version now required: Minecraft 1.21.9

## Version 0.24 — The Greenhouse Update (December 15, 2025)

### Greenhouse Plot (Major New Feature)
- New placeable garden plot unlocked at Garden Level 7
- Smaller than standard (10x10) but yields significantly more per harvest
- Crops grow slower but can progress offline
- Features watering can mechanic with upgradeable tools

### Crop Effects System
- Each crop provides buffs to nearby crops (e.g., Wheat grants 20% Harvest Boost)
- Some crops have negative effects with stronger positive counterparts
- Includes Immunity and Effect Spread mechanics

### Mutations System
- 30+ unique mutations with specific crop arrangement requirements
- Progressive unlock through a Mutation Tree
- Examples: Dustgrain, sleeping flowers, rat-attracting cheese, teleporting chorus fruit
- Varying growth times, water requirements, and unique mechanics

### Three New Crops
- Sunflower, Moonflower, and Wild Rose added
- Each has new tools, farming contests, milestones, pests, presets, and collections
- Helianthus Armor introduced (tier above Fermento)

### Farming Tool Revamp
- New leveling system: gain XP breaking crops, level-ups grant Fortune, Gemstone Slots, rarity increases
- All tools standardized and moved from Anita to SkyMart

### Chip System (New Farming Currency)
- Sowdust: new currency gained from farming
- Chips are special items granting perks when consumed
- Duplicate chips increase rarity, raising max level and perk scaling
- New NPC Jeff in Garden introduces the system

### Major Farming Nerfs
- **Crop Milestones cut by 68% across the board**
- **Visitor Served Milestones reduced 75% (10,000 -> 2,500)**
- Composter Upgrade costs reduced 20% (40% for rare crops)

### Farming Armor Changes
- Added Bonus Pest Chance to all farming armor: Melon 10%, Cropie 12.5%, Squash 15%, Fermento 17.5%
- Biohazard Armor: Removed 3x multiplier, buffed to 16.5% base
- Hedgehog Pet changed from Nocturnal to Hunter's Insight (farming fortune based on Bestiary Pest Tier)

### March 2026 Follow-up Nerfs
- Tier 1 Mutation drop rates reduced by ~50% for 8 crop types
- Dead Crop, Fermento, Squash, Helianthus, and Cropie no longer incorrectly reward Sowdust after growth cycles
- Rosewater Flask 2-hour co-op cooldown added; recipe cost doubled (16 -> 32 Compacted Wild Rose)
- Pest drops reduced for Cricket, Moth, and Slug
- Base crop drops lowered for Mushrooms, Cocoa, Carrots in Greenhouse
- Gloomgourd mutation crop drops reduced by 65%

## Current Meta Shifts (as of March 2026)

### Farming Meta
- Greenhouse is now the dominant farming method for high-yield production
- Mutations provide unique bonuses but Tier 1 mutation drops were nerfed 50%
- Sowdust/Chip system adds new progression layer
- Helianthus Armor is now best-in-slot farming armor (above Fermento)
- Crop Milestones much easier to complete (68% reduction)
- Three new crops (Sunflower, Moonflower, Wild Rose) add new farming options

### Mining Meta
- Dead Man's Chest HOTM perk significantly nerfed (halved)
- Maniac Miner capped at 500 Mining Fortune
- Frozen Corpse RNG Meter provides guaranteed progression for Glacite farming
- Armadillo Pet buffed with Frozen Scute upgrade
- Mithril Powder gains substantially buffed

### Combat Meta
- Mob Types system fundamentally changed how damage enchantments work
- Stat Categories restrict certain stat boosts to relevant activities only
- Soul Eater capped at 1M damage outside Dungeons
- Dungeonbreaker Pickaxe replaced stonking in Catacombs
- Spider Slayer expanded to Tier 9 with new endgame items
- Damage reduction now multiplicative (prevents stacking exploits)

### Fishing Meta
- Baits only consumed on successful catches (significant savings)
- Sea Creatures no longer attack unprovoked
- No more forced movement while fishing
- Bobbin' Time reduced to 5 players but increased per-player stats
- New Tracking stat affects Elusive mob spawns

### Dungeon Meta
- Spirit Mask moved to MM4 Obsidian Chest (15M, removed from Dark Auction)
- Thorn Shard guaranteed from MM4 Emerald+ Chests (now Legendary)
- Shadow Assassin Cloak speed buffed significantly
- Dungeonbreaker Pickaxe is the new standard for block-breaking in dungeons

### Economy Changes
- NPC daily sell cap raised to 500M coins
- Enchanted Hoppers nerfed to 70% NPC sell price
- Many NPC sell prices added/adjusted (Jaderald 1M, Quick Claw 5M, Radioactive Vial 5M)
- Reaper/Final Destination Armor stats removed when unequipped (can no longer AFK with stats)
- Recombobulation disabled on many items

### Mythological Ritual
- Griffin Pet can reach Mythic rarity
- New Tracking stat and Elusive mob mechanic
- Crown of Avarice/Greed completely reworked
- New endgame items: Manticore Claw, Golden Fleece
- Diana perk now gives Tracking instead of luck

### Pet System
- Swappable pet items (no more deletion)
- All crafted pets start at Common (upgrade at Kat)
- Pet crafting randomness removed
- Kuudra Pet perk order changed (Trophy Bait at Uncommon)
