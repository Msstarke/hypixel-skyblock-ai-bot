# Items & Gear

## Reforges
- Applied at the Blacksmith (hub) or Reforge Anvil
- Cost scales with item rarity
- Common reforges: Fierce, Sharp, Ancient, Legendary, Spicy, Withered, Renowned
- Best reforges change with meta — check community resources for current BIS

## Accessories (Talismans)
- Placed in accessory bag (unlocked early game)
- Only highest tier of each family counts (e.g. Speed Talisman → Speed Ring → Speed Artifact)
- Magical Power (MP) stat comes from accessory rarity count
- Hegemony Artifact: important for PvE setups

## Armor Sets (notable)
- **Early**: Lapis, Zombie Slayer, Ender armor
- **Mid**: Hardened Diamond, Mineral, Superior Dragon
- **End-game PvE**: Necron's, Maxor's, Storm's (wither armor)
- **Dungeons**: Starred wither armor, Crimson armor (from Nether)
- **Farming**: Fermento, Squash, Melon armor (farming fortune)

## Weapons (notable)
- **Hyperion**: top sword, uses Mana to deal AoE damage
- **Terminator**: top bow, rapid fire with arrows
- **Livid Dagger**: fast attack speed, good for skill speedrunning
- **AOTE (Aspect of the End)**: early-mid teleport sword
- **AOTS (Aspect of the Spider)**: spider slayer weapon
- **Midas Sword/Staff**: coins-scaling damage

## Pet System
- Pets give passive bonuses based on type and level (1–100)
- Rarity: Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Mythic
- Only one pet active at a time
- Notable pets: Griffin (mythological), Golden Dragon (coins/XP), Bee (farming), Enderman (combat)
- Pet XP can be boosted with Pet Candies (up to 10 per pet)

## Slayer
- Bosses: Revenant Horror (zombie), Tarantula Broodfather (spider), Sven Packmaster (wolf), Voidgloom Seraph (enderman), Inferno Demonlord (blaze), Riftstalker Bloodfiend (vampire — Rift only)
- Tiers: 1–4 (some have tier 5)
- Slayer XP unlocks recipes and items
- Requires spending coins to summon (scales with tier)
