# Rift Dimension

The Rift Dimension is an alternate dimension in Hypixel SkyBlock, accessible through the Wizard Tower. It features unique mechanics, its own currency (Motes), time-limited visits, and a progression system based on Timecharms.

## Accessing the Rift

### Requirements
- SkyBlock Level 12 or higher
- A Dimensional Infusion (consumed upon leaving the Wizard Tower area in the Rift)

### Obtaining Dimensional Infusions
- **Free infusions**: Players receive 1 free Dimensional Infusion every 4 hours, stacking up to 3.
- **Grand Experience Bottles**: Pay the Wizard 32 Grand Experience Bottles.
- **Bits**: Pay the Wizard 200 Bits. Players can also toggle Auto-Buy to automatically purchase infusions.
- **Rift Prism**: Having a Rift Prism in inventory or Accessory Bag, or imbued through Erihann, grants free entry.

### Entry Details
- Enter through the Wizard Portal in the Wilderness (by the mountain on the Hub Island).
- A Dimensional Infusion is NOT consumed upon entering -- only upon leaving the Wizard Tower in the Rift.
- If a player joins the Rift 3 times using the same infusion, it is consumed automatically.
- Server reboots refund the infusion.

## Rift Zones

The Rift has 9 main zones:

### 1. Wyld Woods
- Home of the Argofays
- **Mobs**: Shies (drop Shy Crux)
- **Key NPCs**: Argofay Traffickers (sell Wyld Armor), Argofay Threebrothers (sell Wyld Sword), Argofay Bughunter, Reed, Sad Jacquelle (gives Montezuma Pet), Enigma, Inverted Sirius (Rift Transfer Chest)
- **Activities**: Craft Crux Talisman from Shy Cruxes, buy Wyld Armor set, find Montezuma soul fragments, unlock Enigma Cloak, stabilize Inverted Sirius for item transfers

### 2. Black Lagoon
- Thin strip of dangerous land with mushroom hazards
- **Mobs**: Deadgehogs (drop Deadgehog Spines)
- **Boss**: Leech Supreme (in the Leeches Lair, drops ~3,000 Motes)
- **Activities**: Craft Not-Dead-Gehog Mask, defeat Leech Supreme (leads to a Timecharm), find Montezuma fragment behind a waterfall

### 3. West Village
- Focus of Dr. Emmett's studies; reality is most twisted here
- **Mobs**: Shadows (drop Shadow Crux)
- **Key NPCs**: Dr. Emmett, Sorcerer Okron, Kat, Unhinged Kloon, Gunther, Plumber Joe
- **Activities**: Craft Crux Ring, complete the Mirrorverse puzzles (leads to a Timecharm), collect glyphs for Sorcerer Okron's warding spells, clean Infested House for Disinfestor Gloves, hack terminals for Defective Monitor, complete Gunther's Race for Gunther's Sneakers

### 4. Dreadfarm
- Crops unique to the Rift dimension, fertilized by a long-gone necromancer
- **Mobs**: Volts (drop Volt Crux)
- **Key NPCs**: Shania
- **Activities**: Craft Crux Artifact, collect Wilted Berberis for Reed's boat, harvest crops for Shania to craft Chicken Leggs (leads to a Timecharm), find Montezuma fragment behind a breakable wall (use Horsezooka)

### 5. Village Plaza
- Central village area; dangerous Endermen roam here
- **Mobs**: Scribes (drop Scribe Crux)
- **Key NPCs**: Barry, Damia (Shen's auctions), Cowboy Nick, Marcia, Detective Amog, Seymour, Detransfigured Seraphine
- **Activities**: Craft Crux Relic, help Barry with protestors (leads to a Timecharm), bid on items in Barrier Street, collect Half-Eaten Carrots for Reed and Cowboy Nick, solve the Reverse-Murder Investigation, buy Exceedingly Comfy Sneakers from Seymour

### 6. Living Cave
- Living walls that are as alive as the player
- **Mobs**: Frozilles (drop Frosty Crux), Autocaps, Autochests, Autopants, Autoboots, Autonulls
- **Activities**: Craft Crux Heirloom, craft Self-Recursive Pickaxe from Nullified Metal, juice up armor for Living Metal Suit bonus to enter Living Stillness, craft Living Timecharm (leads to a Timecharm), obtain Living Metal Heart from calming Living Metal Snakes with Frozen Water Pungi

### 7. Colosseum
- Infested by a toxic slime channeling memories of Dante
- **Boss**: Bacte (drops Bacte Fragments, ~6,000 Motes; leads to a Timecharm)
- **Activities**: Collect Bacte Fragments for Reed's boat, defeat Bacte

### 8. Stillgore Chateau
- A castle settled by a cult of vampires
- **Mobs**: Splatters (drop Splatter Crux), Thralls, Fledglings
- **Activities**: Craft Crux Chronomicon, access Vampire Slayer, craft Healing Melon from Vampiric Melons, find Montezuma fragment near Deer's location

### 9. The Mountaintop
- The tip of the dimension where the SkyBlock world originally fractured
- **Mobs**: Puffs (drop Puff Crux)
- **Key NPCs**: Shen Citrinitas, Wizardman, Detective Amog, Madame Eleanor Q. Goldsworth (multiple versions)
- **Activities**: Craft Celestial Starstone, complete Walk of Fame and Cerebral Citadel puzzles (leads to a Timecharm), complete Wizardman Trials for Wizardman Armor, help all the Eleanors (leads to a Timecharm), close the Breach (final goal)

## Rift Time

Rift Time determines how long a player can stay in the Rift before being teleported back to the Hub. Each point equals one second.

### Base Rift Time
- **Base**: 480 seconds (8 minutes)
- **Maximum possible**: ~4,919 seconds (~82 minutes) with all upgrades

### Time Does NOT Deplete In
- Wizard Tower (except bottom floor)
- Rift Gallery
- Mirrorverse
- Near the Colosseum (depletes at half speed)

### Warning
- Players are warned at 10 seconds remaining
- The last 10 seconds are immune to time-penalty effects
- At 0 seconds, the player is teleported to the Hub Wizard Tower

### Ways to Increase Rift Time

**Permanent sources (one-time unlocks):**
- Porhtal's Eyes: up to +280s total (7 eyes, increasing amounts)
- Reed's Boat Project: up to +130s (5 steps)
- Vampire Slayer levels: up to +235s (levels 1-5 grant 20, 30, 45, 60, 80)
- Sun Gecko Essence Shop (Blessing of Time): up to +90s (3 tiers)
- McGrubber's Burger: up to +60s (5 burgers, +12s each)
- Spotlite: up to +125s (5 consumptions, +25s each)

**Equipment-based Rift Time (best in slot per piece):**
- Helmet: Warmer Wizard Face (+130s)
- Chestplate: W Logo (+85s)
- Leggings: Wizardman Leggings (+100s)
- Boots: Ugly Boots (+80s)
- Belt: Golden Belt (+100s + 5%)
- Cloak: Enigma Cloak (+0s to +1,040s based on Enigma Souls collected)
- Necklace: Rift Necklace (+60s to +380s based on Timecharms secured)
- Bracelet: Shen's Ringalia (+50s)
- Gloves: Disinfestor Gloves (+30s)

**Key accessories for Rift Time:**
- Celestial Starstone: +130s base + up to +140s from mob milestones
- Hocus-Pocus Cipher: +65s
- Defective Monitor: +60s
- Ring of Broken/Eternal Love: +80s
- Warding Trinket: +40s
- Bluetooth Ring: +30s
- Crux accessory line: Crux Talisman (+10s) up to Celestial Starstone (+130s)

**Pet:**
- Montezuma Pet: +15s base + 15s per soul piece (9 total) + 30s from Dead Cat Food

## Motes (Currency)

Motes are the primary currency in the Rift. They cannot be used outside the Rift.

### How to Earn Motes

**Orbs:**
- Pink particle orbs spawn throughout the Rift
- Walking into an orb grants +25 Motes and +2 Rift Time
- Orbs in Enigma's Crib grant only +10 Motes with no Rift Time
- Maximum 100 orbs on the island at once; orbs cannot spawn within 20 blocks of another

**Mob drops and boss kills:**
- Leech Supreme: ~3,000 Motes per kill (good early game)
- Autonulls: 400 Motes each (good mid-game with SkyBlock Citizen Timecharm)
- Autoboots: 500 Motes each
- Bacte: ~6,000 Motes per kill (good with Living Timecharm)
- Thralls/Fledglings in Stillgore Chateau: ~200 Motes each; Enchanted Vampiric Melons sell for ~21,000 Motes

**Selling items:**
- Sell items to the Motes Grubber NPC

**One-time rewards:**
- Mirrorverse completion: 60,000 Motes from Dr. Emmett

**Ubik's Cube:**
- Every 2 hours, challenge an NPC to Split or Steal
- The rightmost NPC to Ubik Von Neumann has a 100% chance to split
- Earns 25,000 to 45,000 Motes depending on rounds

### McGrubber's Burger Bonus
Each consumed McGrubber's Burger (up to 5) grants a Grubber Stack:
- +5% Motes from Motes Grubber sales per stack
- +40 Motes on orb pickup per stack (does not affect Enigma's Crib orbs)

### Motes Strategy (Progression)
1. **Early**: Fight Leech Supreme for 3,000 Motes per boss
2. **Mid**: After SkyBlock Citizen Timecharm, farm Autonulls in Living Cave (400 Motes each)
3. **Late-mid**: After Living Timecharm, farm Bacte for 6,000 Motes per boss
4. **Late**: After Globulate Timecharm + Rift Boat, farm Thralls/Fledglings at Stillgore Chateau for Enchanted Vampiric Melons (21,000 Motes each)
5. **Passive**: Ubik's Cube every 2 hours for 25,000-45,000 Motes

## Mirrorverse

The Mirrorverse is a puzzle dungeon in the West Village, accessed after securing the Supreme Timecharm with at least 3:30 of Rift Time remaining. Rift Time is frozen while inside.

### Puzzle 1: Lever Room
- Match levers on your side to the mirror's reflection (all levers face upwards)
- Dr. Emmett in this room allows fast travel to any previously completed puzzle

### Puzzle 2: Lava Path
- Follow the path of Iron Blocks visible in the mirror's reflection
- Stepping on lava sends you back to the start

### Puzzle 3: Crafting Room
- Kill mobs based on their mirrored positions to obtain crafting materials
- Baby zombies drop knalP dooW kaO, cave spiders drop gnirtS, slimes drop llabemilS
- Craft items together to make a remmaH yniT (Tiny Hammer reversed)

### Puzzle 4: Parkour
- Only platforms visible in the mirror (above) are safe to stand on
- Incorrect platforms vanish; lava contact flings you back
- **Hard Mode**: Press the wooden button at the start. Platforms collapse over time, lava does not launch you. Reward: Test Bucket Please Ignore

### Puzzle 5: Mirror Switching
- Four rooms with mirrors on each side; click a mirror to switch to it, freezing the previous
- The last room is optional. Completing it rewards the Big Brain Talisman

### Puzzle 6: Dance Floor
- Stand on colored blocks and follow on-screen commands (Move, Sneak, Stand, Jump, Don't Jump, Punch)
- Commands get more complex as cycles progress
- After cycle 7, a hole appears to continue to Puzzle 7
- **Full Dance**: Continuing all 12 cycles rewards the Tiny Dancer accessory

### Puzzle 7: Vertical Parkour
- Invisible platforms; use the mirror to see which blocks are safe
- Claim an Emmett Pointer from Dr. Emmett to shoot lasers through the mirror highlighting invisible blocks
- 3 mirrors on different walls serve as checkpoints
- Completing without checkpoints rewards the Miniaturized Tubulator

### Reward Room (Room 8)
- Dr. Emmett congratulates you
- Claim 60,000 Motes (one-time)
- Purchase: mrahcemiT esrevrorriM, etalptsehC norI, Enchanted Book Bundle (Reflection I)

## McGrubber's Burger

- **Rarity**: Epic permanent consumable
- **Source**: Tier 4+ Riftstalker Bloodfiend (Vampire Slayer), requires Vampire Slayer 5
- **Drop rates**: Tier 4: 1.22% (1/82), Tier 5: 1.2% (1/83)
- **Maximum**: 5 can be consumed (5 Grubber Stacks)
- **Each stack grants**:
  - +12 Rift Time
  - +5% Motes from Motes Grubber
  - +40 Motes on orb pickup
  - +5 SkyBlock XP
- **Tips**: Grind using Tier 4 bosses (easier, similar drop rate). Use Ananke Feathers to guarantee drops (12 total needed for all 5 burgers within 5 bosses).

## Enigma Souls

Collectible orbs hidden throughout the Rift, traded to the Enigma NPC in Enigma's Crib.

- **Total**: 52 Enigma Souls
- Trade in groups of 4 to Enigma for Motes
- Each trade also grants +2 Speed and +4 Mana Regen on the Enigma Cloak
- Fully upgraded Enigma Cloak provides up to +1,040 Rift Time
- Some souls require purchases from NPCs (Roy, Cryptosis, Fairylosopher) or special interactions (sitting with Lonely Avaeikx for 3 minutes, completing Tel Kar's conversation)

## Key NPCs and Quests

### Porhtal (Fast Travel)
- 7 eyes scattered across zones (Wizard Tower, Lagoon Hut, Dreadfarm, Village Plaza, Colosseum, Stillgore Chateau, Mountaintop)
- Calming each eye unlocks fast travel to that zone and grants increasing Rift Time (+10, +20, +30, +40, +50, +60, +70)

### Reed (Boat Quest)
- Multi-step quest to build the Rift Boat
- Step 1: 16 Larva Silk (Wyld Woods) -- +20 Rift Time
- Step 2: 64 Wilted Berberis (Dreadfarm) -- +20 Rift Time
- Step 3: 128 Half-Eaten Carrots (Village Plaza) -- +30 Rift Time
- Step 4: Living Metal Anchor (Living Cave) -- +30 Rift Time
- Step 5: 8 Bacte Fragments (Colosseum) -- +30 Rift Time
- Total: +130 Rift Time; unlocks the Rift Boat

### Barry (Protestors Quest)
- Help Barry deal with 7 protestors (Creed, Dust, Frankie, Harriette, Soma, Stain, Violetta)
- Leads to the SkyBlock Citizen Timecharm

### Montezuma Pet (Soul Fragments)
- 9 soul fragments hidden across zones
- Each fragment upgrades the Montezuma pet (+15 Rift Time per fragment)
- Obtained from Sad Jacquelle after finding fragments
- Fragment locations: Wyld Woods (under tree), Rift Gallery (house with stone button), Black Lagoon (behind waterfall), West Village (rooftop near Plumber Joe), Dreadfarm (behind breakable wall), Village Plaza (Book in a Book), Village Plaza (floating island in Deja Vu Alley), Living Stillness (under snow), Stillgore Chateau (near Deer)

### Detective Amog (Reverse-Murder Investigation)
- Help solve the planned murder of Ramero -- where you are the murderer
- Returns on Mountaintop for a second and third reverse-murder

### Inverted Sirius (Rift Transfer Chest)
- Located behind the Dark Auction area
- Allows transferring Rift Transferable items between dimensions
- Must stabilize him first by obtaining Stability Elixir from the Shifted Tavern

## Rift Mechanics

### Stats in the Rift
Only certain stats carry over:
- Rift Time, Rift Damage, Speed, Intelligence, Magic Find, Mana Regen, Hearts

### Items and Transfers
- Normal items, stats, and potion effects do NOT carry between dimensions
- **Rift Transferable** items: Can move freely between dimensions via the Rift Transfer Chest (includes Rift Prism, Bluetooth Ring, Blood Donor accessories, Scarf's Studies, Silver Fang, Weird/Weirder Tuba, Eleanor's armor set, and many more)
- **Rift Exportable** items: Can be exported OUT of the Rift but lose Motes value (includes Bubba Blister, Fang-tastic Chocolate Chip, Guardian Lucky Block, Unfanged Vampire Part, Highlite, Discrite, Spotlite)

### Other Mechanics
- No fall damage in the Rift
- Only Mayors Diaz and Aura affect the Rift
- The Crux accessory line (Crux Talisman through Celestial Starstone) provides both base Rift Time and bonus time from mob kill milestones (up to +140s from milestones at max tier)

## Rift Progression Tips

1. **First visit**: Talk to the Wizard atop the Wizard Tower to unlock the Rift Guide (/riftguide). Buy Wyld Armor from Argofay Traffickers and the Wyld Sword from the Threebrothers immediately.
2. **Early priorities**: Get the Enigma Cloak from Enigma, start collecting Enigma Souls, and calm Porhtal's eyes for fast travel and Rift Time.
3. **Build Rift Time first**: Focus on Reed's boat quest steps, Porhtal's eyes, and equipping Rift Time gear before trying to push further zones.
4. **Timecharm progression**: Follow the Rift Guide tab by tab. Each Timecharm unlocks the next set of zones.
5. **Motes farming**: Start with Leech Supreme, progress to Autonulls, then Bacte, then Chateau mobs as you unlock zones.
6. **Mirrorverse**: Bring at least 3:30 of Rift Time. Get the Emmett Pointer for Puzzle 7. Complete the full dance in Puzzle 6 for Tiny Dancer.
7. **Vampire Slayer**: Unlocked at Stillgore Chateau. Required for McGrubber's Burgers (Vamp Slayer 5) and the Vampiric Timecharm.
8. **Endgame goals**: Close the Breach at the Mountaintop (requires substantial Rift Time), complete all Timecharms, max Enigma Cloak (52 souls), obtain all 5 McGrubber's Burgers, complete Wizardman Trials.
9. **Rift Transfer Chest**: Unlock early by helping Inverted Sirius. Essential for moving key items like the Rift Prism, Bluetooth Ring, and exported valuables.
10. **Use /riftguide**: The in-game Rift Guide tracks all tasks by zone and shows what leads to Timecharms.
