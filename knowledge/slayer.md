# Slayer

Slayers are missions which involve killing mobs of a specific type to spawn a strong boss, then slaying that boss. Defeating the bosses grants Slayer XP; leveling up in Slayer unlocks rarer and more valuable drops from the boss, and more powerful items to use against the boss, and eventually in general.

## Mechanics
Slayer Quests are started by talking to Maddox the Slayer underneath the Tavern at -83, 68, -129 , which is to the right of the ⏣ Graveyard and behind the ⏣ Combat Settlement , talking to him remotely using the Maddox Batphone unlocked at Wolf Slayer LVL III , or using an Abiphone with Maddox the Slayer in the contact list. Vampire Slayer Quests, however, are started by talking to Maddox the Slayer (Rift) in the ф Stillgore Château or calling him remotely using the Maddox Badphone . Boss quests require Coins or Motes to start depending on the tier of the boss and the dimension type. Only 1 Slayer Quest can be active at a time per dimension.
Once a quest is started, the specified amount of Combat XP must be earned from killing the corresponding mob type. The current Slayer Quest and the current progress is shown in the scoreboard. In Tier III and higher quests, there is a chance to spawn a Slayer mini-boss per Combat XP earned, which grants higher amounts of Combat XP. When the amount is reached, the boss spawns at the location of the last mob killed.
Slayer Bosses are bosses of that Slayer's type. They each have unique abilities, and higher tiers have stronger abilities. These abilities, along with the boss’s ❤ Health and damage per second, can be viewed in Maddox's GUI. Slayer bosses must be defeated within a certain time after spawning; if the boss is not defeated when the time runs out, the boss will disappear and the quest will fail. They also cannot be within the vicinity of the Village , as the same result will occur.
All Slayer Bosses will perform a critical attack if the Player stands in one place for too long. The damage number is green with an up arrow next to it. A critical attack sound will play to notify the player. The attack sends the targeted player into the air and toward the boss. This attack has unlimited range. Also, all bosses will regenerate ❤ Health rapidly, if the boss has not hit the Player for a while. All bosses are immune to the effects of the Knockback enchantment.
Each successful quest completion awards Slayer XP, which increases the player's slayer level for that boss. Slayer levels give permanent stat boosts as well as unlocking Slayer item recipes (view unlocked recipes by clicking the book while viewing a specific Slayer).
After fighting a boss, the token drop ( Revenant Flesh for the Revenant Horror , Tarantula Web for the Tarantula Broodfather , Wolf Tooth for the Sven Packmaster , Null Sphere for the Voidgloom Seraph , Coven Seal for the Riftstalker Bloodfiend , and Derelict Ashe for the Inferno Demonlord ) is always 1 Guaranteed , with other drops on a separate drop table, and rare items and runes on yet another drop table. Therefore, it is possible, albeit unlikely, to get 3 different drops from a single slayer boss at higher tiers.

## Rewards
Players receive various rewards for reaching certain Slayer levels:
- Slayer Level - Earning levels in each slayer category will award a permanent stat boost.
- Global Combat Wisdom Buff - Earn extra ☯ Combat Wisdom based on unique Slayer boss kills. Beating a new boss on Tier I , Tier II , and Tier III will give +1 ☯ Combat Wisdom , while Tier IV and Tier V bosses will give +2 ☯ Combat Wisdom . The current maximum bonus is a permanent +34 ☯ Combat Wisdom .
- Slayer Bonus Rewards Earn +3 of any slayer boss's main token drop when slaying its mini-bosses. This bonus is unlocked upon reaching LVL 6 in Zombie, Wolf, and Spider Slayer. Slayers are 4% cheaper. This bonus is unlocked upon reaching LVL 7 in Zombie, Wolf, and Spider Slayer.
- Earn +3 of any slayer boss's main token drop when slaying its mini-bosses. This bonus is unlocked upon reaching LVL 6 in Zombie, Wolf, and Spider Slayer.
- Slayers are 4% cheaper. This bonus is unlocked upon reaching LVL 7 in Zombie, Wolf, and Spider Slayer.
- Level 8 Perks - Each slayer path unlocks a special perk unique to that path when the player reaches level 8. Enderman Slayer additionally has a level 9 perk.
- Bartender Mixins - A Mixin from the Bartender is unlocked upon reaching LVL 8 in a slayer. These Mixins can be applied to God Potions or consumed separately to give them bonus special effects.
- Skyblock Levels - Upon levelling Slayer Level or defeating all tiers of Slayer boss in one catagory, Skyblock XP is given, which is used to level up an important Stat called Skyblock Levels .
- Bits - Depending on the tier, each slayer boss will give a certain amount of bits, Tier I - 2, Tier II - 5, Tier III - 8, Tier IV - 12, Tier V - 15.

## Auto-Slayer
Auto-Slayer is a feature unlocked upon reaching LVL 6 in the Zombie , Spider and Wolf Slayers. It allows players to automatically start a Slayer quest of the same type after their current Slayer quest has been completed successfully.
Auto-Slayer will only begin a new quest if the player has sufficient coins in their purse or Bank . The Auto-Slayer system firstly attempts to withdraw coins from the purse: if it fails, it will withdraw from their Bank , similar to opening Dungeon Reward Chests . If neither source contains sufficient coins , a new quest simply won't start and the player must start a new quest manually once they have enough coins .
Auto-Slayer can be enabled and disabled via the Slayer menu accessed from Maddox the Slayer .

## RNG Meter
You can help the wiki by editing this page and reworking it.
The RNG Meter is unlocked for each individual boss type upon slaying their respective Tier III boss. XP gained from killing any slayer boss from Tier III to Tier V will go towards filling up the RNG Meter. By selecting an item, the chance of that item dropping is increased based off of your progress in the RNG Meter: once the meter is filled, the item will be guaranteed to drop the next time one kills that specific Slayer boss.
RNG Meters exist for Zombie, Spider, Wolf, Enderman, Vampire, and Blaze Slayers.

## Trivia
- The mini-bosses that spawn during Slayer quests despawn if they are not defeated for a while.
- Contributing 24 times to the Bartender's Brewery project grants a 5% discount on Slayers. It overrides and does not stack with the 4% discount at all Slayers level 7. This discount is no longer available.
- There are plans for more unique bosses for Slayer, as well as Tier V bosses for Tarantula Broodfather , Sven Packmaster and Voidgloom Seraph .
- If the server reboots when the player has an active slayer quest, the boss will despawn, and progress is set to 1 Combat XP away from spawning the boss. The next mob killed will immediately spawn the boss again.
- When Enderman Slayer was released, all players lost access to Auto-Slayer due to not having Enderman Slayer LVL 6. However, players who had it on before the update would be able to use it, and would not be able to disable it.
- Without any external bonuses, 2000 Tier IV Slayer quests are required in order to get a Slayer to LVL 9. This costs 100,000,000 coins per Slayer not counting failed quests.
- The global combat XP buff is additive with the combat XP buff from the Hunter Talisman or the Hunter Ring and with the Viking's tear brew in experience potions (included in God Potions ).

## Behavior
The Revenant Horror is a Zombie-type mob which is hostile towards all nearby players. It moves at a relatively fast pace and deals damage with its scythe weapon. It receives a speed boost on hit.
All Revenant Horrors are immune to potion effects . They are immune to arrows ; when hit by them, they heal. They are immune to the item abilities of Aspect of the Dragons and Ice Spray Wand .
As with all Slayer bosses, if the Revenant Horror has not dealt damage to a player for some time, it will begin rapidly regenerating health . The regeneration immediately stops when it hits a player again. Additionally, if either it or its target stays in the same block for too long, it performs a slam-dunk attack, knocking the target towards it and dealing ✷ True Damage . If 3 minutes pass without the boss being defeated, it will despawn, failing the Slayer quest. A timer is shown above its head indicating the remaining time.
If the Revenant Horror enters the Village area, it will immediately despawn and the Slayer quest will be failed.
Tier I through Tier IV Revenant Horror have all the abilities of the tiers below them.

### Tier III and Tier IV
After 40 seconds, it will enter a "Mad" state, in which its behavior is not changed. After another 5 seconds, it will enter an "Enraged" state, in which it moves slightly faster and deals highly increased amounts of damage. This enraged state lasts for 12 seconds.

### Tier V (Atoned Horror)
The Tier V boss is much different from the bosses preceding it. It has a different appearance and is named "Atoned Horror" instead of "Revenant Horror". It is also immune to all types of ability damage, Wither Skeleton Pet 's Death's Touch ability, as well as damage from Bonemerang . It has a natural regeneration effect, which heals for 1/1000 of its maximum health(10,000 ❤ Health normally, 20,000 ❤ Health with Mayor Derpy ) per second.
Every second, a circle will appear beneath its target's feet similar to the Gaia Construct , but made of different blocks and larger in size. The center is red and progresses to white. After a short delay, the Atoned Horror will throw a block of TNT to the center of that circle. The TNT explodes on contact with the ground, damaging enemies within the circle for a percentage of their health, depending on how close they are to the circle's center. When the player is hit by the TNT the boss heals 500,000 health. Upon reaching 1/3 health, the boss enters an enhanced stage, throwing TNT twice as often. The boss remains in this state even if it regenerates health back to above 1/3.
After 30 seconds, it will stop moving and attacking, and a circle of expanding bedrock forms around its feet. A text appears above its head labeled "Boom!" and a hidden timer counts down from 5 seconds. Nearby mobs flee from the Atoned Horror when this happens. Upon reaching 0, the Atoned Horror unleashes a barrage of lightning around it, similar to Storm 's Giga Lightning, killing anything within a 7 block radius. This attack bypasses protection from the End Stone Sword and Mithril Coat . After that, it resumes attacking as before.

## Tips
- Any of the Dragon Armors reforged to Fierce are recommended options if the player is looking to take down a Revenant Horror Tier III .
- For the Revenant Horror Tier IV , using Shadow Assassin Armor reforged on to Ancient or a better armor set, and paired with a max enchanted Reaper Falchion can help immensely.
- It is possible to kill the Tier IV or lower very quickly using a MYTHIC Guardian Pet with a highly intelligent Armor Set such as Necrotic Wise Dragon Armor or Storm's Armor .
- If the Revenant Horror gets enraged, it is advised to run away until it reverts to its normal state.
- If the player or the boss stays on the same block space for too long, it will result in the player taking ✷ True Damage and massive ⬱ Knockback from the boss. This only applies to Tier III and above, however.
- Trapping the Revenant Horror in a lift in the ⏣ Deep Caverns can make the fight a lot easier. Additionally, spawning the boss in the ⏣ Diamond Reserve or ⏣ Obsidian Sanctuary and luring it into Lava can hugely reduce the Revenant Horror 's mobility and prevent it from landing hits on the player; however, it can still use Life Drain which can drain the player's ❤ Health from a distance.
- For Tier III or higher bosses, healing items such as Power Orbs and weapons with Life Steal or Drain are essential.
- When standing at -138, 42, -141 in the ⏣ Hub Crypts , the Tier V TNT attack cannot hit the player. Therefore, using this spot can let players focus on ❁ Damage instead of high ❤ Effective Health .

## Behavior
The Tarantula Broodfather is a Spider-type mob hostile to all players. It has the appearance of a cave spider riding a spider. It is agile, able to move quickly, climb walls and jump huge distances. It deals melee damage. Often, it will change targets to other nearby players.
All Tarantula Broodfathers are immune to the abilities of Aspect of the Dragons and Ice Spray Wand .
As with all Slayer bosses, if the Tarantula Broodfather has not dealt damage to a player for some time, it will begin rapidly regenerating health. The regeneration immediately stops when it hits a player again. Additionally, if either it or its target stays in the same block for too long, it performs a slam-dunk attack, knocking the target towards it and dealing ✷ True Damage . If 3 minutes pass without the boss being defeated, it will despawn, failing the Slayer quest. A timer is shown above its head indicating the remaining time.
Each tier has all of the abilities from the ones before it.

### Tier I
- Backstab - The Broodfather will occasionally leap behind the player and inflict 50% more ❁ Damage .

### Tier II
- Backstab - The Broodfather will occasionally leap behind the player and inflict 75% more ❁ Damage .
- Noxious Paralysis - The Broodfather reduces the player's ♨ Vitality by 25% when the boss is alive and reduces their ✦ Speed by 0.5% for every fifth hit it inflicts. This ✦ Speed reduction stacks.

### Tier III
- Backstab - The Broodfather will occasionally leap behind the player and inflict 100% more ❁ Damage .
- Noxious Paralysis - The Broodfather reduces the player's ♨ Vitality by 50% when the boss is alive and reduces their ✦ Speed by 1% for every fifth hit it inflicts.
- Web of Lies - Occurs at 600k ❤ (66%) & 300k ❤ (33%). The Broodfather will leap to a nearby area and begins spewing web to surround itself. While the Web of Lies is ongoing, it disables teleportation magic unless the player breaks 2 Egg Sacs above the boss with 3 health per Egg Sac. If the egg sacs hatch, then they spawn the Broodfather's minions to attack the player.

### Tier IV
- Backstab - The Broodfather will occasionally leap behind the player and inflict 125% more ❁ Damage .
- Noxious Paralysis - The Broodfather reduces the player's ♨ Vitality by 75% when the boss is alive and reduces their ✦ Speed by 1% for every fifth hit it inflicts.
- Web of Lies - Occurs at 1.6M ❤ (66%) & 800k ❤ (33%). The player needs to deal with 3 Egg Sacs above the boss with each Egg Sac having 6 health.
- Bat Barrage - The Broodfather will periodically spawn a horde of bats to inflict 1% of max ❤ Health as ❁ Damage while becoming immune to Magic Damage.

### Tier V (Primordial Broodfather)
Like the Revenant Horror Tier V , the Broodfather has a natural regeneration effect and immunity to Ability Damage .
- Backstab - The Broodfather will occasionally leap behind the player and inflict 150% more ❁ Damage .
- Noxious Paralysis - The Broodfather reduces the player's ♨ Vitality by 99% when the boss is alive and reduces their ✦ Speed by 2% for every fifth hit it inflicts.
- Web of Lies - Occurs at 6.7M ❤ (66%) & 3.3M ❤ (33%). The player must deal with 4 Egg Sacs above the boss with each Egg Sac having 9 health.
- Bat Barrage - The Broodfather will periodically spawn a horde of Primordial Bats to inflict 2% of max ❤ Health as ❁ Damage .
- Till Death Do Us Part - Upon killing the Broodfather, it summons the Primordial Broodmother and fuses to become the Conjoined Brood. Its stats doubles that of Tarantula Broodfather Tier V and only retains its passive abilities ( Backstab & Noxious Paralysis ).

## Tips
- It is recommended to use Tarantula Armor to kill a Tarantula Broodfather Tier IV with at least 10,000 kills on the armor, as its piece bonus grants higher ❈ Defense against spiders, the more spiders have been killed with the set equipped. Alternatively, a high damaging set such as Superior Dragon Armor or Shadow Assassin Armor sets is a sizable improvement over Tarantula Armor in ❁ Damage , and it can decrease the time for each slayer by a measurable amount, due to it focusing on ❁ Damage rather than ❈ Defense .
- Alternatively, a high damaging set such as Superior Dragon Armor or Shadow Assassin Armor sets is a sizable improvement over Tarantula Armor in ❁ Damage , and it can decrease the time for each slayer by a measurable amount, due to it focusing on ❁ Damage rather than ❈ Defense .
- A Radiant Power Orb or higher, paired with a Spider Hat is recommended for fighting Tarantula Broodfather Tier III and above, as an alternative to Tarantula Armor .
- It is highly recommended to use Healing Enchantments like Life Steal and Vampirism on weapons. If the player 's ❤ Health gets too low while fighting a Tarantula Broodfather , killing a couple of nearby spiders can activate Vampirism and keep the player alive.
- While fighting the Tarantula Broodfather , backing up into a corner reduces the effect of its leaps and makes it much easier to land hits on the boss.
- If the player has a high amount of ✎ Intelligence , a Voodoo Doll can be used to drain the Tarantula Broodfather 's ❤ Health quickly.
- It used to be possible to sword-swap between a Giant's Sword and a Scorpion Foil to one-shot a Tarantula Broodfather Tier IV . This method is no longer available, however, as since sword-swapping has since been patched.
- While the bats themselves are immune to Magic Damage, the ability from Giant's Sword counts as melee and it can be used to clear them.
- Since the release of 0.23.5, it is recommended to bring a Spider Shortbow or better to deal with Egg Sacs at higher tiers. On a ♲ Ironman profile, is recommended to grind Tier III bosses until one obtains 3x Spider Catalyst to craft one, as it is much rarer from higher tiers.

## Behavior
The Sven Packmaster is a Wolf-type mob which is hostile towards all players. It is very fast and has a melee attack.
As with all Slayer bosses, if the Sven Packmaster has not dealt damage to a player for some time, it will begin rapidly regenerating health. The regeneration immediately stops when it hits a player again. Additionally, if either it or its target stays in the same block for too long, it performs a slam-dunk attack, knocking the target towards it and dealing ✷ True Damage . If 4 minutes pass without the boss being defeated, it will despawn, failing the Slayer quest. A timer is shown above its head indicating the remaining time.
Like the Revenant Horror, if the Sven Packmaster enters the village it will immediately despawn and you will fail the Slayer Quest.
Each tier has all of the abilities from the one before it.

### Tier III and Tier IV
Upon reaching 50% health, the Sven Packmaster will begin moving very slowly, and no longer regenerates health ; critical particles appear around it. It spawns 1 Sven Pup at its location every second for 5 seconds, during which it is protected from attacks. Each Sven Pup has 10% of the max health of the Packmaster. When it is spawning pups, the text above its head reads "Calling the pups!", after which it says "Protected". If all the pups are killed, the Packmaster returns to its original behaviour. When either the pups or the Packmaster attacked the player, the Packmaster won't regenerate health.

# Voidgloom Seraph

The Voidgloom Seraph (/ˈsɛrəf/, "the burning one"; plural Seraphim /ˈsɛrəfɪm/) is a Slayer Boss in the Enderman Slayer branch. It is an Enderman with strong attacks. It is significantly stronger than the previous Slayer bosses.
Defeating the Tier III Voidgloom Seraph results in unlocking the Blaze Slayer quest and the Inferno Demonlord boss.

## Behavior
The Voidgloom Seraph is an Enderman-type mob hostile to all players. It has the appearance of an enderman with critical particles following it. It has a smaller aggro range compared to other Slayers.
Aspect of the Dragons and Ice Spray Wand don't affect Voidgloom Seraph. They are immune to water damage. Like normal Endermen, they are also immune to the damage from most arrows, except those shot using a Juju Shortbow or Terminator . However, regardless of the bow type, they can all apply 1 hit per arrow.
When the Voidgloom Seraphim's Hitshield is not active, ⫽ Ferocity that is effective on them is divided by 4.
As with all Slayer bosses, if the Voidgloom Seraph has not dealt damage to a player for some time, it will begin rapidly regenerating ❤ Health . The regeneration immediately stops when it hits a player again. If 4 minutes pass without the boss being defeated, it will despawn, failing the Slayer quest. A timer is shown above its head indicating the remaining time.
Voidling Fanatic and Voidling Extremist spawns are suppressed in the vicinity of a Voidgloom Seraph.
Each tier has all of the abilities from the ones before it.

### Tier I
- Malevolent Hitshield appears upon spawning and reaching 200k ❤ (2/3) and 100k ❤ (1/3). The boss does not gain DPS while the shield is up. The shield has no invincibility frames. All types of arrows can remove 1 hit per shot from the shield. It has 15 hits. Purple particles surround the Seraph when its shield is up. When attacked in this state, it teleports into a block within a 1 block radius by up to 60 times per second. Upon the shield being destroyed, the boss resumes attacking as normal.
- Dissonance begin when the boss is alive. The boss teleports behind or next to the player who spawned it, which happens more often if the player doesn't move. It deals half of its DPS to all players fighting it in a 6.5 block radius. However, in and only in Tier I bosses, the AOE damage doesn't apply. The teleportation, however, remains usable for all tiers of bosses.

### Tier II
- Malevolent Hitshield appears upon spawning and reaching 8M ❤ (2/3) and 4M ❤ (1/3). It has 30 hits. The boss gains +3,000 DPS every 3s the shield is up; this bonus DPS is removed once the shield is destroyed.
- Yang Glyphs appear upon reaching 6M ❤ (1/2). The Seraph will begin to hold Yang Glyphs, which appear as a beacon. After 2 seconds, it will throw the glyph to a nearby spot. Any player fighting the Seraph can destroy the glyph by standing adjacent to or on top of it. If it is not destroyed within 5 seconds, it deals 1000 × Max ❤ Health in ✷ True Damage to all players fighting the Seraph. The abilities of the Mithril Coat and Wither Cloak Sword can block this damage. Glyphs cannot be thrown when the Hitshield is up. The Seraph will continue to try to throw Glyphs until it is defeated.

## Trivia
- The Voidgloom Seraph Tier IV used to have 300M ❤ Health , one of the highest ❤ Health of all non-dungeon enemies in the game.
- Yang Glyphs and Nukekubi Fixation heads can be thrown through walls, and in extremely rare cases, to inaccessible locations. For Yang Glyphs , players can use Wither Cloak Sword 's Ability, Creeper Veil to survive. Heads can still be destroyed by looking them.
- Thrown Yang Glyphs can sometimes replace blocks such as stairs, End Stone and glass panes. When the Glyph is destroyed, the block will not be replaced until the server restarts.
- When using Broken Heart Radiation , the particles from the Voidgloom Seraph teleporting to the player will sometimes show. The Seraph will not move.
- The Voidgloom Seraph 's invulnerability during Broken Heart Radiation does not start until the lasers are fired, leaving a brief period of vulnerability in the transition phase.
- If a Voidgloom Seraph Tier IV despawns while attacking with Broken Heart Radiation , the beams will persist indefinitely. These beams are visual only.
- The Voidgloom Seraph 's name may be a reference to Seraph of the End .
- The name of Nukekubi Fixations are a reference to nukekubi , a mythical creature in Japanese folklore whose head detaches from its body.
- When in the Malvolent Hitshield , the Voidgloom Seraph can take damage using bows other than the Juju Shortbow and Terminator , making it easy to quickly defeat the shield.
- The Voidgloom Seraph does not take damage from water, but attacks less when in water.
- If a player deals enough damage to a Tier IV Voidgloom Seraph just as Broken Heart Radiation triggers to also trigger Malevolent Hitshield , the boss will not throw Yang Glyphs during this phase and immediately start in the Hitshields phase after the lasers complete their full rotation.
- Voidgloom Seraph is the only Slayer boss that can be spawned in one type of island, which is ⏣ The End .
- It is possible to grief others' if one hits the Voidgloom Seraph only a couple times by using a invincibility item that blocks ✷ True Damage instead of breaking it yourself.

### Tier I
- Immolate - The Inferno Demonlord shoots fireballs to its target rapidly, only dealing ⬱ Knockback . When the boss is alive, it deals ✷ True Damage depending on its health if players fighting it goes into its aggro range.
- Demonsplit - The boss splits into demons at 50% HP (1,250,000), named Quazii and Typhoeus (a Wither Skeleton and a Pigman appearance respectively). Upon spawning, only Quazii can be damaged. Typhoeus will be immune. Quazii will behave more aggressively, whereas Typhoeus, while immune, will be repelled by the player when within 5 blocks of them. When Quazii is defeated, Typhoeus will adopt more aggressive behavior and can be attacked. When Typhoeus is defeated, the Demonlord reforms and resumes attacking.

### Tier II
- Hellion Shield - The boss has 99% Damage reduction from all sources. Only direct strikes from Blaze Slayer Daggers ( Twilight Dagger , Firedust Dagger , Kindlebane Dagger , Mawdredge Dagger , Pyrochaos Dagger , Deathripper Dagger ) attuned identically to the attunement of the boss's shield, enchantment damage from those strikes, as well as suffocation damage(deals 5% of the demons' health as ✷ True Damage ), will ignore the shield and deal full damage to the boss. This excludes ⫽ Ferocity , so ⫽ Ferocity upgrades are ineffective. Every 8 strikes with the correct attunement, the shield will change its attunement, in order of Ashen > Spirit > Auric > Crystal then it goes back to Ashen . All other attacks have their damage reduced by aforementioned 99%.
- Demonsplit - The Demonlord splits into its demons once at 50% HP (5,000,000). The demons behave as before, but now also have shields, which similarly to Demonlord change every 8 hits. Quazii will alternate only between Spirit and Crystal , while Typhoeus alternates only between Ashen and Auric . When a shield switches, the immunity between Quazii and Typhoeus also switches. Regardless the immunity, both demons walks towards their target, but whoever gets the immunity keeps walking and retreating within a 5 block radius of the target. For example, when Quazii is spawned with Spirit and is hit correspondingly 8 times, Quazii becomes immune to damage, leaves and Typhoeus grants Ashen . Once Typhoeus is hit correspondingly 8 times, Typhoeus becomes immune to damage, leaves and Quazii grants Crystal . This process goes on until the demons are both defeated and the Inferno Demonlord reforms and resumes attacking.
- Fire Pillars - Beginning at 50% HP, every 20 seconds, the boss will prepare a fireball above its head for 3.5 seconds. Then, it will throw it in a random direction, where a 4-block high orange pillar will form. Such pillars must be destroyed within 7 seconds of being placed, by clicking them 8 times while having any Wisp Pet active. The pillar has no invincibility frames. The pillar gets 1 block shorter every two times it is hit until at 8 hits, it is no longer present. If it is not destroyed, all players fighting the Demonlord will take 1000 × Max ❤ Health as ✷ True Damage . This can be blocked by using the Wither Cloak Sword and Mithril Coat 's abilities.

### Tier III
- Demonsplit - The Demonlord splits into its demons twice, at 2/3 HP and 1/3 HP. ROFLcopter - Quazii gains a beam which spins slowly and is centered on Quazii's hitbox. If the player touches the beam, they will take 70% of their ❤ Health in ✷ True Damage , and Quazii will heal 20% of its health (1,000,000 hp at Tier III , and 2,000,000 hp at Tier IV ).
- ROFLcopter - Quazii gains a beam which spins slowly and is centered on Quazii's hitbox. If the player touches the beam, they will take 70% of their ❤ Health in ✷ True Damage , and Quazii will heal 20% of its health (1,000,000 hp at Tier III , and 2,000,000 hp at Tier IV ).
- Fire Pillars - Fire pillars begin at 2/3 HP (30,000,000).
- DDR Apocalypse - Beginning at 1/3 HP (15,000,000), the boss will spawn up to 5 fire pits near the player regularly. Orange terracotta blocks will form in small cross shapes near the player, which turn to fire after 1.5 seconds. The fire deals 200% ❤ Health as ✷ True Damage per second(meaning an instant kill with 100 ❂ True Defense or less, since it reduces the damage of bosses abilities ). Note that fire pits spawn immediately before the demons spawn as well as immediately after.

### Tier IV
The Demonlord functions identically to previous tiers.
- Demonsplit - It splits into its demons twice, at 2/3 HP (100,000,000) and 1/3 HP (50,000,000). Trail of Fire - Typhoeus gains a trail of fire following it. The fire deals 60% of the player's ❤ Health as ✷ True Damage if contact is made.
- Trail of Fire - Typhoeus gains a trail of fire following it. The fire deals 60% of the player's ❤ Health as ✷ True Damage if contact is made.
- Fire Pillars - Fire pillars begin spawning at 2/3 HP (100,000,000).
- DDR Apocalypse - At 1/3 HP (50,000,000), the boss spawns up to 5 fire pits near the player at regular intervals.

## Tips
- While the boss does ✷ True Damage its demons deal high amount of regular ❁ Damage , therefore prioritizing one over the other too much will not work against the Slayer.
- If the player can deal high and rapid damage, the slayer boss will not be able to throw any fire pillars before the split or death. Damage nullification items should be prepared in case the player fails to deal enough damage in time.

## Trivia
- Because of vanilla Minecraft AI and abilities, Typhoeus is stronger than Quazii. Therefore, the Firedust Dagger , Kindlebane Dagger and Pyrochaos Dagger are respectively stronger than the Twilight Dagger , Mawdredge Dagger and Deathripper Dagger if the Burning Vengence ﬗ ability is counted.
- If both Quazii and Typhoeus spawn in a place which is one block large and get pushed, Quazii always gets pushed into the wall and take suffocation damage first.

## Trivia
- Vampire and Enderman Slayers are the only slayer quests exclusive to one island.
- This is the only slayer that does not have minibosses.
- Vampire Slayer is the first slayer of a custom mob.
- Vampire Slayer is the only slayer not to say that it will feature more LVL rewards in the future.
- It is also the only slayer without a Mixin .
