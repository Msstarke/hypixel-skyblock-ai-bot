# Crimson Isle

## Access Requirements

- **Combat Level 22** required to enter
- Accessed via a **Launchpad at the back of the Spider's Den**
- Replaces the old Blazing Fortress as the endgame combat zone
- Mobs on the island have several million HP; this is significantly harder than earlier combat areas

## Areas and Sub-Locations

The Crimson Isle has 21 major sub-locations including:
- **Dragontail** - Barbarian faction hub (Bank, Auction House, Bazaar)
- **Scarleton** - Mage faction hub (Bank, Auction House, Bazaar)
- **Blazing Volcano** - central landmark
- **Crimson Fields** - mob farming area (Magma Cubes)
- **Stronghold** - Blazes
- **The Dojo** - combat challenges
- **Forgotten Skull** - Kuudra entrance
- **Kuudra's Hollow** - Kuudra boss arena
- **Barbarian Outpost**
- **Aura's Lab**
- **The Bastion** - Mage territory (used in Rescue quests)
- **Cathedral** - Barbarian territory (used in Rescue quests)
- **Burning Desert**

## Faction System

Players choose between two factions: **Mages** or **Barbarians**. You can swap factions at any time. Faction choice is made after partially completing the main questline (speaking with Elle).

### Mage Faction
- **Location:** Right side of Crimson Isle (Scarleton)
- **Leader:** Queen Nyx
- **Blacksmith:** Alwin
- **Zone:** Scarleton (guard-patrolled, has Bank/AH/Bazaar)

### Barbarian Faction
- **Location:** Left side of Crimson Isle (Dragontail)
- **Leader:** Chief Scorn
- **Blacksmith:** Igor
- **Zone:** Dragontail (guard-patrolled, has Bank/AH/Bazaar)

### Reputation System

Players start at 0 reputation. Reputation unlocks content and grants SkyBlock XP milestones.

| Title | Reputation Required | SkyBlock XP |
|-------|-------------------|-------------|
| Neutral | 0 | 0 |
| Friendly | 1,000 | 5 |
| Trusted | 3,000 | 10 |
| Honored | 7,000 | 20 |
| Hero | 12,000 | 40 |

### Earning Mage Reputation
- Faction questline completion: +1,000
- Daily faction quests: +30 to +125 each (up to 5/day)
- Daily fights vs. Rollim (7 per day): +25 each
- Pablo's flower requests (every 2 days): +25
- Avorius blood cup (every 3 days): +5
- Mollim experience bottle (once): +20
- Daily mini-boss kills: +20 each
- Kuudra kills: +10/20/30/40/50 (Basic/Hot/Burning/Fiery/Infernal)
- Alchemage quest (once): +250
- Chickzilla kill (requires 7,000 rep, once): +100

### Earning Barbarian Reputation
- Faction questline completion: +1,000
- Daily faction quests: +30 to +125 each (up to 5/day)
- Daily fights vs. Kaus (7 per day): +25 each
- Daily mini-boss kills: +20 each
- Kuudra kills: +10/20/30/40/50 (tier-based)
- Baar's quest (once): +250
- Pomtair's enchanted bone meal: +50/day
- Sirih's sulphur (every 2 days): +10
- Turd's toy recovery (every 5 days): +40

### Reputation Loss
- Killing faction enemies (Fire Mages or Barbarians) reduces reputation by **5**

## Main Questline

### Starting Quest (Both Factions)
**Quest Giver:** Elle (at spawn)

1. Kill 3 Blazes attacking Elle
2. Collect: 4 Magma Chunks (Magma Cubes in Crimson Fields), 3 Blaze Ashes (Blazes in Stronghold), 2 Spectre Dust (Wither Spectres near Elle)
3. Deliver items to Elle
4. Explore the island, speak with 4 villagers from each faction (green particles above heads)
5. Return to Elle and choose Mage or Barbarian faction

### Mage Questline
1. **Batphone Quest** - Receive Maddox Batphone from Elle, present to Dean, obtain Letter of Recommendation
2. **Abiphone Quest** - Purchase Abiphone from Udel or Nuvian, call Dean, meet Elle at spawn
3. **Stolen Research** - Sneak through guards at skull mouth, locate research, report to Dean
4. **Apprentice Mission** - Talk to Captain Ahone, find unconscious apprentice
5. **Resource Collection** - Gather 10 Magma Chunks and 5 Burning Eyes, deliver to Dean
6. **Side Tasks** - Help Igrupan with chickens, obtain Volcanic Stonefish from Odexar, deliver to Odger
7. **Miniboss Defeats** - Kill Bladesoul (Hallowed Skull), Ashfang (Lumino Fiber), Barbarian Duke X (Leather Cloth), deliver to Dean
8. **Final Boss** - Defeat Kuudra in Forgotten Skull

### Barbarian Questline
1. **Initial Mission** - Travel to Burning Desert with Elle, defeat corrupted Flaming Spider, cure injured Piglin (Ugo) with Sulphur, escort to Dragontail
2. **Infiltration** - Sneak past cathedral guards, retrieve research from under Dean's table, deliver to Chief Scorn
3. **Training** - Complete Dojo challenges (Force, Stamina, Mastery at D rank+), defeat Mage Outlaw and Magma Cube Boss
4. **Errands** - Obtain Skeleton Fish and Flyfish for Lasea, obtain Heavy Pearl for Gris
5. **Final Boss** - Defeat Kuudra in Forgotten Skull

Both factions converge on Kuudra as the final boss.

## Faction Quests (Daily)

Players can complete up to **5 faction quests per day** for reputation and rewards. Quests are accessed via the town board at the Community Center and start automatically.

### Quest Types
- **Fishing** - Obtain specific Trophy Fish
- **Fetch** - Deliver materials to the town board
- **Miniboss** - Defeat designated minibosses
- **Rescue** - Find trapped hostages in Cathedral (Barbarian) or The Bastion (Mage)

## Kuudra Boss

Kuudra is a 4-player raid boss described as an "overgrown lava Kraken" located in Kuudra's Hollow, accessed through the Forgotten Skull zone.

### Tiers and Requirements

| Tier | Combat Level | Reputation Required | Crimson Essence (Free Chest) |
|------|-------------|--------------------|-----------------------------|
| Basic | 22 | None | 1-2 |
| Hot | 27 | Unlocked via tier progression | 14-22 |
| Burning | 32 | Higher reputation | 25-30 |
| Fiery | 37 | Higher reputation | 100 |
| Infernal | 42 | Highest reputation | 200 |

### Fight Phases

**Phase 1 - Crates:** Players fish supply crates using lava rods
**Phase 2 - Ballista:** Construct the primary weapon against Kuudra
**Phase 3 - Fuel:** Gather fuel cells to power the ballista
**Phase 4 - Stomach (Burning+):** Players enter Kuudra's interior to break orbs
**Phase 5 - Lair (Infernal only):** Final arena collapse with dodging mechanics

### Enemy Mobs in Kuudra
- Kuudra Followers
- Inferno Magma Cubes
- Wither Sentries
- Dropships
- Various tier-specific adds

### Kuudra Loot

#### Free Chest Rewards
- Basic: 1-2 Crimson Essence
- Hot: 14-22 Crimson Essence
- Burning: 25-30 Crimson Essence
- Fiery: 100 Crimson Essence
- Infernal: 200 Crimson Essence

#### Paid Chest Rewards
- **Armor Sets** (4.31% Basic, scaling down to ~2.95% Infernal): Aurora, Crimson, Fervor, Hollow, Terror
- **Weapons:** Aurora Staff (~1.05%), Hollow Wand (~1.05%)
- **Currency:** 500-2,500 Crimson Essence, 3-100 Kuudra Teeth, 3-10 Heavy Pearls
- **Guaranteed:** 80-2,000 Crimson Essence (Slot 3), 1-3 Kuudra Teeth (Slot 4)
- **Attribute Shards:** Various mob shards by tier (Kraken Shard guaranteed in Slot 5)

#### Rare Drops
- **Tentacle Dye:** 0.001%-0.005% (extremely rare)
- **Burning Kuudra Core:** 0.12% (Burning) to 0.51% (Infernal)
- **Hellstorm Wand:** 0.10% (Infernal only)
- **Tormentor:** 0.10% (Infernal only)
- **Mandraa:** ~1% at higher tiers
- **Wheel of Fate:** 0.53% (Basic) to 1.65% (Infernal)

#### Enchanted Books from Kuudra
- Fatal Tempo, Inferno (rare, ~0.03%)
- Ferocious, Hardened Mana, Mana Vampire, Strong Mana (levels I-V, 9-22%)

## Kuudra Armor Sets

All five Kuudra armor sets follow the same tier system: Basic, Hot, Burning, Fiery, Infernal. Each tier requires the previous tier at 10 Stars, then prestige at a Blacksmith. Stars are lost on prestige.

### Combat Level Requirements (All Sets)
| Tier | Combat Level | Kuudra Tier Required |
|------|-------------|---------------------|
| Basic | 22 | Basic completion |
| Hot | 27 | Hot completion |
| Burning | 32 | Burning completion |
| Fiery | 37 | Fiery completion |
| Infernal | 42 | Infernal completion |

### Upgrade Cost per Piece (to Infernal)
- 141,005 Crimson Essence
- 45 Heavy Pearls
- 160 Kuudra Teeth
- 7,740,000 Coins

### Crimson Armor
- **Role:** Melee DPS / Generalist
- **Set Bonus (Dominus):** Melee hits grant stacks. At 10 stacks, perform a Swipe dealing 15-25% of last melee hit damage (double to first enemy)
- **Stack bonuses:** Spell Rupture, Ferocity (Burning+), +10% Damage (Infernal)
- **Notable:** Considered the best out-of-dungeons generalist melee armor set at Infernal tier
- **Key stats:** High Strength, Crit Damage, HP, Defense

### Terror Armor
- **Role:** Ranged DPS (Archer)
- **Set Bonus (Hydra Strike):** Arrow hits grant stacks. At 10 stacks, fire two additional arrows
- **Stack bonuses:** Arrow Speed increase, side arrow damage scaling by tier
- **Bonus damage at 4/4 Infernal:** +15%
- **Key stats:** High Crit Damage, Speed, HP, Defense
- **Known issue:** Hydra Strike interferes with Overload enchantment and Spirit Bow

### Aurora Armor
- **Role:** Mage DPS
- **Set Bonus (Arcane Energy):** Magic damage grants stacks. At 10 stacks, sneaking fires homing missiles (5,000 base damage, 0.3x ability scaling)
- **Missile count:** 3 (Basic), 4 (Hot/Burning), 5 (Fiery/Infernal)
- **Key stats:** Very high Intelligence, HP, Defense, Ability Damage (helmet)

### Fervor Armor
- **Role:** Tank
- **Set Bonus (Fervor):** Melee attacks generate stacks. At 10 stacks, sneaking triggers Ground Pound (damage scaled by defense, hits within 10 blocks)
- **Full Set Bonus (Intimidate):** 50% chance on attack to redirect aggro of up to 3 nearby enemies onto you
- **Key stats:** Balanced Strength, Defense, HP

### Hollow Armor
- **Role:** Support / Healer
- **Set Bonus (Spirit):** Gain stacks when nearby players hit enemies (requires proximity)
- **Cooldown per stack:** 3s (2 pieces), 2s (3 pieces), 1s (4 pieces)
- **Key stats:** Balanced Strength, Crit Damage, Speed, Intelligence

### Shared Properties (All Sets)
- All sets are Legendary rarity
- Tradeable and auctionable (not salable)
- Reforgeable and enchantable
- 2 Combat gemstone slots + Flawless Ruby, Sapphire, Amethyst, Jasper slots
- Drop rate from Kuudra paid chests: ~4.31% (Basic) to ~2.95% (Infernal)

## The Dojo

Located at coordinates -235, 108, -597 on the Crimson Isle. Overseen by **Master Tao**.

### Requirements
- Empty inventory and no equipped armor to begin challenges
- Pets are disabled during challenges

### Challenges

**Test of Force**
- Knock mobs into lava
- Zombies with iron helmets: 10 points
- Zombies with gold helmets: 20 points
- Baby zombies with diamond helmets: 30 points
- Avoid negative-point mobs

**Test of Stamina**
- Navigate through holes in moving walls
- Avoid getting pushed into lava
- Points earned by remaining on platform
- Difficulty increases with wall color changes

**Test of Mastery**
- Shoot targets before they disappear (2 minute duration)
- Green targets: 8 points
- Yellow targets: 16 points
- Red targets: 32 points
- Tip: Prioritize red targets for maximum points

**Test of Discipline**
- Match sword type to zombie helmet color:
  - Wooden Sword -> Leather Helmet
  - Iron Sword -> Iron Helmet
  - Gold Sword -> Gold Helmet
  - Diamond Sword -> Diamond Helmet
- New sword every 30 seconds

**Test of Swiftness**
- Stand on temporary wool blocks forming a path
- Green blocks: safe | Orange blocks: disappearing soon | Red blocks: about to vanish
- Difficulty increases with jump requirements

**Test of Control**
- Stare directly at a moving skeleton
- Accuracy determines points and time in arena
- Risk of falling into lava

**Test of Tenacity**
- Avoid fireballs shot by ghasts on a circular platform
- Multiple ghasts spawn over time
- Points earned by surviving

### Belt Ranks and Rewards

| Belt | Points Required | Combat XP Reward |
|------|----------------|-----------------|
| Yellow | 1,000 | 80,000 |
| Green | 2,000 | 100,000 |
| Blue | 4,000 | 120,000 |
| Brown | 6,000 | 140,000 |
| Black | 7,000 | 160,000 |

Scoring 1,000+ (S rank) on individual challenges awards SkyBlock XP.

### Restrictions During Challenges
No access to: SkyBlock Menu, Bazaar, Auction House, Storage, Pets, Backpack, Recipe Book, Collections, Skills, Sea Creature Guide, Calendar, Potion Effects, Wardrobe, or Profile Management.

## Vanquishers

Mini-boss wither mobs that spawn randomly while killing mobs on the Crimson Isle.

- **Health:** 5,000,000 HP
- **Damage:** 5,000
- **Level:** 100
- **Base spawn chance:** 1/640 per mob kill

### Spawn Rate Increases
- 640 kills without spawn: 2x rate (1/320)
- 960 kills: 3x rate (1/213)
- 1,280 kills: 4x rate (1/160)
- Kuudra Pet (Legendary, Lv100): 1.2x rate
- Enderman Slayer IX: 1.15x rate
- Wither Piper perk: up to 1.1x rate
- Kuudra Pet's Wither Bait perk: 0.1-20% bonus
- Crimson Serendipity perk: 2-20% bonus

### Vanquisher Drops
- **3 Nether Stars** (100% guaranteed)
- **Cyclamen Dye** (0.0004% - extremely rare)
- 1,000 Combat XP, 50 Experience, 100 Coins

### Notable Mechanics
- Becomes immune to arrow damage below 50% HP (like vanilla Withers)
- Gains significant damage resistance immediately after spawning
- Nether Stars are used for crafting and can be loot-shared

## Progression Tips

1. **Getting started:** Reach Combat 22, enter via Spider's Den launchpad, complete Elle's starter quest
2. **Choose a faction:** Both are viable. Mages have slightly more passive rep sources; Barbarians require Dojo completion early. You can switch factions anytime
3. **Daily reputation grind:** Do 5 faction quests daily, fight Rollim/Kaus 7 times, kill minibosses, and run Kuudra
4. **Kuudra progression:** Start with Basic tier, prestige armor through each tier. You need 10 Stars before each prestige (stars are lost on upgrade)
5. **Armor choice:** Pick based on playstyle - Crimson (melee), Terror (archer), Aurora (mage), Fervor (tank), Hollow (support)
6. **Dojo early:** Complete Dojo for belt rewards and Combat XP. Black Belt requires 7,000 total points across all challenges
7. **Vanquisher farming:** Use Wither Piper perk and Kuudra Pet to increase spawn rates. Nether Stars are the primary reward
8. **Essence investment:** Prioritize Kuudra perks (Strongarm, Fresh Tools, Headstart) before cosmetic/mining perks
9. **Full Infernal armor cost per piece:** 141,005 Crimson Essence + 45 Heavy Pearls + 160 Kuudra Teeth + 7.74M coins. Budget accordingly - full set is 4x this
10. **Attribute shards:** Higher Kuudra tiers drop rarer attribute shards. Infernal has exclusive shards (Taurus, Lord Jawbus, etc.)
