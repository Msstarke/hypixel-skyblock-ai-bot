# Accessories & Magical Power

## What Are Accessories?

Accessories are a category of items that grant buffs while held in a player's inventory or Accessory Bag. They provide passive stat bonuses, special abilities, and Magical Power. Accessories must be in the player's inventory or Accessory Bag to work — items in Ender Chests, Backpacks, Personal Vaults, or Private Island chests do NOT provide any buffs or Magical Power.

## Accessory Lines (Families)

Accessories come in groups called **Accessory Lines** (families). Each line may have multiple tiers, typically following the naming path:
**Talisman → Ring → Artifact → Relic**

- Only the **highest tier** accessory from each line counts for both effects and Magical Power.
- If you have a Feather Talisman, Feather Ring, and Feather Artifact, only the Artifact's effects and MP count.
- Duplicates of the same accessory or lower tiers in the same line are wasted slots.
- Exception: Personal Compactors and Personal Deletors can all work simultaneously with separate configurations, but only one of each grants MP.

The Crux line in The Rift goes beyond Relic: Crux Talisman → Crux Ring → Crux Artifact → Crux Relic → Crux Heirloom → Crux Chronomicon → Celestial Starstone.

## Accessory Bag

The Accessory Bag is a special storage accessible from the SkyBlock Menu that holds accessories. All accessories inside it still grant their effects and Magical Power.

### Unlocking & Upgrading the Accessory Bag

The bag is unlocked at **Redstone Collection II**. It can be expanded through several methods:

| Source | Slots | Details |

| Jacobus NPC | Up to 198 slots | Purchased for coins (total cost: ~1.76B coins for all) |

## Magical Power (MP)

Magical Power is the core stat system for accessories. Each accessory in your Accessory Bag contributes MP based on its rarity. Your total MP determines how strong the stat bonuses from your chosen Power are.

### MP Per Accessory Rarity

| Rarity | Magical Power |
|--------|--------------|
| COMMON | 3 |
| UNCOMMON | 5 |
| RARE | 8 |
| EPIC | 12 |
| LEGENDARY | 16 |
| MYTHIC | 22 |
| SPECIAL | 3 |
| VERY SPECIAL | 5 |

### Special MP Rules
- **Hegemony Artifact**: Grants DOUBLE the Magical Power for its rarity.
- **Rift Prism**: Grants 11 MP when imbued at Erihann in The Rift.
- **Abicases**: Grant +1 extra MP per 2 contacts in the player's Abiphone.
- **Dungeon Accessories**: Give DOUBLE MP when inside Dungeons.
- **Recombobulator 3000**: Upgrading an accessory's rarity by +1 increases its MP contribution (e.g., EPIC→LEGENDARY goes from 12→16 MP).

### Stats Multiplier Formula

The stats from your chosen Power are multiplied by:

```
Stats Multiplier = 29.97 × (ln(0.0019 × MP + 1))^1.2
```

This means MP has **diminishing returns** — early MP is extremely valuable, while later MP gives smaller incremental gains.

## Accessory Powers

Powers replaced the old accessory reforge system. They are applied through **Maxwell** (the Thaumaturgist NPC) and determine what stats your Magical Power translates into.

### Maxwell (Thaumaturgist)
- **Location**: Thaumaturgist area in the Hub (coordinates: -66.5, 70, -66.5)
- **Function**: Change your Power, unlock new powers with Power Stones, manage Stats Tuning
- **Abiphone Contact**: Available (costs 2x Ultimate Carrot Candy)

## Power Stones

Power Stones are special items that can be combined (9 of the same type) at Maxwell to unlock **Stone Powers**. Stone Powers are generally stronger than default/intermediate powers because they also grant **Unique Power Bonuses** — flat stat bonuses on top of the MP-scaled stats.

### How to Unlock Stone Powers
- Collect **9 copies** of the same Power Stone
- Bring them to Maxwell to combine and unlock that power
- Each Stone Power requires a specific **Combat level**

## Stats Tuning (Thaumaturgy)

Stats Tuning is a feature accessed through Maxwell that lets players allocate **Tuning Points** to customize their stats.

### How It Works
- Every **10 Magical Power** grants **1 Tuning Point**
- Tuning Points can be allocated to increase specific stats
- Points can be freely reassigned at any time
- Tuning templates can be saved/loaded (cost 30,000 Bits each to unlock)

### Available Tuning Stats
- Health
- Defense
- Speed
- Strength
- Crit Damage
- Crit Chance
- Attack Speed
- Intelligence

### Tuning Strategy
- Most players put all tuning points into **Speed** (to hit speed cap) or **Crit Damage** / **Strength** depending on what their build needs
- Tank builds may tune into **Health** or **Defense**
- Mage builds tune into **Intelligence**
- Save multiple templates for different setups (farming, dungeons, slayer, etc.)
- At 1000 MP you get 100 tuning points — significant stat boost

### Enrichment Tips
- Only **one** enrichment per accessory
- Buy from Elizabeth (Bits Shop) for 5,000 Bits each
- **Accessory Enrichment Swapper**: Changes ALL enriched accessories to a different enrichment at once — much cheaper than re-enriching individually
- **Pro tip**: Buy the cheapest enriched accessory on AH, then use the Swapper to change it to what you need
- As of 0.20.5, max 51 accessories can be enriched (must be Legendary+)
- Most players enrich for **Speed** (to hit cap), **Magic Find**, or **Attack Speed** depending on build

## Upgrading Accessories

| Method | Effect | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Recombobulator 3000** | +1 rarity tier | One-time per accessory. Increases MP contribution. Applied at Reforge Anvil. |
| **Enrichment** | Adds a small stat bonus | Legendary+ only. One enrichment per accessory. |
| **Gemstone** | Small stat boost | Only certain accessories have Gemstone Slots. Applied at Gemstone Grinder. |

### Recombobulation Priority
Recombobulate accessories in this order for max MP gain:
1. **EPIC → LEGENDARY** (+4 MP, from 12 to 16)
2. **LEGENDARY → MYTHIC** (+6 MP, from 16 to 22)
3. **RARE → EPIC** (+4 MP, from 8 to 12)
4. **UNCOMMON → RARE** (+3 MP, from 5 to 8)
5. **COMMON → UNCOMMON** (+2 MP, from 3 to 5)

Best value: Recombobulate your highest-rarity accessories first for the biggest MP jumps.

## Common Questions

**Q: Do accessories work from the inventory?**
A: Yes, accessories work from both inventory and Accessory Bag. But using the bag frees inventory space.

**Q: Do duplicate accessories stack?**
A: No. Only one accessory per line counts. Having duplicates or lower tiers is wasted.

**Q: Should I reforge accessories?**
A: No — the old reforge system for accessories was replaced by Powers in the April 2022 update. You choose a Power at Maxwell instead.

**Q: What's more important — Power choice or MP amount?**
A: Both matter. Higher MP increases the multiplier on your Power's stats. But choosing the right Power for your build is also critical. Generally focus on getting more MP first, then optimize your Power choice.

**Q: How do I check my MP?**
A: Look in your Accessory Bag — it shows your total Magical Power. You can also see it through Maxwell's interface.

**Q: Is the Accessory Bag per-profile or per-account?**
A: The bag contents are per-profile. Community Center upgrades are account-wide. Redstone collection and Jacobus upgrades are per-profile.

**Q: What Power should I use?**
A: For most players doing combat:
- Early: Warrior or Fortuitous (free)
- Mid: Bloody or Silky (Stone Powers)
- Late: Strong, Hurtful, or Scorching depending on build
- Mage: Sighted or Bizarre
- Tank: Adept, Healthy, or Pleasant

## SkyBlock XP from MP

For each Magical Power the player has, they earn **1 SkyBlock XP**. At max MP (1,963), that's nearly 2,000 free SkyBlock XP.
