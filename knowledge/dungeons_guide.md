## Catacombs Level Requirements

Items have minimum Catacombs (Cata) levels required to equip:
- Cata 0: Can equip any dragon armor, adaptive armor, zombie/skeleton soldier armor
- Cata 12: Shadow Assassin Armor (minimum requirement)
- Cata 14: Goldor, Storm, Maxor armor pieces
- Cata 17: Necron's Armor
- Cata 20+: Aurora Armor
- Cata 24+: Terror Armor
- Cata 28+: Fervor Armor

## Early Game Armor (Cata 0–10, F1–F4)

All items below are usable at Cata 0 and can be bought on AH. Prices vary — use live AH data.

### Best value for budget (buyable on AH, no Cata requirement)

- **Superior Dragon Armor** (LEGENDARY) — Best all-round Cata 0 armor. Set bonus: +5% all stats. No Cata requirement. Buy on AH.
- **Strong Dragon Armor** (LEGENDARY) — Best for Berserk class. +100 Strength per piece. Set bonus: double Strength. No Cata requirement. Buy on AH.
- **Unstable Dragon Armor** (LEGENDARY) — Best for Archer/Berserk. +15% Crit Damage per piece. Set bonus: +50% Crit Chance. No Cata requirement. Buy on AH.
- **Wise Dragon Armor** (LEGENDARY) — Best for Mage class. +100 Intelligence per piece. Set bonus: +4× Mana Regen. No Cata requirement. Buy on AH.
- **Young Dragon Armor** (LEGENDARY) — Best for Speed. Set bonus: +70 Speed. No Cata requirement. Buy on AH.
- **Adaptive Armor** (RARE) — Stats scale with dungeon class. Obtainable as drops from F1+ dungeons OR buyable on AH. No Cata requirement.

### Budget by class at Cata 0 (use live AH prices)

- **Any class**: Superior Dragon (all-round best)
- **Berserk**: Strong Dragon or Unstable Dragon
- **Archer**: Unstable Dragon
- **Mage**: Wise Dragon
- **Healer**: Adaptive Armor or Wise Dragon
- **Tank**: Adaptive Armor or Strong Dragon

## Wither Armor Sets (F6 and F7 drops)

The 4 wither armor sets obtained from Floor 6 and Floor 7 boss fights are:

1. **Necron's Armor** — Drops from Necron boss (F7). +50 Crit Damage per piece. Set bonus: reduce all damage taken. Best Berserk mid-game armor. Requires Cata 17.
2. **Maxor's Armor** — Drops from Maxor boss (F6). All-round stats. Requires Cata 14.
3. **Storm's Armor** — Drops from Storm boss (F6). +Intelligence, +Mana. Best early Mage dungeon armor. Requires Cata 14.
4. **Goldor's Armor** — Drops from Goldor boss (F6). High Defense. Best early tank armor. Requires Cata 14.

**"Wither Armor" is NOT a real set name** — do not use it. The 4 sets are Necron, Maxor, Storm, and Goldor.

All can be bought on AH. Use live AH prices for current cost.

## Dungeon Classes

- **Berserk** — Melee DPS. Best armor: Strong/Unstable/Shadow Assassin/Fervor. Best weapons: Livid Dagger, Shadow Fury, AOTD.
- **Archer** — Ranged DPS. Best armor: Unstable/Shadow Assassin. Best weapons: Juju Shortbow, Terminator.
- **Mage** — Ability DPS. Best armor: Wise/Storm/Aurora. Best weapons: Hyperion, Astraea.
- **Healer** — Support. Best armor: Adaptive, Wise Dragon. Best weapons: Astraea.
- **Tank** — Defense. Best armor: Goldor, Adaptive. High HP accessories.
