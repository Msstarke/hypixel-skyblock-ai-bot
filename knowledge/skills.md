# Skills

## Skill List
Farming, Mining, Combat, Foraging, Fishing, Enchanting, Alchemy, Taming, Carpentry, Runecrafting, Social

## XP to Level (same formula for most skills)
- Level 1–50 uses standard XP table
- Key milestones: Level 25 = ~3.5M XP | Level 50 = ~111M XP | Level 60 (farming cap) = ~275M XP

## Farming
- Cap: Level 60
- Best XP/hr crops: Cactus, Sugar Cane, Pumpkin, Mushroom
- Farming Fortune increases crop yield
- Jacob's Farming Contest: held every ~20 min in-game, gives medals and rewards

## Mining
- Cap: Level 60
- Heart of the Mountain (HotM) tree unlocked after reaching Dwarven Mines
- Powder: Mithril Powder and Gemstone Powder used to upgrade HotM
- Crystal Hollows: special mining zone with gems and crystals

## Combat
- Cap: Level 60
- XP from killing mobs — dungeons give a lot
- Catacombs level is separate from Combat skill

## Fishing
- Cap: Level 50
- Trophy Fish added in Crimson Isle update
- Best spots: Jerry's Workshop (winter), Crimson Isle lava fishing

## Enchanting & Alchemy
- Both cap at Level 60
- Enchanting XP from enchanting items on the table
- Alchemy XP from brewing potions (best: auto-brew setups)

## Skill Average
- Average of all skills (excluding Carpentry & Runecrafting in official calculation)
- Used as a general progression metric
