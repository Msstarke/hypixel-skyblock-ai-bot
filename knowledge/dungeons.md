# Dungeons (Catacombs)

## Classes
- Healer: healing aura, bonus HP for party
- Mage: ability damage boost, mana regen
- Berserk: melee damage boost, speed
- Archer: ranged damage boost
- Tank: damage reduction, draws aggro, more HP

## Floors
- Entrance: tutorial floor, 0 secrets minimum
- Floor 1 (Bonzo): beginner
- Floor 2 (Scarf): requires more secrets
- Floor 3 (The Professor): Guardian fights
- Floor 4 (Thorn): movement mechanics
- Floor 5 (Livid): clone mechanic
- Floor 6 (Sadan): giant fights, jump challenges
- Floor 7 (Necron): endgame, best loot

## Master Mode
- Master Mode Floors 1–7 (MM1–MM7)
- Harder versions, better loot including Crimson armor, Necron's Handle
- Requires Catacombs Level 30+ for MM1, higher for later floors

## Catacombs XP
- Gained by completing floors
- Catacombs Level 50 is soft cap, goes to ~Level 99
- Higher cata level = better dungeon performance stats

## Secrets
- Hidden rooms contain secrets (bats, chests, interact, levers)
- More secrets = higher score = better chest rewards
- 3-star run requires 100% score

## Score & Rewards
- S rank: 270+ score
- S+ rank: 300 score (perfect)
- Better score = more loot in post-boss chest
- Keys: Wood (F1), Iron (F2), Gold (F3), Diamond (F4), Emerald (F5), Obsidian (F6+)

## Notable Drops
- Livid Dagger (F5): fast right-click weapon
- Hyperion / Astraea / Scylla / Valkyrie (F7 Necron): top-tier swords
- Terminator (F7): top-tier bow
- Wither armor pieces (F6/F7): Necron, Maxor, Storm, Goldor
- Starred (⚡) items: dungeon version, soul-bound but stronger
