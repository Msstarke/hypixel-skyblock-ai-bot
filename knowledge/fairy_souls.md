# Fairy Souls

## Overview

Fairy Souls are collectibles scattered around the SkyBlock world that appear as rainbow-colored orbs. Players collect them by right-clicking (left-click also works but has a smaller hitbox). Each soul can only be collected once per profile. A chat message confirms collection or indicates the soul was previously discovered.

There are **267 Fairy Souls** in total across the entire game.

**Note:** The Hub was revamped in v0.24.2 (Feb 2026). Some Hub fairy soul locations may have moved. Use mod waypoints (NEU, Skytils, SkyBlocker) for current positions — they update faster than written guides.

## Exchange Mechanics — Tia the Fairy

Fairy Souls are exchanged in groups of 5 with **Tia the Fairy**, an NPC located in the **Wilderness** area of the Hub at coordinates **X: 119.5, Y: 65, Z: 147.5**.

- Tia only accepts exactly 5 souls at a time — even if you have more, she takes 5 per exchange.
- Each exchange grants **+10 SkyBlock XP**.
- Certain exchanges also unlock **Storage Backpack slots**.
- With 267 total souls, you can make up to **53 exchanges** (with 2 leftover souls).

### Backpack Slot Unlocks

Backpack slots are unlocked at exchange numbers: #1, #2, #4, #6, #8, #10, #13, #16, #19, #22, #25, #28, #31, #34, #37, and #41. That is **16 backpack slots** total from fairy soul exchanges.

## Stat Rewards (Historical)

Fairy Souls **no longer grant permanent stat bonuses**. As of version 0.15, permanent stats (Health, Defense, Strength, Speed) were removed from Fairy Soul exchanges. The rewards were replaced with SkyBlock XP (+10 per exchange) and the backpack slot unlock system.

Previously, each exchange of 5 souls used to grant cumulative boosts to Health, Defense, Strength, and Speed, making fairy souls one of the most important early-game progression steps for combat stats. This is no longer the case.

## Tips for Finding Fairy Souls

### Use Mod Waypoints
The most efficient way to find all fairy souls is using mods that display waypoint markers at every fairy soul location:
- **NEU (NotEnoughUpdates)** — has built-in fairy soul waypoints
- **SkyBlocker** — another mod with fairy soul waypoint support
- **Skytils** — popular mod with fairy soul waypoint feature (mark found/unfound)
- **SBA (SkyBlock Addons)** — older mod, also supports fairy soul waypoints

These mods show colored markers in the world at each soul's location and often let you track which ones you have already collected.

### Movement Tools
It is strongly recommended to use an **Aspect of the End (AOTE)** or **Aspect of the Void** and **Ender Pearls** to reach fairy souls in hard-to-access locations (rooftops, high trees, hidden caves).

### Quest Log Tracking
Players can view a breakdown of how many Fairy Souls they have collected in total and from which islands in their **Quest Log** in the SkyBlock Menu. This helps identify which islands still have uncollected souls.

### General Tips
- Many souls are hidden behind walls, under platforms, or in secret rooms — look everywhere.
- Some souls require parkour or specific movement abilities to reach.
- Dungeon fairy souls only appear in specific dungeon rooms and are not always present in a run.
- Fishing fairy souls come from treasure catches, so they cannot be found by exploring.
- The Crimson Isle has the most fairy souls of any single island (29).

## Importance for Progression

While fairy souls no longer grant combat stats (as of v0.15), they remain important for:
- **SkyBlock XP**: 53 exchanges x 10 XP = 530 total SkyBlock XP, contributing to your SkyBlock level.
- **Storage Backpack Slots**: 16 extra backpack slots are extremely valuable for inventory management. These slots are essential for carrying more items, especially in dungeons and while farming.
- **Completionist Goal**: Collecting all 267 fairy souls is a common milestone for SkyBlock profiles.

## Tia the Fairy — Additional Details

- Tia explains that fairy souls are "the souls of my fallen sisters" scattered across the islands.
- Tia can be added as an **Abiphone contact**, which adds a 9f Operator Chip to the player's Abiphone.
- When wearing **Fairy Armor**, Tia sends relays directing players to 9f Network locations needing completion.
- Lore note: According to the NPC Tal Ker, Tia revealed that the fairies were killed rather than dying peacefully. This is demonstrated in Stillgore Chateau.
