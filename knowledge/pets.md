# Pets

Pets are companions that can be summoned, giving bonus Stats and one or more different perks. They can be accessed and managed in the Pets Menu, which can be accessed through the SkyBlock Menu .
The stats and perks that a Pet gives are dependent on its level and rarity , and every pet has a specific Skill attributed to it. Pets can level up by gaining Skill XP, and they gain the most Skill XP when the player gains XP for its attributed Skill (i.e. a Zombie Pet would gain the most XP from Combat , a Rabbit Pet would gain the most XP from Farming , etc.); the rarity of a Pet can be increased by upgrading them at Kat .
The higher level and rarity a Pet has, the better its stats and perks are, and the more perks it has: commonly, at LEGENDARY , a Pet will have 3 perks, although some will have 4 or 5 perks at max rarity. Most pets belong to one of 8 skills: Combat , Mining , Fishing , Foraging , Farming , Enchanting , Alchemy , and Taming . The Bingo Pet is an All Skills pet, the Wisp Pet is a Gabagool pet, and the Montezuma Pet is a Fractured Soul pet; Gabagool Pets are levelled up by being fed Gabagool .
Pets can be obtained in multiple ways:
- Crafting, through the use of an Egg , Enchanted Egg , or Super Enchanted Egg ;
- Mob drops, from mobs like Ender Dragon s, Scatha s, Yeti s, or Loch Emperor s, which are affected by ✯ Magic Find and ♣ Pet Luck ;
- Purchasing, from places like the Dark Auction or specific merchants;
- Forging, through The Forge ;
- Milestone rewards, such as Mining , Fishing , and the Kuudra boss collection.
All pets that are crafted start at COMMON , and must be upgraded through Kat ; however, some pets only start at certain rarities, like RARE or EPIC . Some pets can be upgraded to MYTHIC at Kat , and will need a special item to upgrade.

## Pets Menu
The Pets Menu can be opened by using the Commands /viewpetsmenu , /petsmenu , /petmenu , /pets (empty by default), or by accessing it from the SkyBlock Menu . Here, players can view and manage their pets, view their Pet Score and Autopet rules, and manage more options.

### Exp Sharing
Exp Sharing is a system that allows players to level up Pets without having them be actively deployed. Players can assign a Pet to an Exp Share slot, upon which the Pet will gain the summoned Pet's XP at a reduced rate. Note that XP gained this way is still subject to the ×0.33 penalty of levelling a different skill than the Pet's.
Players have 1 slot unlocked by default, while the other 2 slots are only functional while Diana is in office with her Sharing is Caring perk active.
The player can increase the percent shared by leveling their Taming level ( 0.2% per Taming Level, up to a maximum of 12% at Taming LX ), by using an Exp Share which gives a 15% boost (with an additional 10% from both Sharing is Caring and Why Not More ), to a total of 37% .
Pets in Exp Share slots do not gain XP if a pet is not summoned; additionally, pets in Exp Share slots that are actively deployed do not gain increased XP.

### Pet Score
Players gain additional Pet Score for every unique pet they collect. Higher rarity pets will grant more Pet Score : additionally, pets at max level will give an additional +1 Pet Score . Reaching thresholds of Pet Score awards additional ✯ Magic Find : Pet Score also rewards 3 SkyBlock XP each.
If the player has multiple of the same Pet in their Pets Menu, only the Pet with the highest level and rarity will contibute to Pet Score .

### Upgrading Rarity
At the Pet Collector's Shop, resides Kat in a balcony attached to it. Kat will take a pet and upgrade its rarity at the cost of coins . The cost varies depending on the pet, its rarity, and the level. There is a discount on the coin cost based on the level of the pet before it is upgraded. The upgraded pet will have its level adjusted based on its XP. For example, a RARE pet with 4.6 million cumulative XP (level 86) will be adjusted to level 81 when it is upgraded to EPIC .
Using a Tier Boost Pet Item will boost the rarity of a pet by 1 (similar to a Recombobulator ) until the Pet Item held by the Pet is changed.
Some pets cannot be rarity upgraded from Kat . For example, the Kuudra Pet , Griffin Pet and Grandma Wolf Pet cannot be upgraded using Kat.
Players can also use a Kat Flower , purchased in the Bits Shop for 500 Bits or Kat Bouquet costing 2,500 Bits , to skip 1 day or 5 days of waiting respectively. Enchanted Clock can be used to skip 1 hour of waiting, with a cooldown of 48 hours .

### Selling
On the first floor of the Pet Collector's Shop, there is another NPC named George . Pets can be sold directly for Coins based on their type and Rarity . However, George doesn't give very good deals, so it's not recommended to sell pets to George because they are heavily underpriced.

## Leveling

### Normal Pets

#### Skill Experience
Summoned pets can be leveled up to level 100. Except for the Golden Dragon Pet , Jade Dragon Pet and Rose Dragon Pet which can go to level 200. The main way non-Gabagool Pets gain experience is from the players gaining Skill experience while they are equipped. The skills Carpentry , Taming , Dungeoneering , Runecrafting and Social do not level pets.
There are numerous ways that affect the pet experience gained by the pet, all of which are multiplicative and work on feeding Gabagool Pets as well.

#### Pet Food
Another way to level pets is by giving them Carrot Candies or Chyme . There are four types of Carrot Candies, each giving different amounts of pet experience: Simple Carrot Candy , Great Carrot Candy , and Superb Carrot Candy unlocked from Carrot Collection 2, 6, and 8 respectively. The Ultimate Carrot Candy can be crafted with 8 Superb Carrot Candies and the Ultimate Carrot Candy Upgrade , the latter bought from Elizabeth 's Bits Shop for 8,000 Bits at the Community Center . Chyme gives 90,000 Pet experience and is obtained from Aranya . Pet Cake gives 10,000 Pet experience and is obtained from Oringo on ⏣ The Garden .

## Pet Items
Pet Items are items that can boost the benefits given by pets. Each pet can hold only one item at a time; once used, the item cannot be retrieved. Pet Items can be given by right-clicking the pet item onto the pet physically while it is active (not in the pets menu).
Zog sells various Pet Items in exchange for coins , most of which can be upgraded with a Zog Anvil : other Pet Items can be obtained from the Mythological Ritual , event rewards, or mob drops.

# Pet Items

Pet Items are items that can be given to Pets to improve them. Each pet can only hold one item at a time. To use them, equip a pet and right-click the pet while holding a pet item.
Pet items are consumed on use. Upon equipping a new item, the old item can be swapped out for the new item for a fee. An existing item can be removed with the Super Scrubber , bought from Plumber Joe .

A Super Scrubber can be used to remove a Pet Item without applying a new one: the cost still applies and the Super Scrubber is consumed in the process.

- All Pet XP boosts work multiplicatively together, such as Taming , Diana , and the Beastmaster Crest .
- If player has multiple of the same Pet Item in their hotbar and they right-click their pet, it will always consume the item in the slot closest to the first, regardless of which slot they used on their pet.
- Crafting a Saddle grants the Saddle Up! Achievement.
- Equipping a Textbook to a pet grants the Knowledge is Power! Achievement.
