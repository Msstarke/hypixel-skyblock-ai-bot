# Pets - Comprehensive Guide

Pets are companions that grant bonus stats and perks. They are managed in the Pets Menu (/pets, /petsmenu). Stats and perks depend on level and rarity. Every pet has a specific skill type. Pets level up from gaining Skill XP (best when matching their attributed skill). Rarity can be upgraded at Kat.

## Quick Answers
- Best overall combat pet: Golden Dragon (endgame), Ender Dragon (mid-late), Tiger (melee DPS)
- Best mining pet: Scatha (gemstone mining), Mithril Golem (mithril/powder), Bal (magma fields), Armadillo (Crystal Hollows traversal)
- Best farming pet: Elephant (general farming fortune), Mooshroom Cow (mushroom drops + farming fortune from strength), Rabbit (farming wisdom + minion speed), Hedgehog (pest farming)
- Best fishing pet: Flying Fish (mythic for lava fishing), Megalodon (shark fishing), Ammonite (Crystal Hollows fishing), Dolphin (sea creature chance)
- Best dungeon pet: Ender Dragon (damage), Golden Dragon (damage), Wither Skeleton (wither mobs), Baby Yeti (tank/EHP), Jellyfish (healer), Spirit (if you die first)
- Best money pet: Golden Dragon (coins from bank), Black Cat (magic find), Scatha (gemstone powder)
- Best pet for magic find: Black Cat (Legendary gives +15 Magic Find + Pet Luck), Golden Dragon (scaling MF)
- Best pet item for damage: Crochet Tiger Plushie (+35 attack speed), Antique Remedies (+80% strength)

## Pet Mechanics

### Obtaining Pets
- **Crafting**: Using Egg, Enchanted Egg, or Super Enchanted Egg (crafted pets start at COMMON)
- **Mob Drops**: Affected by Magic Find and Pet Luck (e.g., Ender Dragon, Scatha, Yeti, Phoenix)
- **Purchasing**: Dark Auction or specific merchants
- **Forging**: Through The Forge
- **Milestones**: Mining, Fishing, and Kuudra boss collection rewards

### Rarity Tiers
COMMON -> UNCOMMON -> RARE -> EPIC -> LEGENDARY -> MYTHIC
- Higher rarity = better stats, more abilities (typically 3 perks at Legendary, some have 4-5)
- Each rarity tier generally unlocks a new ability
- Pet score per rarity: Common=1, Uncommon=2, Rare=3, Epic=4, Legendary=5, Mythic=6

### Active vs Inactive Pets
- Only ONE pet can be active/summoned at a time
- Only the active pet grants stat bonuses and abilities
- Inactive pets can gain XP through Exp Share slots (1 slot default, up to 3 with Diana)
- Autopet rules can auto-switch pets based on conditions

### Exp Sharing
- 1 slot unlocked by default, 2 more during Diana's mayorship (Sharing is Caring perk)
- Base share rate increased by Taming level (+0.2% per level, max +12% at Taming 60)
- Exp Share pet item gives +15% boost (with additional +10% from perks)
- Maximum total: 37% of summoned pet's XP goes to Exp Share pet
- Pets in Exp Share slots do NOT gain XP if no pet is summoned
- XP is still subject to the x0.33 penalty if different skill type

## Pet Leveling

### XP Requirements
Pets level from 1 to 100 (Golden Dragon, Jade Dragon, Rose Dragon go to 200). The XP needed increases per level. Total XP for level 100 varies by rarity:
- COMMON: ~5,624,785 XP
- UNCOMMON: ~8,644,220 XP
- RARE: ~13,017,140 XP
- EPIC: ~19,849,900 XP
- LEGENDARY: ~25,353,230 XP

### XP Sources
1. **Skill XP**: Main source. Pet gains XP when you gain Skill XP while it's equipped
   - 100% rate for matching skill type
   - 33% rate (x0.33) for different skill types
   - 8.33% (x0.08333) for Alchemy and Enchanting (unless pet matches)
   - Fishing and Mining give x1.5 bonus for leveling pets
   - Carpentry, Taming, Dungeoneering, Runecrafting, Social do NOT level pets
2. **Pet Candy** (max 10 candies per pet lifetime, Caducous Feeder bypasses limit):
   - Simple Carrot Candy: 20,000 XP
   - Great Carrot Candy: 200,000 XP
   - Superb Carrot Candy: 750,000 XP
   - Ultimate Carrot Candy: 1,500,000 XP (1,650,000 via Caducous Feeder)
   - Chyme: 90,000 XP (no candy limit)
   - Pet Cake: 10,000 XP (no candy limit)
3. **Pet Training (Fann)**: Pay coins for passive XP over time
   - Free: 20,000-50,000 XP/day (scales with Taming)
   - Light (200k coins): 250,000 XP/day
   - Moderate (1M coins): 500,000 XP/day
   - Expert (2M coins): 750,000 XP/day
   - Ultra (2.75M coins): 1,500,000 XP/day
   - Turbo! (3M coins + 500 Bits): 2,500,000 XP/day
   - Owl Pet in session increases XP by ~10%

### XP Multipliers (all multiplicative)
- Taming level: x1.01 to x1.6
- Diana's Pet XP Buff: x1.35
- Pet Items (skill-specific): x1.1 to x1.5
- Beastmaster Crest: x1.001 to x1.05
- Reindeer Pet: x2 (first ability)
- Fishing/Mining skill: x1.5
- Battle Experience enchant: x1.1 (combat only)

## Upgrading Rarity (Kat)

Kat upgrades pet rarity for coins. Cost varies by pet, rarity, and level. Higher level = discount. The upgraded pet keeps its XP but adjusts level to new rarity curve (e.g., Rare level 86 becomes Epic level 81). Some pets cannot be upgraded at Kat (Kuudra, Griffin, Grandma Wolf). Waiting can be skipped with Kat Flower (1 day, 500 Bits) or Kat Bouquet (5 days, 2,500 Bits).

### Mythic Upgrades
