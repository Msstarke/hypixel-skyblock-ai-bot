# Hypixel SkyBlock Reforges - Comprehensive Reference

> **CRITICAL**: Each reforge can ONLY be applied to its specific item type. NEVER recommend a tool reforge (like Auspicious) on armor, or an armor reforge (like Ancient) on a tool. This is the bot's #1 hallucination.

## How Reforging Works

- Reforging adds bonus stats to Weapons, Armor, Tools, Equipment, and Fishing Rods
- An item can have only ONE reforge at a time (replacing the old one)
- Basic reforges are random from the Blacksmith or Reforge Anvil
- Special reforges require **Reforge Stones** applied via Advanced Reforging (Reforge Anvil, Malik, or Smithmonger anvil)
- Stats scale with item **Rarity**: Common (C) < Uncommon (U) < Rare (R) < Epic (E) < Legendary (L) < Mythic (M) < Divine (D)
- Divine rarity items get NO stat buff from reforges (except reforge abilities like Renowned's 1% boost)
- Accessory reforging was replaced by **Accessory Powers** (via Maxwell) in April 2022
