# Collections

Collections are a progressive mechanic where collecting resources unlocks milestones that reward recipes, items, upgrades, SkyBlock XP, and skill experience. Items obtained from the Bazaar, Merchants, or Trading do NOT count toward collections. Only items gathered through mining, farming, combat drops, fishing, and foraging count.

There are two types: Resource Collections (Farming, Mining, Combat, Foraging, Fishing) and Boss Collections (Dungeon bosses, Kuudra). The Rift also has its own small collection set.

## How Collections Work
- View collections: SkyBlock Menu > Collection
- Each collection has multiple tiers (Roman numerals I, II, III, etc.)
- Each tier requires collecting a cumulative amount of that resource
- Tier rewards include: recipes, minion recipes, accessory bag upgrades, sack recipes, enchant discounts, skill XP, and more
- At 25,000 of a resource collected, you can see your global ranking
- Each tier also grants SkyBlock Level XP (+4 for resource collections, +10 to +30 for boss collections)

# Best Ways to Grind Collections Quickly

## Farming Collections
- **Wheat/Potato/Carrot/Nether Wart**: Build large farms on your island or in the Garden. Use Rancher's Boots + farming tools with Turbo enchants. Garden plots give the most efficient farming.
- **Pumpkin/Melon**: Semi-auto farms with water. Pumpkin Dicer/Melon Dicer from Garden are fastest.
- **Sugar Cane**: Sugar Cane Hoe with Turbo-Cane enchants in Garden.
- **Cocoa Beans**: Cocoa farms in Garden with Cocoa Chopper.
- **Mushroom**: Mushroom Desert or island farm.
- **Cactus**: Auto cactus farm on private island.

## Mining Collections
- **Cobblestone**: Mine cobble generators on island or use minions. Very easy to get to high tiers.
- **Redstone**: Mine in Deep Caverns (Pigman's Den) or Redstone veins. Minions help a lot for passive gain. Use Redstone Minion with Super Compactor for fastest passive collection.
- **Diamond**: Diamond mines in Deep Caverns. Diamond Minions help passively.
- **Emerald**: Mine in Deep Caverns or use Emerald Minions. Villager trading does NOT count.
- **Coal**: Coal Mine area, coal minions.
- **Iron/Gold**: Deep Caverns, respective minions.
- **Lapis**: Lapis Mine in Deep Caverns, lapis minions.
- **End Stone**: The End island. Enderman grinding also gets ender pearls.
- **Obsidian**: Obsidian Sanctuary in Deep Caverns.
- **Mithril/Hard Stone**: Dwarven Mines. Mining mithril with efficient setup.
- **Glacite/Tungsten/Umber**: Glacite Tunnels mining.
- **Gemstone**: Crystal Hollows gemstone mining with high mining speed.

## Combat Collections
- **Rotten Flesh**: Kill zombies anywhere. Crypt Ghouls in hub graveyard are efficient.
- **Bone**: Kill skeletons. Skeleton grinder spots or minions.
- **String**: Kill spiders in Spider's Den. Spider's Den cave grinding.
- **Ender Pearl**: Kill Endermen in The End. Zealot/Enderman grinding.
- **Gunpowder**: Kill creepers. Gunpowder Minion helps.
- **Blaze Rod**: Kill blazes in Blazing Fortress.
- **Ghast Tear**: Kill ghasts in Blazing Fortress.
- **Magma Cream**: Kill Magma Cubes in Blazing Fortress.
- **Spider Eye**: Kill cave spiders or spiders.
- **Slimeball**: Kill slimes in Deep Caverns or Spider's Den.
- **Chili Pepper**: Kill mobs on Crimson Isle (Crimson Isle exclusive).

## Foraging Collections
- **Any wood type**: Use Treecapitator (Jungle Log VII) in the respective Park area. Monkey Pet or Ocelot Pet boosts foraging.
- The Park has all wood types in different areas.

## Fishing Collections
- **Raw Fish/Salmon**: Fish at the Hub pond, Mushroom Desert, or any fishing spot.
- **Pufferfish/Clownfish**: Jerry Workshop (winter) or specific fishing spots.
- **Sponge**: Kill sea creatures (fishing). Deep Sea Orb fishing.
- **Prismarine**: Kill sea creatures or Guardian fishing.
- **Magmafish**: Lava fishing on Crimson Isle with Magma Rod or better.

## Boss Collections
- **Dungeon bosses**: Run the corresponding dungeon floor repeatedly. Higher floors give more drops.
- **Kuudra**: Run Kuudra fights on Crimson Isle. Higher tiers give more collection credit.
