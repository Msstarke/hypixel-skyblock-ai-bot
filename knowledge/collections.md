# Collections

Collections are a progressive mechanic where the player collects a certain resource in order to unlock milestones which reward the player with recipes , item trades , SkyBlock XP and items.
There are two different types of Collections , spilt into sub-sections: resource collections, that are spilt into Farming , Mining , Combat , Foraging and Fishing and boss collections that are spilt into each of the Dungeon 6 BOSS mobs and Kuudra .
The player can view their Collections through SkyBlock Menu → Collection . Once the player has collected 25,000 of a resource, they can see their global ranking for that item. Obtaining items from the Bazaar , Merchants or Trading do not count towards Collections .
