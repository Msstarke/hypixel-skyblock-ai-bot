# General Skyblock Info

## Currency & Economy
- Coins are the main currency
- Bits are earned from community shop / booster cookies, used at the Community Shop NPC
- Gems are premium currency (real money)
- Bank interest: 2% daily up to 5M, 1% daily up to 20M, 0.5% above 20M

## NPC Sell Prices (fixed, won't change)
- Enchanted Cobblestone: 160 coins
- Enchanted Diamond: 2,048 coins
- Enchanted Gold: 384 coins
- Enchanted Iron: 192 coins
- Enchanted Lapis: 160 coins
- Enchanted Redstone: 160 coins
- Enchanted Emerald: 427 coins
- Enchanted Obsidian: 512 coins
- Enchanted Quartz: 120 coins
- Enchanted Coal: 96 coins

## Enchanted Item Crafting Ratios
- Most enchanted items require 160x the base material (e.g. 160 diamonds → 1 enchanted diamond)
- Exception: some items use different ratios — check collection or crafting table
- Enchanted stacks: 160 base → 1 enchanted, then 160 enchanted → 1 enchanted² (e.g. Enchanted Diamond Block)

## Collections
- Collections are unlocked by gathering items and unlock recipes, minions, and perks
- Collection tiers: I through XII (varies per item)

## Profiles
- Up to 4 profiles per account (Co-op counts as one)
- Ironman profiles: cannot use Bazaar or player trading

## Backpack & Storage
- Personal Bank: stores coins, accessible from your island
- Ender Chest: 9 pages expandable with Ender Chest upgrades
- Backpacks: craftable, come in Small/Medium/Large/Jumbo sizes
