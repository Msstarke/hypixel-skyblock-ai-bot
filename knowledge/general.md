# Fairy Souls

Fairy Souls are collectibles scattered around the SkyBlock world. Collecting them grants SkyBlock XP and Storage Backpack slots once exchanged at Tia .

## Mechanics
Fairy Souls are collectibles that take the form of rainbow-coloured orbs scattered and hidden around parts of the map. There are 267 Fairy Souls in total.
Players can interact with Fairy Souls by right-clicking them. (Left-click may also work, but the right-click hitbox seems to be bigger.) Each soul is unique and is able to be collected only once. A message in the chat is displayed to the player each time they interact with a soul informing them they have collected the soul or previously discovered it.

### Exchange
Fairy Souls may be exchanged in groups of 5 with Tia the Fairy , an NPC in the ⏣ Wilderness . For each exchange, the player will receive 10 SkyBlock XP and will unlock Backpack slots at exchange #1, #2, #4, #6, #8, #10, #13, #16, #19, #22, #25, #28, #31, #34, #37 and #41.

## Quest Log
Players can view a breakdown of how many Fairy Souls they have in total and from which islands in their Quest Log in the SkyBlock Menu .

### Tips
- It is recommended to use a mod that highlights the fairy soul, to ease accessing it. The Not Enough Updates (NEU) mod (1.8.9) or SkyBlocker (1.21) have waypoints for Fairy Soul locations. NEU waypoints can be enabled using /neusouls on and SkyBlocker waypoints can be enabled by going to the config ( /skyblocker config ) -> Helpers -> Fairy Souls Helper. Alternatively, the player could set up waypoints for each soul individually using a mod such as SkyTils.
- Even though some Fairy Souls are stated to be walkable, it is still strongly recommended to use an Aspect of the End or Void and Ender Pearls . Early-game players may choose to opt for the Grappling Hook , a cheaper alternative to an AOTE.

## Miscellaneous Souls
There are several Fairy Souls that cannot be obtained through normal means.

### Fishing
Three Fairy Souls are obtained from Fishing . The 2nd, 9th, and 24th treasure catches fished up by the player are guaranteed to be Fairy Souls. Two additional Fairy Souls have a 0.5% chance to be fished up in a ⏣ Fairy Grotto .

### Garden
The first order accepted from Tia the Fairy when she visits ⏣ The Garden grants a single Fairy Soul.
Likewise, the first order accepted from Bruuh when he visits ⏣ The Garden grants a Fairy Soul.

### Dungeons
Up to 9 Fairy Souls can be found in various Dungeon rooms.

### Glacite Mineshafts
A Fairy Soul can be found near the Vanguard Corpse in Fairy Mineshafts.

## Boss Fight
Kuudra's boss fight consists of multiple phases, depending on the selected tier.

### Phase 1: Crates
Phase One involves using a Lava Rod to fish up Supply Crates in the lava surrounding the main platform and bringing them to the center of the arena. Players can either use a Starter Lava Rod , Topaz Rod , Magma Rod , Inferno Rod , or Hellfire Rod to fish up the crates; those lacking a Lava Rod can purchase an Elle's Lava Rod from the Perk Shop.
Once the supply crate reaches the platform, players must stand still until the meter reaches 100% to pick up the crate. To reduce the time taken to fish and pick up supply crates, one can upgrade the Steady Hands perk unlocked from the Specialist route or have a Kuudra's Kidney , Kuudra's Lung , or Kuudra's Heart in their inventory or Accessory Bag .
Taking too long to place the supply crate or taking too much damage will cause the crate to fall back into the lava.

### Phase 2: Ballista
Phase Two involves building the Ballista by using the supplies obtained during Phase One. By sneaking and left-clicking the piles of supplies on the ground, players make progress towards completing the construction of the Ballista, which occurs once the meter reaches 100% . The Ballista will be the primary weapon that players use against Kuudra.
To reduce the time taken to build the Ballista, one can upgrade the Ballista Mechanic perk unlocked from the Specialist route or have a Kuudra's Kidney , Kuudra's Lung , or Kuudra's Heart in their inventory or Accessory Bag .
During this phase, mobs will attempt to attack the Ballista, bringing the meter back down to 0% .

### Phase 3: Fuel
Phase Three involves fishing up Fuel Cells to fuel the Ballista constructed during Phase Two. They can be fished up with a Lava Rod, similar to supply crates, and are used with the Ballista to attack Kuudra. Once all 4 Fuel Cells have been secured, players can mount the Ballista and fire at Kuudra.
To reduce the time taken to fish up Fuel Cells, one can upgrade the Steady Hands perk unlocked from the Specialist route.
During Burning Tier Kuudra and above, Kuudra must be stunned before the Ballista can be fired: otherwise, very little damage will be dealt and Kuudra will submerge.

### Phase 4: Stomach
Kuudra's stomach
Phase Four is exclusive to Burning Tier and higher, and involves players firing themselves into Kuudra's stomach. In order to enter Kuudra's stomach, players can either:
- Purchase a Human Cannonball , allowing you to fire yourself into Kuudra's mouth;
- Stand in the Consumption Zone , allowing yourself to be consumed by Kuudra's tentacles.
Several Orbs can be found in Kuudra's stomach, which must be broken to stun Kuudra. Breaking the orbs with a Pickaxe will reduce their health by 5% ; players who do not have a Pickaxe can purchase a Elle's Pickaxe in the Kuudra Perk Shop.
During this time, several Magma Bacteria will spawn, who fire projectiles at the player that deal knockback. When Kuudra submerges, the stomach will fill up with lava and all players within will be launched out.

### Phase 5: Lair
Kuudra's true lair
Phase Five is exclusive to the Infernal Tier that begins once Kuudra's health reaches 25% . It involves players dodging Kuudra's tentacles and firing at Kuudra's head until its health reaches 0 .
The main arena of ⏣ Kuudra's Hollow collapses to reveal a lower section surrounded by lava, with Wandering Blaze s surrounding the top of the arena, killing any players above them.
During this phase, all deaths are considered Final Kills : however, players can revive if their party has a member alive who has the Support Route selected, who can revive players using Tokens .

## Loot
Each tier of Kuudra has its own loot pool that its Reward Chests roll from. There will be a free chest, which rewards Crimson Essence , and a paid chest: depending on the tier selected, you will need its respective Kuudra Key (e.g. an Infernal Kuudra Key is required to unlock the paid chest in Kuudra Infernal Tier).
You are guaranteed to obtain Crimson Essence , Kuudra Teeth , an Attribute Shard , or an Enchanted Book , as well as an extra piece of gear. Having the Kuudra Teeth Plaque maxed out at SPECIAL grants a small chance to obtain a Kuudra Mandible .

# Magic Find

✯ Magic Find ( ✯ MF ) is one of the core player statistics. It is a Stat that affects the drop chance of obtaining rare drops from monsters and bosses.

## Mechanics
✯ Magic Find is the bonus chance to find rare drops from enemies.
✯ Magic Find increases the drop chance of rare items using this formula: C h a n c e = B a s e C h a n c e ∗ ( 1 + ( M a g i c F i n d 100 ) ) {\displaystyle Chance = BaseChance * (1 + (\frac{MagicFind}{100}))} If the drop is a Pet , this formula is used: C h a n c e = B a s e C h a n c e ∗ ( 1 + ( P e t L u c k + M a g i c F i n d 100 ) ) {\displaystyle Chance = BaseChance * (1 + (\frac{PetLuck + MagicFind}{100}))}
When a rare item is dropped, the amount of ✯ Magic Find the player currently has is displayed at the end of the drop message. This does not increase ✯ Magic Find .
✯ Magic Find does not affect drops from most Mobs with a higher drop chance than 5%. Consequently, having high ✯ Magic Find can decrease the drop chance of 3 Occasional drops from Slayer bosses.

## Increasing Base Magic Find
Players have 0 base ✯ Magic Find . The base ✯ Magic Find can be increased by collecting unique pets.
