# Community Corrections

## Catacombs Gear Requirements
Can't use Livid Dagger, Shadow Fury, Adaptive full set, or Bonzo Staff at Cata 0. These require Cata level 1+.

## Snow Minion Income
T11 Snow Minion with Diamond Spreading makes approximately 1.2M coins/day.

## Magical Power Guidelines
For Floor 7 (F7) dungeons, you want at least 400-500+ Magical Power. For Master Mode, aim for 700+. Get MP from accessories in your Accessory Bag — higher rarity accessories give more MP.

## Mining Fortune Sources
Main sources of Mining Fortune (in rough order of impact):
1. HotM perks — Mining Fortune (max level 50, gives +2 fortune per level = 100 total) and Fortunate Mineman (renamed from Mining Fortune II, costs Gemstone Powder)
2. Divan's Armor — full set gives massive base fortune, slot Perfect Jade gemstones in every slot for even more
3. Auspicious reforge on pickaxe/drill (NOT Spelunker — Spelunker is not a valid tool reforge)
4. Fortune IV enchantment on your mining tool
5. Mining pet — Mithril Golem for mithril, Scatha for gemstones
6. Mining skill level — each level gives +4 Mining Fortune
Note: Mineral Armor gives Block Fortune (different from Mining Fortune). Mineral Armor is for breaking blocks, not for ore mining fortune.

## Mineral Armor Details
Mineral Armor gives BLOCK Fortune, NOT Mining Fortune. Do NOT recommend it as a mining fortune upgrade.
Do NOT invent specific stat numbers for Mineral Armor upgrades (like "+25 Mining Fortune per jade gem"). If you don't know the exact stat, don't make one up.
Do NOT recommend "Auspicious reforge" on Mineral Armor — Auspicious is a TOOL reforge, not an armor reforge.

## Do NOT Invent Specific Numbers
NEVER make up specific stat values like "+25 Mining Fortune", "+10 Mining Fortune per gem", "+4 Health per book" etc. unless those exact numbers appear in the data provided to you. If you don't have the exact number, say "check the wiki for exact values" or give a general description without specific numbers.

## HotM Menu Access
The HotM tree is accessed via the /hotm command, or through the Mining menu in Dwarven Mines/Crystal Hollows. There is NO "HotMines menu" — that term does not exist. Always say "/hotm" or "Heart of the Mountain menu".

## Armor Reforges vs Tool Reforges
IMPORTANT: Armor reforges and tool reforges are DIFFERENT. Do NOT recommend tool reforges on armor or vice versa.
- Auspicious is a TOOL reforge (pickaxes/drills) — gives Mining Fortune. Do NOT recommend Auspicious on armor.
- Armor reforges for mining: Loving (from accessories), Reinforced, etc. Do NOT invent reforges — if unsure, say "check the wiki for current best armor reforges."

## Mining Tool Reforges
The ONLY valid reforges for pickaxes/drills in Skyblock are:
- Auspicious: gives Mining Fortune (NOT Mining Speed — it gives FORTUNE)
- Fortunate: gives Mining Fortune
- Other valid reforges exist but Auspicious is best for fortune
INVALID reforges (do NOT exist): "Spelunker", "Very Lucky", "Powerful" (for mining tools)

## Mining Tool Enchantments
Valid mining enchantments:
- Fortune IV (NOT Fortune III — Skyblock goes to IV, gives Mining Fortune)
- Efficiency V (gives Mining Speed)
- Smelting Touch (auto-smelts ores)
- Compact (auto-compresses mined items)
- Pristine V (chance for Flawless gemstones)
- Telekinesis (auto-collects drops — note: this was removed, items auto-collect now)
INVALID enchants for mining tools: "Sharpness", "Smite", "Spelunker IX" — combat enchants do NOT go on pickaxes/drills

## Pickobulus Details
Pickobulus is a Pickaxe Ability (HotM Tier 2). It throws your pickaxe, creating an explosion that mines all ores within a 3-block radius. It does NOT give a speed boost. Cooldown: 40-60s depending on level.
