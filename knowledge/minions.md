# Minions

## Basics
- Minions are placed on your private island and generate resources over time
- Max 25 minion slots by default (expandable via upgrades and achievements)
- Each unique minion type + tier unlocks higher slots count
- Minions must be within 32 blocks of your island center to work when you're offline

## Tiers
- Tiers I–XII (not all minions have all tiers)
- Higher tier = faster speed + more storage capacity
- Upgrading requires the previous tier + materials

## Fuels (speed boosts, placed in fuel slot)
- Enchanted Lava Bucket: +25% speed, unlimited uses
- Plasma Bucket: +35% speed, unlimited uses
- Foul Flesh: +25% speed, unlimited uses (from Mythological event)
- Hamster Wheel: +50% speed, 150k actions
- Bejeweled Handle: +80% speed, 200k actions
- Kat Flower: +25% speed, unlimited

## Storage Upgrades
- Small Storage: +3 slots
- Medium Storage: +9 slots
- Large Storage: +15 slots
- X-Large Storage: +21 slots
- XX-Large Storage: +27 slots

## Output Upgrades
- Super Compactor 3000: auto-compacts items into enchanted form (very important for profit)
- Compactor: basic version, compacts into regular blocks
- Expander: increases storage by 3 slots

## Coins Per Day Factors
- Minion speed (base + fuel)
- Storage size (longer AFK time before full)
- What the minion produces + current Bazaar sell price
- Super Compactor multiplies coin/hr significantly for most resources

## Best Profit Minions (approximate, changes with market)
- Snow Minion (with Super Compactor): consistent, low-cost setup
- Lapis Minion: reliable mid-game coins
- Magma Cube Minion: good with Super Compactor
- Wheat/Cocoa Bean Minion: good with farming fortune setups
