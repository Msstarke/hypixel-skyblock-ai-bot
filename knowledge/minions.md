# Minions

Minions are NPCs that produce items while placed on a player's private island. Each minion will generate and harvest resources within a 5x5 Area . The resource generated varies by minion type. Minions can be upgraded in order to increase their speed and carrying capacity, to a maximum craftable tier of XI (11) and XII (12) obtainable from the Bartender , Terry's Shop , and Bulvar . Players start out with a Tier I Cobblestone Minion on their ⏣ Private Island .
Minions cannot be traded on the ⏣ Auction House , but they can be traded through player to player Trading .
Minions with a description that includes "Time Between Actions: Xs" will generate collectable resources every other action. For example, if a Tier I Cobblestone Minion does an action every 14 seconds, the minion will generate 1 Cobblestone every 28 seconds and not 14 seconds.
Minions can be crafted into higher tiers to increases their collection speed and maximum storage size, with tier XI being the highest tier for most minions, over time however, some minions received 12th tier. Minion tiers can be distinguished by the colored patch on their heads, with light green patches at tiers II-IV (2-4), purple patches at tiers V-VII (5-7), orange patches at tiers VII-X (7-10), and cyan patches at tier XI (11). There is a quick crafting diamond for quick tier upgrades.
Initially, only five minions can be placed. Extra minion slots can be gained from crafting unique minions and minion tiers, along with Profile Upgrades . See increasing minion capacity for more information.
All players in a Co-op can interact with all Minions on their island.
Crafting new unique Minions rewards SkyBlock XP .

### Quick Answers

## Minion Interface
Minions have four types of slots:
- Minion Skins (Green, x1 ) - These are purely cosmetic.
- Fuel (Orange, x1 ) - Increases minion production speed. Most will only have limited usage and will destroy itself when used up. The exceptions are the Enchanted Lava Bucket , the Magma Bucket , the Plasma Bucket , the Everburning Flame , and the Solar Panel . All 5 power up the minion indefinitely, but the Solar Panel works only in daylight.
- Automated Shipping (Blue, x1 ) - Minions will automatically sell generated items once its inventory and Minion Storage Chest (if used) are full. This can be utilized for low-effort minion setups which rely on high NPC sell-value or minion setups aimed at long times away from the game.
- Minion Upgrades (Yellow, x2 ) - Unlike the other slots, both upgrade slots have a variety of different functions they can supply, although some upgrades are exclusive to certain minions.

These items modifies the speed, capacity, or appearance of a minion.

### Cosmetic Modifiers
- Minion Chair - Counts as a furniture item. Sitting minions do not work or count to the minion total. A minion skin cannot be applied to a sitting minion.
- Minion Skins - Cosmetic overrides to a specific minion's appearance. They do not affect performance. See more in Minion Skins .

### Minion Speed Calculation
Minion speed boosts are additive. The Minion's Time Between Actions is decreased according to the following formula:
Time Between Actions = Base Time Between Actions 1 + Combined Speed Boost Percentage {\displaystyle \text{Time Between Actions}=\frac{\text{Base Time Between Actions}}{1 + \text{Combined Speed Boost Percentage}}}

#### Example Calculation
For a Tier XI Clay Minion with Enchanted Coal, the Base Time Between Actions is 16 seconds, and the speed increase from the Enchanted Coal is +10% , so the new Time Between Actions would be as follows:
Time Between Actions = 16 seconds 100 % + 10 % ≈ 14.55 seconds {\displaystyle \text{Time Between Actions}=\frac{16\,\text{seconds}}{100\%+10\%}\approx 14.55\,\text{seconds}}

## Crafted Minions
Crafted Minions are minions that the player has crafted before, has gained SkyBlock XP from it and unique minion crafts from it. With enough minions crafted, the player's minion slots can increase. It can be opened through Recipe Book in Skyblock Menu, or via command /craftedgenerators .

## Mob Spawning Mechanics
A two-dimensional representation of where a mob can spawn around a minion (top block height is based on the height of mob).
The mechanics of mob spawning minions are similar to that of mob spawners in vanilla Minecraft :
- The "ideal layout" shows the general spawning area for mobs around a minion.
- Minions can spawn mobs only within a 5x5 area centered around them, unless they have a Minion Expander upgrade.
- The vertical range in which they spawn is 6 blocks (3 below, 2 above, and 1 at eye level with the minion).
- As long as there is a block for them to spawn on and spawning will not cause them to take suffocation damage, they will spawn in accordance with the minion's normal spawning times.
- Mobs that are wider than 1 block (slimes, magma cubes, ghasts, etc.) are limited by the horizontal range, but they will still spawn partially inside nearby walls and may possibly escape.
- If a mob is outside of a minion's range, it will not kill it automatically and collect the loot.

## Tips
- A minion with a Super Compactor 3000 or Dwarven Super Compactor will turn items into their enchanted form, when there are enough in its inventory, even if the player has not unlocked the enchanted recipe of the item yet.
- The player's island is loaded when the player, guests, or co-op teammates enter the island. Approximately one minute after all players have left the island, the island is unloaded. By loading the island, some minions can be set up the perform the same action endlessly depending on their surroundings. For example, a Cobblestone Minion next to a cobblestone generator will only break cobblestone and will not place any new cobblestone if all other blocks are not cobblestone or air. Similarly, players can make mob minions spawn mobs so they die to fall damage and set up Hopper system to collect the resources. This will make the minion twice as efficient since they don't have to kill the spawned mob. But it won't grant Skill XP or collection.

## Calculations
Fuels increase the speed of the Minion by X%, which means that the Minion's Time Between Actions is decreased according to the following formula:
Time Between Actions = Base Time Between Actions 100 % + Speed Increase {\displaystyle \text{Time Between Actions}=\frac{\text{Base Time Between Actions}}{100\%+\text{Speed Increase}}}

### Example Calculation
For a Tier XI Clay Minion with Enchanted Coal, the Base Time Between Actions is 16 seconds, and the speed increase from the Enchanted Coal is +10%, so the new Time Between Actions would be as follows:
Time Between Actions = 16 seconds 100 % + 10 % ≈ 14.55 seconds {\displaystyle \text{Time Between Actions}=\frac{16\,\text{seconds}}{100\%+10\%}\approx 14.55\,\text{seconds}}

## Notes
- Once a Minion fuel is placed inside a Minion, it cannot be reclaimed unless it has an infinite duration.
- If the player picks up a Minion that has fuel in it, the fuel will be destroyed unless it is an infinite fuel.
- Minion fuel can be stacked, allowing the player to extend how long it will last.
- The fuels that have a multiplier (x2, x3, x4) instead of a percentage don't affect the Minion's Time Between Actions. Instead, they duplicate each item that the Minion produces.
- In version 0.7 , when the Catalyst was added, the duration was changed from using in-game time to using real time. The actual amount of time didn't change.
- Fuel works even when the player is not online, with the exception of the Solar Panel , which only works with a player on the island.
- The Solar Panel only works during the day (6AM to 7PM SkyBlock Time), but it can be used in combination with a Day Saver to have it always work. However, the Solar Panel will still only work when the player's island is loaded.
- Excluding infinite fuels, Enchanted Bread is the cheapest fuel by the hour, making it a good choice for new profiles.
- Minion Fuels are additive with other Minion speed upgrades. For example, combining Enchanted Coal (+10%), a Minion Expander (+5%), and a Floating Crystal (+10%) results in a total boost of +25%.
- The maximum speed boost for a minion is 175%. It can be archieved by using Foul Flesh (90%), 2 Flycatcher (both together 40%), Mithril Infusion (10%) and a floating Mithril Crystal (10%) on a mining minion while Coles Prospection perk (25%) is active

# Super Compactor 3000

Super Compactor 3000 is a RARE Minion upgrade unlocked at Cobblestone X . The upgrade is similar to the Compactor , however instead of compressing items into blocks, they will turn into their enchanted form if enough materials are available.

## Usage
Super Compactor 3000 compacts all the items in the minion's inventory once the minion makes produce. This means that Super Compactor 3000 doesn't compact if it is given to a minion with full inventory.
Although not mentioned in their item description, it can also compact enchanted items into their further compacted forms (such as Enchanted Pork to Enchanted Grilled Pork ).
Players are not required to have recipe of the enchanted item or enchanted block form of the item unlocked for their minion equipped with the Super Compactor to produce one.

## Trivia
- Players used to be able to purchase Cobblestone from the Mine Merchant for 215,040 coins , but this is now really inefficient due to the addition of Bazaar and 640 of each unique item from NPCs / day limitation.
- Upgrading a minion with Super Compactor 3000 grants "Time to go on vacation." Achievement , reference to its ability to greatly "increase" the storage capacity of a minion and thus how long it can work for before being full.
- Super Compactor 3000 can compact items that have been turned into their block form, which means it can be used with a Compactor , although it's not recommended.
- The Dwarven Super Compactor has the exact same function as using both a Super Compactor 3000 and an Auto Smelter , with the added benefit that it uses only one slot instead of both. However,  it is more expensive, costing an extra 56x Enchanted Mithril (or 8,960x Mithril ) to craft.
