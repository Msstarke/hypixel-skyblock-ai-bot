# Gemstones - Hypixel Skyblock

## Overview

Gemstones are twelve types of Mining resources in Hypixel Skyblock. They can be applied to items with Gemstone Slots to boost specific stats. Applied gemstones can be removed for a coin fee, returning the gemstone to the player. Gemstones are also used as crafting ingredients and in Forge recipes.

## Gemstone Qualities (Tiers)

Gemstones come in 5 tiers, from lowest to highest:

| Tier | Color | Obtaining | Crafting | Collection Req | Rough Equivalent |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|----------------|------------------|
| Rough | White | Mining, Treasure Chests | N/A | N/A | 1 |
| Flawed | Green | Pristine proc, Treasure Chests, Crystal Nucleus | 80 Rough (5x16 cross pattern) | Gemstone II | 80 |
| Fine | Blue | Treasure Chests, Crystal Nucleus | 80 Flawed (5x16 cross pattern) | Gemstone V | 6,400 |
| Flawless | Purple | Treasure Chests, Crystal Nucleus | 80 Fine (5x16 cross pattern) | Gemstone IX | 512,000 |
| Perfect | Orange | Forged (5 Flawless + Gemstone Crystal, 20h) | The Forge only | Gemstone X + HotM 5 | 2,560,000 |

The crafting recipe for each tier upgrade uses a cross/plus pattern: 16 in each cardinal direction + 16 in center = 80 total of the previous tier.

## Applying and Removing Gemstones

### Applying
Gemstones are applied at the Gemstone Grinder located near the NPC Geo. Some slots must be unlocked first by paying coins and providing a gemstone. The application itself is free.

### Removal Costs

| Gemstone Tier | Removal Cost |
|---------------|-------------|
| Rough | 1 coin |
| Flawed | 100 coins |
| Fine | 10,000 coins |
| Flawless | 100,000 coins |
| Perfect | 500,000 coins |

### Slot Display Format
- Locked slot: Dark gray icon and brackets
- Unlocked empty slot: Gray icon, dark gray brackets
- Filled slot: Icon color = gemstone type, bracket color = gemstone tier (White=Rough, Green=Flawed, Blue=Fine, Dark Purple=Flawless, Gold=Perfect)

## Key Items with Gemstone Slots

### Armor of Divan (Legendary, Mining Set)

Each piece has 5 gemstone slots (unlocked via Gemstone Chambers applied in an Anvil):
- 2x Jade slots
- 2x Amber slots
- 1x Topaz slot

This is the best mining armor set. Unlock priority: Topaz slot first, then Jade slots, then Amber slots.

### Divan's Drill

Has 5 gemstone slots:
- 2x Amber slots
- The 5th slot is a Mining slot (accepts Topaz, Jade, or Amber)

### Other Notable Items with Gemstone Slots

- **Sorrow Armor:** Has Universal gemstone slots, allowing any gemstone type including Topaz
- **Yog Armor:** Has Universal gemstone slots
- **Mastiff Armor:** 5 gemstone slots (tied with Divan's Armor)
- **Gemstone Gauntlet:** 5 gemstone slots (mining tool)
- **Necron's Armor:** Combat-focused, has Combat gemstone slots (accepts Ruby, Amethyst, Sapphire, Jasper, Onyx, Opal)
- **Storm's Armor:** Mage-focused, has Combat gemstone slots
- **Goldor's Armor:** Tank-focused, has Defensive gemstone slots
- **Various dungeon weapons:** Livid Dagger, Shadow Fury, Giant's Sword, Hyperion, etc. have Combat slots
- **Drills:** Titanium drills have Mining slots; higher-tier drills have more slots
- **Talisman/Ring/Artifact of Power:** Has gemstone slots but stat bonuses are halved

## Best Gemstones for Different Builds

### Mining Build
- **Jade** (Mining Fortune): Most important for increasing ore drops. Use Perfect Jade on Legendary+ Divan's Armor for +40 Mining Fortune per slot.
- **Amber** (Mining Speed): Increases mining speed. Perfect Amber on Legendary items gives +80 Mining Speed per slot.
- **Topaz** (Pristine): Gives a chance to upgrade Rough gem drops to Flawed while mining. Valuable for gemstone mining profit.
- Optimal Divan's Armor setup: Perfect Topaz in Topaz slot, Perfect Jade in Jade slots, Perfect Amber in Amber slots.

### Combat Build (Berserker/Melee)
- **Jasper** (Strength): For raw damage. Perfect Jasper on Mythic gives +16 Strength per slot.
- **Onyx** (Critical Damage): For crit builds. Perfect Onyx on Mythic gives +12 Crit Damage per slot.
- **Ruby** (Health): For survivability. Perfect Ruby on Mythic gives +30 Health per slot.
- Use in Combat slots on Necron's Armor, weapons like Hyperion, Shadow Fury, etc.

### Tank Build
- **Amethyst** (Defense): Perfect Amethyst on Mythic gives +30 Defense per slot.
- **Ruby** (Health): Perfect Ruby on Mythic gives +30 Health per slot.
- **Opal** (True Defense): Perfect Opal on Mythic gives +13 True Defense per slot.
- Use in Defensive slots on Goldor's Armor.

### Mage Build
- **Sapphire** (Intelligence): Perfect Sapphire on Mythic gives +30 Intelligence per slot.
- Use in Combat slots on Storm's Armor and mage weapons.

### Farming Build
- **Peridot** (Farming Fortune): Perfect Peridot on Mythic gives +10 Farming Fortune per slot.

### Foraging Build
- **Citrine** (Foraging Fortune): Perfect Citrine on Mythic gives +20 Foraging Fortune per slot.

### Fishing Build
- **Aquamarine** (Fishing Speed): Perfect Aquamarine on Mythic gives +2 Fishing Speed per slot.

## Gemstone Progression Guide

### Early Game
1. Start mining Rough gemstones in the Crystal Hollows (requires access to Dwarven Mines / Crystal Hollows).
2. Craft Flawed gemstones once you reach Gemstone Collection II.
3. Apply Rough/Flawed gems to items with open slots for minor stat boosts.

### Mid Game
1. Craft Fine gemstones (Gemstone Collection V).
2. Work toward Flawless gemstones (Gemstone Collection IX).
3. Use Fine/Flawless gems in your main gear.
4. Consider obtaining Sorrow Armor or early Divan's Armor pieces.

### Late Game
1. Forge Perfect gemstones (Gemstone Collection X + HotM 5).
2. Requires 5 Flawless gemstones + the corresponding Gemstone Crystal per Perfect gem.
3. Forge time: 20 hours per Perfect gemstone.
4. Apply Perfect gems to fully chambered Divan's Armor (5 slots each piece = 20 total slots).
5. A single Perfect gem requires 2,560,000 Rough gem equivalents.

### Cost Perspective
- Perfect gemstones are extremely expensive to self-craft due to the massive Rough gem requirements.
- Most players buy Fine/Flawless/Perfect gems from the Bazaar rather than crafting from Rough.
- All gemstone tiers except Flawless and Perfect can be sold to NPCs (3 coins base for Rough).
- Flawless and Perfect can only be traded via Bazaar (not auctionable, not NPC-sellable).

## Crafting and Forge Uses

Gemstones are used in forging many items including:
- **Drill upgrades:** Ruby Drill Model TX-15, Topaz Drill KGR-12, Jasper Drill Model X, Titanium Drill DR-X655
- **Drill engines:** Amber-Polished, Ruby-Polished, Sapphire-Polished Drill Engines

- **Other:** Gemstone Mixture, Hot Stuff, Gemstone Gauntlet, Glacite Amalgamation
- **Pets:** Ankylosaurus, Spinosaurus, Penguin, T-Rex, Mammoth, Goblin pets
- **Accessories:** Talisman of Power, Ring of Power
- **Enchantments:** Pristine I book (40 Fine Topaz + 24 Paper)
- **Power Scrolls:** 16 Fine gems + 48 Enchanted Paper (Ruby, Sapphire, Jasper, Amethyst, Amber, Opal variants)
- **Sacks:** Small/Medium/Large Gemstone Sacks
- **Misc:** Travel Scroll to Dwarven Base Camp, Secret Railroad Pass, Dwarven Handwarmers, Tungsten Keychain

## Trivia

- Gemstones are the only Collection items with 5 quality tiers.
- The only way to instantly mine Gemstones is with any valid tool combined with the Armadillo Pet's Tunneller ability.
- All gemstones in the Crystal Hollows regenerate every 5 minutes.
- Gemstones were added in the Crystal Hollows update (v0.12).
- Opal was added on March 8, 2022.
- Onyx, Aquamarine, Citrine, and Peridot were added on March 21, 2024.
- Items created before Sep 13, 2021 have all gemstone slots unlocked by default.
