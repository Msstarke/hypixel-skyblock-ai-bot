# How to Obtain / Craft / Forge Items in Hypixel Skyblock

Most endgame items are NOT crafted at a crafting table — they are forged, dropped, or obtained through special methods.
The Hypixel items API does NOT provide recipe data. Do NOT guess recipes.

---

## Divan's Armor (Divan's Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots)

**How to obtain:** Forged in the Dwarven Mines Forge (requires Heart of the Mountain 7).
**Forge materials per piece:**
- Armor of Divan piece (the base item — purchasable on AH or obtained from King Yolkar)
- Mithril, Titanium, Gemstone Powder used in the forging process
- Takes ~143 days per piece without Quick Forge. With Quick Forge 20: ~100 days.
**Cannot be crafted at a crafting table.**

---

## Hyperion

**How to obtain:** Drops from Wither Skeletons in F7/M7 (Necron boss fight). Can also be bought on AH.
**Upgrading:** Apply Wither Essence to star it up (upgrade_costs field in items API).
**Cannot be crafted.**

---

## Astraea

**How to obtain:** Drops from Wither Skeletons in F7/M7. Can be bought on AH.
**Cannot be crafted.**

---

## Terminator (Juju Shortbow)

**How to obtain:** Juju Shortbow — crafted from Jungle Arrow + special materials from Spider's Den. Check wiki for exact recipe.
**Terminator** — drops from F7/M7. Can be bought on AH.

## Necron's Armor / Storm Armor / Goldor Armor / Maxor Armor / Aurora Armor / Terror Armor

**How to obtain:** All obtained as drops from their respective Dungeon bosses (F6/F7/M1–M7).
**Cannot be crafted.** Buy on AH or grind the respective floor.
- Goldor/Storm/Maxor — F6 boss
- Necron — F7 boss
- Aurora — M1 boss
- Terror — M3 boss
- Fervor — M5 boss

## Shadow Assassin Armor

**How to obtain:** Drops from dungeon mobs in F4/F5. Can be bought on AH.
**Cannot be crafted.**

## Dragon Armor (Strong, Unstable, Superior, Young, Wise, Old, Protector, Holy, Unstable)

**How to obtain:** Crafted from Dragon Fragments dropped by the respective dragon in The End.
- Dragon Fragments are obtained from killing Dragons in The End (Protector, Strong, Wise, etc.)
- Each piece requires Dragon Fragments + Enchanted Obsidian and Enchanted Diamond Blocks
**Check the in-game recipe book or wiki for exact fragment counts per piece.**

## Bonzo's Staff / Bonzo Staff Final Form

**How to obtain:** Bonzo's Skull drops from Bonzo boss in F1. The staff is assembled from dungeon fragments.
**Buy on AH** or craft from floor drops — check in-game for exact recipe.

## Scythe / Farming Tools

**How to obtain:** Most farming tools are crafted. Check in-game recipe book for exact ingredients.

---

## General Rule

- **Dungeon armor/weapons** (Necron, Hyperion, etc.) → Floor drops, buy on AH
- **Dragon armor** → Dragon Fragment farming in The End, buy on AH
- **Mining tools/armor** (Divan's, Titanium Drills) → Forged in HotM Forge
- **Most other gear** → Check in-game recipe book (press R on an item) or the Hypixel Skyblock wiki
- **Recipe data is NOT available via the bot** — use in-game recipe book or hypixel-skyblock.fandom.com for exact recipes
